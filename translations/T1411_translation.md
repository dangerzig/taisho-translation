# 佛說蓮華眼陀羅尼經
## Sūtra of the Lotus Eye Dhāraṇī Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1411

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dānapāla (施護)

---

**[Dhāraṇī]**

*namaḥ ratnatrayāya | namo āryapadmanītrāya tathāgatāya | namo sarvanīvaraṇaviṣkambhini bodhisattvāya mahāsattvāya | tadyathā | oṃ tara tara tiri tiri turu turu kara kara kiri kiri kuru kuru vara vara vīri vīri vuru vuru naya naya kuru kuru mokṣāpaya svāhā | jvālanāvāhini niḥ svāhā | sarvatathāgatadhiṣṭhite svāhā | mama sarvaSattvānāñca svāhā |*

This Lotus Eye Dhāraṇī: if there be any person who, at the break of dawn, makes reverent offerings, and then recites this dhāraṇī one hundred and eight times — whether for one day, two days, or up to twenty-one days, reciting it with wholehearted and sustained devotion — all karmic obstructions and hindrances will be utterly extinguished. Moreover, one will not suffer from diseases of the eyes, ears, nose, tongue, or body, and the mind will not be led astray into confusion.

One will further attain the purity of the five kinds of eyes, the purity of the five kinds of the nose, the purity of the five kinds of the tongue, and the purity of the five kinds of the mind. The bodily form will become upright and correct, and the mind will be freed from defilement and stain. Throughout all future existences, one will continuously give rise to the mind of enlightenment; together with the assembly of noble ones, one will always be able to behold the Buddha and hear the wondrous Dharma — until, at last, arriving at the seat of awakening and the attainment of perfect enlightenment.
