# 略法華三昧補助儀
## Abbreviated Supplementary Rite for the Lotus Samādhi

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1943

Translated from the Chinese. Translator unknown (失譯)

---

*Abbreviated Supplementary Rite for the Lotus Samādhi, with Preface*

First, bearing incense and flowers. Invite the Buddha and all Buddhas and Bodhisattvas. Request the Dharma. Bow to the Buddha and the remaining Buddhas and Bodhisattvas. Bow to the Dharma. Universally confess on behalf of all. Prior to [the main practice], cultivate the ten minds in reverse and forward sequence.

---

**Translator's note:** This text is an extremely condensed liturgical rubric of forty-eight characters, outlining the preparatory sequence for a Lotus Samādhi (*Fǎhuá Sānmèi*) retreat. The "ten minds in reverse and forward sequence" (*nìshùn shí xīn*) refers to a Tiantai contemplative framework used in repentance liturgies, in which ten mental states of moral entanglement are reviewed in ascending and descending order as a basis for confession.
