# 曼殊室利菩薩吉祥伽陀
## Auspicious Gāthā of Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1196

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢).

---

[Gāthā]

*Praṇidhiga saṅga-kulīre durbhava-bodhi-citta mañjuśriye vimala-bodhi-sukhābhiṣeke yad-namaṅgala-nāvari-sudā sugītāṃ tad maṅgalaṃ bhavatu te paramābhiṣeke*

*Śrī-vajra-sattva-guṇa-maṇḍala-saṃpraveśaye lakṣaṇi-vibhuvana-sārāvilāśanī-bhiye yad-namaṅgalaṃ sukha-kalpā-pravarā pragītāṃ tad maṅgalaṃ bhavatu te paramābhiṣeke*

*Adehita-sanutsārita-disaye rāśe-sattva-vibodhi sattva-dā-sugatābhiṣeke pad-namaṅgalaṃ surāvarai pragītāṃ tad maṅgalaṃ bhavatu te paramābhiṣeke*

*Śrī-mantri-loka-vijaye vara-maṇḍala-gṛhīte railokeyarā-nāya vija-yuttama-nātha-sekuye yad-namaṅgalaṃ surāvarai-saspraṇati-saspra-gītāṃ tad maṅgalaṃ bhavatu te paramābhiṣeke*

*Nānā-jagad-vidanāya sarūja-pāṇi-saṃbuddha-ratnā-makuṭa-mita-thābhiṣeke yad-namaṅgalaṃ kamāla-rāga-viśuddhi-gītāṃ tad maṅgalā bhavatu te paramābhiṣeke*

*Ākāśa-garbha-maṇi-ratna-vibhūtiramī sarvārtha-siddhi-sukha-naśa-mahābhiṣeke yad-namaṅgalaṃ śubha-vibhūti-karaisu-gītāṃ tad maṅgalaṃ bhavatu te paramābhiṣeke*

*Śāsana-kravārti-śubha-maṅgala-gīti-kayuyad-dhunpuniya-ratna-mama-duttunalā-māyā pad-dhāti-nāstu-sarvagata-bhūmi-mahābhiṣeke viśuddha-vara-kīrti-sa mañju-nātha*
