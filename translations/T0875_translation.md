---

Abbreviated recitation: the method for recitation during travel and when in haste. First, protect the body. From "one's own vessel-world" through donning the armor, next form the Small Vajra-Wheel Seal and recite its mantra. Then form the Root Seal and recite the root mantra, empowering the four places. Then make the vajra añjali, place it above the crown, and recite the syllable twenty-one times: this is the offering method. Then form the Root Seal and recite the root mantra twenty-one times. From this point on, it is not necessary to count with a rosary; thereafter one proceeds as circumstances permit.
