# 佛說較量一切佛剎功德經
## Sūtra of Comparing the Merit of All Buddha-Lands Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0290

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢)

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time, the Buddha was in the city of Rājagṛha, at the Dharma-Field Bodhi-site, seated upon a lion throne of vajra and maṇi jewels. An assembly of bodhisattva-mahāsattvas surrounded him on all sides, and there were also ten buddha-lands of hundred-thousand koṭi-nayutas of beings listening to the Buddha teach the Dharma.

At that time, within the assembly there was a bodhisattva-mahāsattva named Inconceivable Light King. He rose from his seat, surveyed the great assembly of all the bodhisattvas, and addressed them: "All sons of the Buddha! Listen now with careful attention! One full great kalpa of this sahā world, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Buddha Amitāyus in the western world of Sukhāvatī.

"Sons of the Buddha! One great kalpa of the world of Sukhāvatī, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Tathāgata Vajra-Sāgara-Pramardin in the world of Animiṣa.

"Sons of the Buddha! One great kalpa of the world of Animiṣa, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Tathāgata Lotus-Body-Manifest in the world of Non-Retrogressive Wheel Radiance.

"Sons of the Buddha! One great kalpa of the world of Non-Retrogressive Wheel Radiance, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Tathāgata Dharma King in the world of Dustless.

"Sons of the Buddha! One great kalpa of the world of Dustless, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Tathāgata Lion in the world of Lamp Light.

"Sons of the Buddha! One great kalpa of the world of Lamp Light, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Tathāgata Vairocana in the world of Excellent Light.

"Sons of the Buddha! One great kalpa of the world of Excellent Light, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Tathāgata Dharma-Manifest-Body in the world of Difficult to Surpass.

"Sons of the Buddha! One great kalpa of the world of Difficult to Surpass, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Tathāgata All-Penetrating-Mind-King in the world of Moha.

"Sons of the Buddha! One great kalpa of the world of Moha, measured against time, is one day and night in the buddha-land of the Tathāgata Praise in the world of Manifest Perfect Radiance.

"Sons of the Buddha! Thus, all worlds, calculated by kalpas, surpass ten buddha-lands of hundred-thousand koṭi-nayutas of dust-mote kalpas. Then there is a world named Lotus Auspicious. The World-Honored Buddha of that world is named Wise Auspicious. That constitutes one day and night of that buddha-land.

"Sons of the Buddha! In that land there is a bodhisattva ground called the Ground of Universal Practice, where they practice such immeasurable bodhisattva practices.

"Sons of the Buddha! Furthermore, if there is a person who receives, upholds, reads, recites, and explains to others this 'Sūtra of Comparing the Merit of All Buddha-Lands,' when that person's life ends, Buddhas as numerous as the sands of the Ganges in the buddha-lands of the ten directions will all appear before them and fulfill all the wishes of that being."

When the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the Bodhisattva Inconceivable Light King and all the great assembly, hearing what the Buddha taught, were all greatly joyful, and they accepted it with faith and practiced it.
