# 佛說療痔病經
## Sūtra on Healing Hemorrhoid Disease, Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1325

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Yijing (義淨)

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time, the Bhagavān was dwelling in Veṇuvana, the Bamboo Grove, in the great city of Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus.

At that time, many bhikṣus were suffering from hemorrhoid disease. Their bodies had grown thin and wasted; pain and suffering wound around them, and day and night they experienced extreme anguish. The Venerable Ānanda, having observed this situation, went to where the World-Honored One was, prostrated himself at the World-Honored One's feet, stood to one side, and said: "World-Honored One! In the city of Rājagṛha at this time, many bhikṣus are suffering from hemorrhoid disease. Their bodies have grown thin and wasted; pain and suffering wind around them, and day and night they experience extreme anguish. World-Honored One, how can these various hemorrhoid diseases be remedied and healed?"

The Buddha said to Ānanda: "You should hear this *Sūtra on Healing Hemorrhoid Disease*. Recite it, uphold it, fix it in your mind without forgetting, and also proclaim it widely to others. All these hemorrhoid diseases shall be entirely cured. They are: wind hemorrhoids, heat hemorrhoids, phlegmatic hemorrhoids, triple-combined hemorrhoids, blood hemorrhoids, abdominal hemorrhoids, nasal hemorrhoids, dental hemorrhoids, tongue hemorrhoids, eye hemorrhoids, ear hemorrhoids, crown hemorrhoids, hand-and-foot hemorrhoids, back-and-spine hemorrhoids, fecal hemorrhoids, and all manner of hemorrhoids arising throughout the joints and limbs of the body. All such hemorrhoids and fistulas shall entirely dry up, fall away, and be extinguished. They will be completely healed without doubt. All should recite and uphold the following divine dhāraṇī."

Then he spoke the dhāraṇī:

[Dhāraṇī] *tadyathā grāme śrī śrī māgaśiti samatu svāhā*

"This dhāraṇī:

[Dhāraṇī] *tadyathā arante arāme śrīvī śrī śrī māgaśiti sambhavatu svāhā*

"Ānanda! To the north of here there stands the great King of Snow Mountains. Within it there grows a great śāla tree called 'Durjaya'; the Unvanquishable. This tree bears three kinds of flowers: the first newly bloomed, the second fully opened, the third dried and withered. Just as those flowers dry out and fall away, so too shall all these hemorrhoid diseases be the same; let blood no longer flow out, let pus no longer run; all pain and suffering shall be permanently removed, and everything shall dry up completely. Furthermore, whoever constantly recites this sūtra shall attain the knowledge of previous abodes and be able to recall events from seven past lives. When the dhāraṇī practice is accomplished; *svāhā*."

Then he spoke another dhāraṇī:

[Dhāraṇī] *tadyathā jame jame śajame jambuni śajani svāhā*

When the Buddha had finished speaking this sūtra, the Venerable Ānanda and all the great assembly were filled with great joy, and having faith, received and practiced it.
