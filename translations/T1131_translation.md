# 聖金剛手菩薩一百八名梵讚
## Sanskrit Praise of the 108 Names of Holy Vajrapāṇi Bodhisattva

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1131

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢).

---

### First Assembly

*Vajrasattva mahāsattva vajraratna mahāmāla vajradharma mahāśuddha vajra-ākarṣaṇa namastute*

### Second Assembly

*Vajranetra mahācakṣu vajramaitrī mahādṛṣṭi vajrayakṣa mahātīvra vajrarakṣaṇa namastute*

### Third Assembly

*Vajramuṣṭi mahāmuṣṭi vajramuṣṭi mahāsukha vajrahāsa mahāhāsa vajrabhāṣaṇa namastute*

### Fourth Assembly

*Bodhicittaṃ mahābodhi buddhasarva-tathāgata vajrayāna mahāyāna vajrajñāna namastute*

### Fifth Assembly

*Sattvārtha-sarvārtha mahāsattvārtha smṛti-sarvajñā sarvasarvottama sarvasiddhi namastute*

### Sixth Assembly

*Vajrātmaka-su vajra-grāha vajravīra-su vajra-dṛk mahāsamaya tattvārtha mahāsattva namastute*

### Seventh Assembly

*Vajratuṣa mahājvāla vajrajvāla mahāprabha vajraprabha prabodha-yukta buddha-prabha namastute*

### Eighth Assembly

*Vajrarāja-grāhya vajra-grāhya megha-grāhya grāhya-nārūtma vajrūtma mahā-grāhya grāha vajrūtma namastute*

### Ninth Assembly

*Vajradhātu mahāguhya vajraguhya suguhya dṛgjñāna vajra-mahāvajra vajra-karṣaṇa namastute*

### Tenth Assembly

*Buddha-grāhya buddha-vajra-grāhya bodhi-buddha mahābuddha vajrabuddhāya buddhāya bodhi-buddha namastute*

### Eleventh Assembly

*Buddhapūjā mahāpūjā sattvapūjā supūjā kā ahūpāya mahāprajñā mahāsiddha namastute*

### Twelfth Assembly

*Tathāgata mahākāya tathāgata-sārasvati tathāgata mahācitta vajravajra namastute*

### Thirteenth Assembly

*Tathāgata mahātattva bhūtakoti mahānaya sarvapramātattva jñāna paramṛtaka namastute*

### Fourteenth Assembly

*Samantabhadra rāja-grāhya māra māra paramṛṇaka sarvagrahya samantajñāna sarvatra-ka namastute*

### Fifteenth Assembly

*Buddha hūṃkāra hūṃkāra vajra-hūṃkāra nāyaka vajra vajraṃga vajraṃga vajrapāṇi namastute*

### Sixteenth Assembly

*Vajrapāṇi satama satama satkṛtame tathāgatai yat siddhiṃ sarvajñāta citta-bodhicitta sattu stuteḥ*

### Seventeenth Assembly

*Kṛtadhikāra hi saṃbuddha-mitra staṃbhi sarvadṛṣṭana saṃbhūta saṃbha-viśāntitatva-ma sa tathāgata*

### Eighteenth Assembly

*Sarvakāra-jñāta sattva-darśi-bhava tathāgate vajrasattva samanviṣkṛn hṛnaya sarvadehina*

### Nineteenth Assembly

*Iti idaṃ śraddhāte śraddhā suvadai idaṃ sa tathāgate sarvabuddha mitaṃ stutat śrīya-śrūṇu satkṛtam*

### Twentieth Assembly

*Sarva-pāpa-viśuddha-ātma sarva-bodhi-samudbhava ādineṇa stutaṃ trāraṃ naca tu soktam subhaktaka*
