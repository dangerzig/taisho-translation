# 法相宗章疏
## Treatises and Commentaries of the Dharma-Characteristics School

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 2180

Compiled in Japan, recorded by Heiso (平祚錄)

---

The foregoing are commentaries on Mahāyāna sūtras.

The foregoing are commentaries on Mahāyāna śāstras.

The foregoing were composed by the patriarchs of the Great Tang.

---

*Note: This text (T2180) is a fragmentary bibliographic catalog of the Fǎxiàng (Dharma-Characteristics/Yogācāra) school's commentarial literature, compiled in Japan. Only three colophon lines survive in the Taishō edition, each marking the end of a section in the original catalog. The full catalog to which these lines belonged is no longer extant in this form.*
