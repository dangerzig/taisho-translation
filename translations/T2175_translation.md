# 錄外經等目錄
## Catalog of Sūtras and Other Works Outside the Standard Catalogs

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 2175

Japanese composition in Classical Chinese. Kamakura period; compiled by Shinzon (親尊)

---

**Supplementary Catalog of [Texts] Brought [from China]**

**Supplementary Catalog of Jōgyō**

**Supplementary Catalog of Eon**

**Supplementary Catalog of Zenrin**

The catalogs of the above five collections' supplementary works and so forth are contained in the Renge'ō-in catalog. The remaining three collections are not [there]. Beyond these, entries should be written in as they are encountered or heard of. The three collections of the other school are appended separately at the back.

**Supplementary to the Catalog**

Ritual Manuals for Sequential Recitation

**Three Collections of the Other School**

**Supplementary Catalog of Saichō**

**Supplementary Catalog of Ennin**

**Supplementary Catalog of Enchin**

On the eighteenth day of the fifth month of the eighth year of Kenchō [1256 CE], [this was] annotated at Kakudō-in of Murakami Daigo. Entries should be added as they are encountered or heard of. There are certainly many errors; future scholars must correct and revise them.

Vajra Buddha-son Shinzon

---

*Note: T2175 is a brief Japanese bibliographic catalog compiled in 1256 by the Tendai monk Shinzon (親尊), listing supplementary catalogs of texts brought from Tang China by various Japanese monks (Jōgyō, Eon, etc.) and maintained at Renge'ō-in, along with supplementary catalogs attributed to the Tendai patriarchs Saichō, Ennin, and Enchin.*
