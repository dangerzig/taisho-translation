# 釋迦牟尼佛成道在菩提樹降魔讚
## Praise of Śākyamuni Buddha's Attainment of the Way beneath the Bodhi Tree and Subjugation of Māra

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0941

Translated from the Chinese. Translator unknown

---

*Namo śākyamunaye*

*Ṛta-māraka-jī-raja-gaṇi-kā-gu-surā-yakṣa-mahura-gaṇi-niyā-naṃ-tuñ-cātu-sā-suni*

*Śata-māraka-jī-moṅgu-ṇā-miṃ-ghanma-haṃ-praṇa-mā-bhi-sadā-bhāva-bhimā-mahur-nadi-mādhāya-gataṃ-kṛ-pada-tāra-vaṃ-smā-vekṣya-janaṃ-pari-moca-itu-nāya-iha-praṇi-praca-kāra-vihaṃ-sta-māhaṃ-praṇa-tā-giri-rāja-ni-bhāśa-rānī-lāndi-yaṃ-su-mukhaṃ-su-bhuja-vāra-rūpa-dhāraṃ-su-gatiṃ-gatā-maṃ-prati-su-gataṃ-prāṇa-tuṣmi-sadā-jaga-dathā-kāleṅ-kā-nāṃ-kalpa-prabhāya-pari-pṛta-tu-dvāra-dundā-lānabhi-tu-yeda-vāla-guru-taṃ-gaja-haṃ-svi-lāṃ-bhi-vāta-dhi-ragatiṃ-śirā-sabhināṃ-tuṣmi-guṇi-kā-nidi-tā-rudā-kā-smaira-cale-rānā-yine-sphuru-dugra-māha-danī-bhimā-ravainā-mujī-prā-hale-ra-śiśa-kti-dhāre-cari-tuṣvi-bhūra-nā-hitu-vikri-dailā-vāri-phala-pañju-ru-khaṇḍa-puṭa-jicā-bhāra-vināṃ-bhi-tā-gatra-latā-nāma-dāsa-tvā-śe-kuru-nāṃ-gaṃ-su-tā-śva-sidai-skhāri-tu-rā-bhi-kā-gai-tu-ndarā-vā-ku-sumā-sta-vā-kā-bhira-ṇā-vahuṃ-ratnā-sahasra-jī-cita-śca-nāṃ-gana-tathā-bhirā-jī-bhauya-bhi-staṃ-janāya-kti-yathā-tavā-vira-guṇā-sphuṭa-jī-citra-patana-vahuṃ-yukti-yu-taṃ-gamā-kaṃ-vā-ca-nānta-vā-kāra-ṇā-su-khaṃ-śubha-māra-ga-phalāṃ-prā-smi-kṣya-janaṃ-daśū-nāndi-puṇya-tāri-tihā-yavā-ca-iti-vāśa-ranāṃ-smā-vekṣya-janaṃ-nā-patā-bhiye-ṣpi-kālpa-paśa-dai-ni-rāye-vini-hā-bhaya-cā-ni-ṣāri-puṃ-vahuṃ-rikṣa-pari-yānti-śubha-vāra-mokṣa-pūrṇa-tvā-saimā-ya-tā-yā-pā-ya-bhi-bhūta-vā-nāṃ-virā-jati-śiti-kā-gaṃ-gānī-tavā-kañca-nā-ku-kumā-sprā-bhāya-prā-bhāya-bhi-hā-tu-nāṃ-vi-stira-vṛta-vā-nā-tha-śubhe-vā-da-naṃ-bru-haiṃ-nā-yanā-bhā-gamā-rānī-pata-jī-tāri-nāṃ-prati-buddhā-dre-kā-māre-vi-māre-bhāra-māra-i-vā-puñja-maśa-tā-kuri-taṃ-iti-tuṣṭa-ka-vā-nir-tivā-tura-rū-pari-kṣi-tāya-māya-tā-vā-vāra-ṇā-lāṃ-bhī-yā-nu-pā-śi-tā-māniyā-śubha-vi-pūlāṃ-śa-jemā-stu-tā-tu-bhi-vi-devā-nir-gānī-māra-vi-jayā-stu-tatrāṃ-smā-patā-bhi-ti*

Colophon: Collated by Jōgon of the Reiun-ji school, twenty-first of the second month of the fourth year of Jōkyō [1687 CE]. First collation completed.
