# 佛說觀想佛母般若波羅蜜多菩薩經
## Sūtra of Visualizing the Mother of Buddhas Prajñāpāramitā Bodhisattva Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0259

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Devaśāntika (天息災)

---

Abhiṣeka dhāraṇī:

*Oṃ namaḥ Śākyamunaye tathāgatāya arhate samyaksaṃbuddhāya.*

Recite this dhāraṇī seven times, and with the hand perform the abhiṣeka upon the crown of the head and touch it throughout the body. Then still the mind and, with focused intent, perform the visualization practice of the Mother of Buddhas Prajñāpāramitā Bodhisattva.

Visualize this bodhisattva with three faces and three eyes. Her body is the color of true gold, and she sits upon the Auspicious Treasury Lion Throne, upon a seat of a thousand-petalled golden lotus. She has six arms. Of the three arms on the right side: the first arm holds a rosary, the second arm holds an arrow, and the third arm makes the wish-granting mudrā. Of the three arms on the left side: the first arm holds a sūtra, the second arm holds a bow, and the third arm holds a cintāmaṇi jewel. These six arms are adorned with various ornaments. From her body she further emits innumerable hundred thousand nayuta koṭi rays of light, filling all worlds. Further visualize all the Tathāgatas and Tārā Bodhisattva and all other bodhisattvas, complete with marks and adornments upon their bodies, and offer them all manner of incense and flowers as personal offerings.

Having completed this visualization, further recite the heart dhāraṇī:

*Oṃ namaḥ Śākyamunaye tathāgatāya arhate samyaksaṃbuddhāya. Tadyathā: oṃ mune mune mahāmunaye svāhā.*

Next, recite the root dhāraṇī:

*Tadyathā: oṃ jaye jaye padma-abhyavāmi avāmi sara-saraṇi dhiri dhiri radhiri dharadhiri nivāta-nupara-nibuddha-rāṇi pūraya pūraya bhagavati sarva-śamama-paripūraya sapari-varasya sarva-sattvānāṃ ca sarva-karma-āvaraṇāni viśodhaya viśodhaya buddhi-adhiṣṭhānāni svāhā.*

Having recited this dhāraṇī, further perform the visualization. Visualize that to the right of the Mother of Buddhas Prajñāpāramitā Bodhisattva are: the World-Honored Śākyamuni, the World-Honored Dīpaṃkara, the World-Honored Amitāyus, the World-Honored Wisdom Resolute, the World-Honored Radiance King, the World-Honored Thunder-Voice Roar, the World-Honored Golden Flower, and the World-Honored Scattered Flower.

To the left of the bodhisattva: the World-Honored Vipaśyin, the World-Honored Krakucchanda, the World-Honored Kanakamuni, the World-Honored Kāśyapa, and the World-Honored Jewel Hand.

Before the bodhisattva: the World-Honored Mahāvairocana, the World-Honored Ratnasaṃbhava, the World-Honored Amitābha, the World-Honored Amoghasiddhi, and the World-Honored Akṣobhya.

Behind the bodhisattva: the World-Honored Supreme Lotus, the World-Honored Supreme Jewel, the World-Honored Joy Auspicious, the World-Honored Vaiḍūrya Light, and the World-Honored Inconceivable Auspicious.

Before the bodhisattva there are also: the Bodhisattva Holy Avalokiteśvara, the Bodhisattva Maitreya, the Bodhisattva Samantabhadra, the Bodhisattva Mañjuśrī, the Bodhisattva Namas, the Bodhisattva Inconceivable Auspicious Voice, the Bodhisattva Vajrapāṇi, the Bodhisattva Akṣayamati, the Bodhisattva Pratibhānakūṭa, the Bodhisattva Anantamati, the Bodhisattva Ākāśagarbha, the Bodhisattva Vimalakīrti, the Bodhisattva Free Practice, the Bodhisattva Dharmodgata, the Bodhisattva Sadāprarudita, the Bodhisattva Candraprabha, the Bodhisattva Dharmamegha, the Bodhisattva Kṣitigarbha, the Bodhisattva Ratnagarbha, the Bodhisattva Ratnadhvaja, the Bodhisattva Śikhin, the Bodhisattva Gandhahastī, and the Bodhisattva Kumbhīra. All such bodhisattva-mahāsattvas fill the buddha-lands.

There are also the Wrathful Vidyārāja Bhrūkuṭī and others, also before the bodhisattva.

Having visualized each of these holy beings one by one, further visualize exquisite incense, flowers, precious jewels, and offering implements from the human and heavenly realms, and use them to make offerings to the Mother of Buddhas Prajñāpāramitā Bodhisattva together with all her retinue of bodhisattvas. Having performed this visualization, that person will before long accomplish perfect enlightenment.
