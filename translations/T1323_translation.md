# 除一切疾病陀羅尼經
## Dhāraṇī Sūtra for Eliminating All Diseases

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1323

Translated from the Chinese. Tang Dynasty, translated by Amoghavajra (不空)

---

Thus have I heard.

At one time the Bhagavān was dwelling at Śrāvastī, in the Jeta Grove, the Garden of Anāthapiṇḍada, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus, and many great bodhisattvas mahāsattvas. At that time the World-Honored One addressed Ānanda, saying: "Ānanda! There is a dhāraṇī capable of eliminating all diseases in the world. You should receive and uphold it, recite and master it, and apply it with right attention." Then he spoke the mantra:

> *tadyathā vimale vimale vana-kukile śrīmati kuṇḍali nanu-bhi indra-giṇi mure svāhā*

The Buddha addressed Ānanda: "Whoever recites and upholds this dhāraṇī — those suffering from indigestion, cholera, disorders of wind, bile, and phlegm, hemorrhoids, fistulas, urinary obstruction, ascending qi, cough, malaria, alternating cold and heat, headache, hemicrania, or affliction by demons and spirits — all shall be cured and healed. I behold such a person with the Buddha-eye, and among the gods, Māra, Brahmā, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas who would create obstacles and difficulties, except for those whose fixed karmic retribution has run its course, none shall be able to transgress and create such obstacles.

"The Tathāgata, the Worthy One, the Perfectly and Completely Enlightened One declares: Among all sentient beings, the Tathāgata is supreme; among all dharmas, the Dharma of liberation from desire is supreme; among all assemblies, the Saṃgha is supreme. By this truth, may I and all sentient beings digest whatever food and drink we consume, and attain true and lasting well-being. Svāhā."

At that time, the World-Honored One having spoken this sūtra, the assembly of bhikṣus together with the bodhisattvas mahāsattvas, the entire great assembly, the gods, dragons, and the eight classes of beings received the Buddha's words with joy and put them into practice.
