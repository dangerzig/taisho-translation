# 佛說慈氏菩薩陀羅尼
## The Dhāraṇī of Maitreya Bodhisattva, Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1142

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢)

---

*tathā śrī śrī sara sara suru suru dhara dhara cara cara mahācara vara vara mahāvara kampa kampa mahākampa hūṃ masara dharma agra sāgara svāhā*

---

**Translator's note:** This text consists entirely of a phonetic transcription of a Sanskrit dhāraṇī. The above represents a reconstructed Sanskrit reading from the Chinese phonetic characters. The text is attributed to the bodhisattva Maitreya (慈氏, "One of Loving-Kindness").
