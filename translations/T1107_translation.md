# 聖多羅菩薩梵讚
## Brahmā-Hymn to Holy Tārā Bodhisattva

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1107

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dānapāla (施護).

---

[The entire text consists of a Brahmā-hymn to Holy Tārā Bodhisattva, rendered in Chinese transliteration of Sanskrit. The hymn is a devotional praise-text comparable to similar Indian stotra literature.]
