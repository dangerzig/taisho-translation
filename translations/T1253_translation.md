# 大吉祥天女十二契一百八名無垢大乘經
## Mahāyāna Sūtra of the Twelve Mudrās and One Hundred and Eight Immaculate Names of Mahāśrī Devī

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1253

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Amoghavajra (不空).

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time the Bhagavān was dwelling in the world of Sukhāvatī, together with a great assembly of bodhisattvas, namely: Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, Mahāsthāmaprāpta Bodhisattva, Sarvanivaraṇaviṣkambhin Bodhisattva, Kṣitigarbha Bodhisattva, Samantaprabhā Bodhisattva, Ākāśagarbha Bodhisattva, Vajrapāṇi Bodhisattva, Sarvabhayahara Bodhisattva, Sarvaśuddhamaṅgaladhārin Bodhisattva, Sarvapuṇyalakṣaṇadhārin Bodhisattva, Sūryacandratrikāladhārin Bodhisattva, and Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva. Such bodhisattva mahāsattvas were the foremost. At that time, Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva went to the World-Honored One, bowed his head at his feet, withdrew, and sat to one side. Then Mahāśrī Devī also went to the Buddha's presence, bowed her head at his feet, circumambulated immeasurable hundreds of thousands of times, bowed to all the bodhisattvas dwelling in the world of Sukhāvatī, withdrew, and sat to one side.

At that time, the World-Honored One, seeing that Mahāśrī Devī was adorned with immeasurable hundreds of thousands of blessings, surrounded by koṭis of tathāgatas, praised and extolled by all Śakras, Brahmās, and world-protectors, spoke in a great brahma-voice to Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva: "Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva! If there are kings, princes, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, upāsikās, brāhmaṇas, kṣatriyas, vaiśyas, or śūdras who receive and uphold the twelve mudrās and one hundred and eight immaculate names and praises of Mahāśrī Devī, then in the domains of those kings and kṣatriyas, all fears and afflictions of the sentient beings will be completely eliminated. All enemies, bandits, and terrors from humans and non-humans will also be unable to cause harm. All wealth and grain will be abundant. Mahāśrī Devī will constantly dwell within the residences of those kings and kṣatriyas." At that time the bodhisattva mahāsattva spoke these words: "Excellent, excellent! The World-Honored One has wondrously spoken these words. If anyone upholds the name of Mahāśrī Devī, they will obtain such blessings." Then Abhayāvalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! At what place did Mahāśrī Devī formerly plant her wholesome roots?" The Buddha said: "She planted her wholesome roots in the presence of Ganges-sands of tathāgatas, arhats, and perfectly awakened ones. Abhayāvalokiteśvara Bodhisattva! I recall that in a past life, in the Ratnasambhava World, there appeared a buddha named Ratnakusumaguṇasāgaravaiḍūryasuvarṇaprabhāśrī Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Awakened One. Mahāśrī Devī planted wholesome roots in his presence, and also in the presence of many other tathāgatas. By reciting the names of such tathāgatas, Mahāśrī Devī accomplished wholesome roots. These tathāgatas constantly accompany Mahāśrī Devī. They can deliver from all sins, eliminate all afflictions, cause all bodies to be immaculate, attract and increase all wealth and grain, remove poverty, attract all devas, nāgas, yakṣas, rākṣasas, gandharvas, asuras, garuḍas, kiṃnaras, and mahoragas, pacify all afflictions, disputes, and battles, and accomplish the six pāramitās. They are:

"Homage to Śrī-Guhya Tathāgata.

"Homage to Gaṅgā-Sarva-Tīrtha-Maṅgala-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Candana-Kusuma-Nakṣatra-Prabhā-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Samanta-Prabhāsa-Vijaya-Saṃgrāma-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Guṇa-Sāgara-Prabhāsa-Maṇḍala-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Dharma-Abhijñā-Dhvaja-Vīrya-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Prabhā-Śānta-Gandha-Prabhāsa-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Sattvāśaya-Śānta-Kāya-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Praṇidhāna-Sāgara-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Suparikīrtita-Nāmadheyā-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Avaivartika-Cakra-Ratna-Sthāna-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Sūrya-Maṇḍala-Prabhāsa-Udaya-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Asaṃkhyeya-Vīrya-Supratiṣṭhita-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Apramāṇa-Supratiṣṭhita-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Svara-Vyaṅga-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Prajñā-Dīpa-Asaṃkhyeya-Prabhā-Dhvaja-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Nārāyaṇa-Śīla-Kavaca-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Brahma-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Maheśvara-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Sūrya-Candra-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Gambhīra-Dharma-Prabhā-Rāja-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Ākāśa-Dīpa-Pramuditā-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Sūrya-Prabhā-Dhvaja-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Gandha-Dīpa-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Sāgara-Garbha-Sambhava-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Nirmāṇa-Megha-Sughoṣa-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Sarva-Prabhāsa-Vyūha-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Vṛkṣa-Rāja-Pravardhana-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Ratna-Jvālā-Giri-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Jñāna-Jvālā-Sāgara-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Mahā-Praṇidhāna-Vīrya-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Mahā-Megha-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Smṛti-Dhvaja-Rāja-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Indra-Dhvaja-Patāka-Rāja-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Ākarṣaṇa-Sarva-Dhana-Dhānya-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Ākarṣaṇa-Śānti-Śrī Tathāgata.

"Homage to Ākarṣaṇa-Maṅgala-Śrī Tathāgata.

"If anyone reverently receives, upholds, reads, and recites such tathāgata names, those good men and good women will generate a great mass of merit. All tathāgatas have bestowed a prediction upon Mahāśrī Devī: 'You shall in the world of Śrī-Ratna-Vyūha attain perfect awakening as Śrī-Maṇi-Ratnasambhava Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Awakened One. That world will be adorned with various celestial treasures. In that world, only this tathāgata will serve as the light. The bodhisattva assembly in that buddha-world will naturally possess radiance, and their lifespan will be immeasurable. From space will emanate the sounds of Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha. All bodhisattvas born in that buddha-world will all be born by transformation upon lotus pedestals.'

"What are the twelve mudrās and one hundred and eight immaculate names and praises? Abhayāvalokiteśvara! Listen carefully now. They are:

"Anointed by all tathāgatas. Mother of all tathāgatas. Mother of all devas. Auspiciousness of all tathāgatas. Auspiciousness of all bodhisattvas. Auspiciousness of all worthy śrāvakas and pratyekabuddhas. Auspiciousness of Brahmā, Viṣṇu, and Maheśvara. Foremost auspiciousness of all devas. Reaching all places in auspiciousness. Auspiciousness of all devas, nāgas, yakṣas, rākṣasas, gandharvas, asuras, garuḍas, kiṃnaras, and mahoragas. Auspiciousness of all vajra-holders. Auspiciousness of the four world-protecting kings. Auspiciousness of the eight luminaries and twenty-eight lunar mansions. Oṃ sāvitrī dhātrī mātr, auspiciousness of the four vidyās. Auspicious ghost-mother. Supreme, most supreme. All fords of the Ganges. All auspiciousness. Immaculate auspiciousness. All sin-removing. Unceasing moon auspiciousness. Sun auspiciousness. All luminaries' auspiciousness. Riding a lion. Hundred-thousand koṭi bimbara. Lotus-adorned. Lotus. Great lotus. Lotus-seated. Lotus-treasury. Lotus-holder. Lotus-endowed. Immeasurable jewel-light. Wealth-bestowing. White. Great white lotus. White-armed. Holder of all auspicious adornments. Possessor of all merit-bodies. Gentle one. Hundred-thousand arms. Hundred-thousand eyes. Hundred-thousand heads. Wearing various mixed maṇi-crowns. Wondrous color. Various colors. Renowned. Most renowned. Tranquil. Nirmita. Pure-haired. Moonlight. Sunlight. Maker of beauty. Auspiciousness facing all sentient beings. Holy one. Depending on flowers. Flower-sovereign. Auspiciousness of all Sumeru mountain kings. Auspiciousness of all rivers. Auspiciousness of all seas. Auspiciousness of all fords. Auspiciousness of all herbs, trees, wealth, and grain. Gold-bestowing. Food and drink bestowing. Color-pure. Body-colored one. Sovereign of all tathāgatas. Auspiciousness facing all deva hosts. Auspiciousness foremost among Yama, Varuṇa, Kubera, and the Vasus. Giver. Feeder. Possessing awesome light. Endowed with awesome light. Abundant. Flourishing. Increasing. Ascending. Dharma-auspiciousness. Depending on spring. Kumuda-treasury. Compassionate one. Depending on a man's body. All-pure. Auspicious hand. Remover of all inauspiciousness. Attracting all merit and auspiciousness. Auspiciousness of all earth-kings. Auspiciousness of all vidyā-holders. Auspiciousness of all ghosts, yakṣas, rākṣasas, hungry ghosts, piśācas, kumbhāṇḍas, and mahoragas. Auspiciousness of all celestial palaces and devas. Auspiciousness of all recitation and homa. Radiant, most joyful, meritorious sport. All ṛṣi-pure auspiciousness. All-auspiciousness. Most victorious auspiciousness of all palaces. Auspiciousness of all kiṃnaras. All-sun-victorious auspiciousness. Sinless. Flowing one. Music-delighting one. Beloved of Kubera. Dharma-king auspiciousness."

The mantra for the preceding one hundred and eight names:

*Oṃ vilokaya tārāya mocaya sarvānukte viyak sarva-puṇya-saṃbharaṇa-mama-ukuru svāhā oṃ gaṅgā-sarvā-tīrtha-mukhe svāhā oṃ sāvitrī svāhā sarva-maṅgala-dhāriṇī svāhā catur-veda sarva-nakṣatra-graha-gaṇa-adhimūr-ditai svāhā brahma-maya svāhā viṣṇu-vai svāhā rudrāya svāhā viśva-mukhāya svāhā oṃ gṛhṇi-gṛhṇi sarva-kārya-saṃsādhanī sidhnī-sidhnī-nī-nī-nī-nī ārakṣāmi mena-śaya āvāhane viśrī-vaiśravaṇāya svāhā suvarṇa-dhana-dhānīya-kṛṣṇi svāhā sarva-puṇya-kṛṣṇi svāhā śrī-devata-kṛṣṇi svāhā sarva-pāpa-śamanī svāhā sarva-lakṣmī-praśamanī svāhā sarva-tathāgata-abhiṣiktāyai svāhā sarva-devata-pramukha-śrīyai svāhā āyur-amara-varṇa-karāyai svāhā sarva-pavitra-maṃ-saraha-satāyai svāhā saṃjīvāhināyai svāhā padma-saṃbhūtā svāhā sarva-kṛte-kakūrda-vidāśināyai svāhā*

"Abhayāvalokiteśvara Bodhisattva! This great auspicious mantra, together with the one hundred and eight names, can eliminate all afflictions, crush all sins, attract all merit, remove all inauspiciousness, and attract all blessings. If anyone receives, upholds, reads, and recites this and the names of the tathāgatas, that person should rise early, offer incense and flowers to all buddhas, and burn sandalwood incense for Mahāśrī Devī, and read this sūtra. That person will before long obtain all auspiciousness, all peace, happiness, and joy, the protection of all devas, and the accomplishment of all undertakings." When the Buddha had finished speaking this sūtra, Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, Mahāśrī Devī, and the entire great assembly, having heard what the Buddha proclaimed, were all greatly delighted, faithfully received the teaching, and put it into practice.
