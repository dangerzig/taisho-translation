# 諸佛心印陀羅尼經
## Sūtra of the Heart-Seal Dhāraṇī of All the Buddhas

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0919

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dharmadeva (法天)

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time, the Buddha was in the Tuṣita Heaven, within the maṇḍala of the jewel-adorned bodhisattva palace. Innumerable bodhisattvas, adorned with the major and minor marks, who knew the true boundary of the Dharma, sons of the Tathāgata, had each come from various buddha-lands. They all bowed at the Buddha's feet and withdrew to sit to one side.

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the great assembly: "Good men! There is a dhāraṇī called the Buddha's Heart-Seal. Tathāgatas as numerous as the sands of the Ganges have proclaimed it. I now wish to benefit the devas and humans of Tuṣita, causing them to obtain the corresponding happiness. If a good man receives, upholds, reads, recites, explains, or hears this dhāraṇī, you should know that this person obtains the knowledge of former lives. Heavy karma is extinguished, and one constantly receives happiness, never falling into the evil destinies. Loved and cherished by all, protected and upheld by all, with both worldly and world-transcending wealth abundantly fulfilled, neither humans nor non-humans can invade or harm them. For a thousand kalpas of transmigration, one will not be born in the realm of Māra. unsurpassed enlightenment will be swiftly realized."

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke the mantra:

*Tadyathā buddhe buddhe buddhe māte samanta-buddhā-nugayate buddha buddha buddha buddha buddha buddha buddha ananta-buddha-viṣaya ananta-dharma-niśānā svāhā*

The Buddha said: "This great dhāraṇī of the Buddha's Heart-Seal possesses great awesome power and benefits sentient beings.

"I shall now further proclaim the Heart-Seal Dhāraṇī of All the Buddhas:

*Tadyathā hara hara cāra cāra saṃcāra saṃcāra tāra tāra saṃtāra saṃtāra hini rini samārini raṇa-pati raṇa-pati loka-dhāri loka-dhāri loka-dhāni loka-dhāriṇī vara vara prura prura vara vara mahā-vijaya-vāhite hana hana sarva-buddha-pṛṣṭhite sarva-paṭi-sanā aparāṅgite pratibhāna-saṃpanni sattva-valokite sarva-buddha-pratimanite bhagavatī sara sara prasara prasara visara visara sarvā-nuśa-apagate svāhā*"

At that time, when the World-Honored One spoke this mantra, all the celestial palaces, the entire great earth, the mountains, rivers, forests, and wilds, Mount Sumeru and others, experienced the six kinds of trembling, and the great ocean surged and boiled. The kings of Māra were stricken with sorrow and fear. Joining their palms, they took refuge: "All the Buddhas of the three times, all the bodhisattvas: grant us fearlessness! We and our retinues vow to protect the Buddha-Dharma."

At that time, when the World-Honored One had finished teaching this Dharma, all the bodhisattvas and all the devas and humans, having heard what the Buddha taught, faithfully received and practiced it.
