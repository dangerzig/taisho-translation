# Sūtra of the Divine Talismans of the Seven Thousand Buddhas

## *Qīqiān Fó Shénfú Jīng* (七千佛神符經)

Taishō 2904

Translator unknown. An apocryphal talismanic text.

*Note: This is an apocryphal text blending Buddhist invocation with Chinese folk religion and Daoist talisman practice. It invokes "seven thousand buddhas" to protect the body, extend life, and ward off evil spirits, while incorporating the Northern Dipper (Big Dipper) star cult, the sixty-year cycle (jiǎzǐ), and the language of Daoist exorcistic commands ("urgent, urgent, in accordance with the statutes and ordinances"). It reflects the deep syncretism of popular religion in medieval China.*

The human body's life is extended and its reckoning increased. The lifespan is one hundred and twenty years. In the *jiǎchén* cycle the reckoning is increased. Seven thousand buddhas protect the human body, causing it not to encounter evil, and rescue me to increase the reckoning. The seven thousand buddhas protect the human body and sever all *jiǎzǐ* cycles. From the Buddha the reckoning of the human body and life is increased. When life reaches its end, a thousand buddhas descend and give me increased reckoning.

The seven thousand buddhas rescue and protect the person. They increase the food-reckoning. Seven hundred and seventy buddhas descend and give me increased reckoning. The seven hundred and seventy buddhas descend to the body. When there is illness, bodhisattvas destroy Māra. Thirty-five buddhas descend and give me increased reckoning. Seven hundred and seventy buddhas protect the human body, causing it to be firm, strong, courageous, and vigorous. Fifty-three buddhas descend and give me increased reckoning. Seven hundred and seventy protect the human body and life for hundreds of millions of years, constantly attaining the buddha-way. Six buddhas descend and give me increased reckoning. Seven hundred and seventy buddhas protect the person and increase their bodily life, open their wisdom and understanding, and reach penetration without obstruction. Seven buddhas descend and give me increased reckoning. Thirty-five buddhas protect and guide the human body. Their dignified bearing is without deficiency. All hosts of Māra are naturally awed. Eighty thousand bodhisattvas give me increased reckoning. Seventy-three buddhas: may the buddhas, all bodhisattvas, śrāvakas, and pratyekabuddhas, and all the arhats increase the reckoning of their disciples.

The talisman of the seven thousand buddhas strikes and destroys all hosts of Māra, the Way of Poison (*gǔ*, 蠱), mountain and forest spirits, shrine spirits, cliff-bank spirits, and northern spirits: all are naturally extinguished. The talisman of a thousand buddhas strikes and destroys all hosts of Māra, the wind king, the stove lord, the water dragon spirit, and the earth lord: all are naturally extinguished. The talisman of a thousand buddhas strikes and destroys the five wasters, six depleters, evil dreams, mournful cries, wild-path curses, and sorcery: all are naturally extinguished.

The Destroyer-of-Armies Star rules over wasting and depletion. The Military-Melody Star rules over lawsuits. The Chaste-and-Upright Star rules over disputes of the tongue. The Literary-Melody Star rules over evil dreams. The Salary-Preservation Star rules over the hundred ghosts. The Great-Gate Star governs wrong deaths. The Greedy-Wolf Star rules over evil vapors. In the Northern Dipper stars, life and fate are recorded. In the Northern Dipper stars, the hundred ghosts are registered. The Three Terrace Stars rule over protecting the body and life.

The first is the Enmity Star; the second is the Auspicious Star; the third is the Warding Star; the fourth is the Blaze-Confusion Star; the fifth is the Danger Star, which can dissolve calamity and receive difficulty. It wards off all pestilent vapors, illnesses, evil and malicious spirits, and chaotic ghosts throughout the world, sending them all beyond a thousand, ten thousand *lǐ*.

Urgent, urgent, in accordance with the statutes and ordinances!

The first: talisman for opening the heart. The second: talisman for increasing the reckoning. The third: talisman for rescuing and protecting the body and life. The fourth: talisman for preventing the mutual conflict of metal, wood, water, fire, and earth. The fifth: divine talisman against the death-ghosts and wasting spirits that are recorded over living people. All evil things that encounter the talisman scatter and are extinguished.

Urgent, urgent, in accordance with the statutes and ordinances!

The general is in the *zǐ* position. The twentieth day, *guǐchǒu*, of the sixth month, *rénzǐ* new moon, at the *kāibái* hour. The talisman of the divine thousand buddhas protects and petitions for one hundred years. Whether the month is in decline or the day is in decline, whether the year is in decline or the hour is in decline, with day-taboos: after wearing the talisman, there are no more declines or taboos.

The talisman of the thousand buddhas suppresses and causes the person to pass through calamity, overcome difficulty, extend their years, and increase their reckoning. After receiving the talisman, lifespan is prolonged. The divine talisman of the seven thousand buddhas petitions for life. I pray to receive one hundred and twenty years, attaining a full reckoning.

For the body of the one born: after wearing the talisman, all buddhas give me birth, all sūtras carry me, radiant light illuminates me, yin and yang nourish me, the four seasons sustain me, the five great generals lead me, the five-colored clouds canopy me, bodhisattvas protect me, the six hundred *jiǎzǐ* cycles shelter me, the five emperors assist me, the five tones delight me, the five weapons guard me, the stars and constellations align for me, the five-colored threads depend on me, the five fragrances perfume me, a golden bed receives me, a brocade quilt covers me, immortals support me, jade maidens attend me, the Azure Dragon leads me, the White Tiger flanks me, dukes and marquises cause people to receive me, the five surname-clans respect me, the myriad clans honor me, elders follow me, and neighbors and villages cherish me.

Bows, arrows, blades, weapons, and bandits leave me. Tigers, wolves, and venomous snakes avoid me. The five poisons and *gǔ*-sorcery avoid my body.

After wearing the talisman, heaven opens in all four directions. The hundred ghosts do not approach. Falling into water, I do not drown. Blades and weapons do not harm me. Going out and coming in are auspicious. I do not encounter misfortune or calamity. The generals of the four quarters and five peaks protect me. The Way is fully equipped. Protection is proclaimed before me. The worthy and wise respect me. Good medicine is given me. Divine talismans protect me. The hundred ghosts avoid me. The hundred spirits love me. The heavenly gate opens for me. The earthly door lets me out. Cool springs refresh me. Wind, frost, rain, and the rain-master clear the road.

Those who face the talisman die. Those who oppose the talisman perish. The talisman suppresses the ghosts of death-pollution, old and new possession, male and female calamity-breaking, and chain-linked haunting. The talisman suppresses the ghosts of mountains, forests, and local shrines. The talisman suppresses the ghosts that roam the heavens. The talisman suppresses the red-tongued ghosts. The talisman suppresses the ghosts of the five earths of neighboring houses. The talisman suppresses the ghosts that roam the daily earthly vapors. The talisman suppresses the ghosts of stellar death. The talisman suppresses the ghosts of travelers who died abroad. The talisman suppresses the ghosts of those who died by weapons or in prison.

After the disciple wears the talisman of the thousand buddhas: going out in all four directions, a thousand roads are without limit, ten thousand roads are without difficulty. Lying down, there are no evil dreams. What is sought is always obtained. What is wished for follows the heart. The dharma of the thousand buddhas is correct, in accordance with the talisman.

Commanded: urgent, urgent, in accordance with the statutes and ordinances!
