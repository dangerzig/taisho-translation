# 金剛頂經觀自在王如來修行法
## Practice Method of Avalokiteśvara-rāja Tathāgata from the Vajraśekhara Sūtra

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0931

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Amoghavajra (不空)

---

I now expound the essentials of the Vajra-Lotus Dharma method based on the *Vajraśekhara Sūtra*. One must first enter the abhiṣeka samaya-maṇḍala, generate the great bodhicitta, renounce body, life, and possessions for the benefit of all, practice with courageous vigor in accordance with each thought, and maintain loving-kindness, compassion, sympathetic joy, and equanimity without interruption. Only such a person should undertake this practice. The methods for the maṇḍala, painted images, and so forth are as extensively taught in other sūtras.

Whenever entering the vihāra to perform recitation, first place the five limbs upon the ground and bow to the principal deity, Avalokiteśvara-rāja Tathāgata. Next, bow to Amoghasiddhi Tathāgata of the north, and then to Akṣobhya, Ratnasaṃbhava, and Vairocana Tathāgata. Pay homage to all according to the Dharma with utmost sincerity. Kneeling on both knees with lotus-palms joined, confess the transgressions of the three karmas:

> "Since beginningless kalpas I have sunk in the ocean of birth-and-death.
> Now, with a pure mind, I openly confess and repent.
> As all the Buddhas have confessed, I now do likewise.
> May I and all sentient beings become entirely pure."

The mantra:

*Oṃ svabhāva-śuddhaḥ sarva-dharma svabhāva-śuddho 'haṃ*

Next, one should rejoice in the merit and wisdom accumulated by all the Buddhas and bodhisattvas of the past, present, and future:

> "The Buddhas of the three times, the bodhisattvas, and all sentient beings:
> All the roots of merit they have gathered, with palms joined, I entirely rejoice."

Next, with the right knee on the ground and open palms joined above the crown, visualize bowing at the feet of all the Buddhas, Tathāgatas, and bodhisattvas. Recite the mantra:

*Oṃ padma-vīta*

Having bowed to all the Buddhas, sit according to the seat-seal. Enter concentration and contemplate innumerable Tathāgatas pervading the Dharma-realm, with oneself present in that assembly. Then form the Secret Samaya Seal: the firm binding, with the ring-fingers and little-fingers of both hands joined and raised upright. With this, awaken and alert the holy assembly. Recite the mantra:

*Oṃ vajra-padma samaya-sattvaṃ*

Next, form the seal of All Tathāgatas' Joyful Delight Samaya. Same as the previous seal, but bend the middle fingers into the palms. The mantra:

*Oṃ samaya-hṛsūrata-sattvaṃ*

Next, form the Open-Heart Seal. Contemplate upon the two breasts the syllable *trāṭ*. With the vajra binding, clap three times to open. The mantra:

*Oṃ vajra-bandha trāṭ*

Next, form the Entering-Wisdom-Syllable Seal. Before one's face, contemplate a red lotus upon which rests the syllable *sa*. With the firm binding, the ring-fingers and forefingers pressed together, visualize picking up that syllable and placing it within the heart. Recite the mantra:

*Oṃ vajra-veśa aḥ*

Next, form the Closing-Wisdom-Syllable Seal. Without changing the previous seal, the forefingers press against the back of the ring-fingers. Visualize closing the heart-gate. The mantra:

*Oṃ vajra-muṣṭi vaṃ*

Next, form the Expulsion and Boundary-Sealing Seal. The middle fingers cross right over left externally; the ring-fingers press together; the forefingers hook the middle fingers; the thumbs pinch the tips of the index fingers. The mantra:

*Oṃ amṛta-udbhava hūṃ phaṭ*

With this seal, rotate to the left three times to expel the unwholesome; rotate to the right three times to seal the boundary at whatever distance one wishes.

Next, form the Samādhi Seal. Both palms face up and cross; the forefingers stand upright facing each other; the thumbs pinch the tips of the forefingers. Place upon the lap. Recite the mantra:

*Oṃ samādhi-padma-nāma hrīḥ*

With each breath, in and out, observe clearly. Contemplate innumerable Buddhas in empty space! At that moment, all snap their fingers to alert the practitioner, saying: "How will you now accomplish unsurpassed enlightenment, not knowing the Tathāgatas' essential Dharma of true reality?" The practitioner, hearing this alert, addresses the Buddhas: "What is called true reality? May the Tathāgatas explain it for me." At that time all the Buddhas tell the practitioner: "Excellent, excellent! You are able to ask thus. Visualize the syllable *aḥ* that was placed within your heart, shining and pervading the heart. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ citta-prativediṃ-karumi*

"Silently recite once, then visualize it becoming a moon-disc as though within a light mist. In order to make the moon-disc pure, recite this mantra:

"*Oṃ bodhi-citta-utpādayāmi*

"Having recited, carefully contemplate the heart-moon, making it supremely pure, like dwelling in the great void, vast and without blemish. Then upon the heart-moon, visualize a lotus. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ tiṣṭha-vajra-padma*

"Visualize the lotus and moon gradually expanding, pervading the Dharma-realm, their extent equal to empty space. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ spharaṇa-vajra-padma*

"From within the expanding lotus, boundless light radiates, and flowing with the light, boundless, limitless Sukhāvatī worlds appear. Each world is wondrously adorned with precious jewels, and in each, Avalokiteśvara-rāja Tathāgata is surrounded by the holy assembly in front and behind. Having thus contemplated, gradually contract the lotus. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ saṃhāra-padma*

"As all the Buddhas and Tathāgatas of all worlds are contracted with the lotus, their extent returns to that of one's own body. Then transform one's own body into Amitāyus Buddha, whose body is the color of red crystal, radiating great light, forming the samādhi seal upon a vajra-lotus seat. Having visualized oneself as the Tathāgata, and fearing that distraction might cause regression, next form the empowerment seal to empower oneself. With the firm binding, bend the forefingers like lotus petals. The thumbs stand upright. Seal the heart, forehead, throat, and crown. At each place sealed, visualize the syllable *hrīḥ*. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ vajra-dharma hrīḥ*

"Next, form the Abhiṣeka Seal. With lotus-palms joined, the forefingers press together like a jewel, with the index fingers raised and separated. Place upon the forehead. The mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-ratna-abhiṣiñca sarvaṃ hrīḥ trāṃ*

"Next, form the Lotus-Garland Seal. With the previous seal, divide the hands from the forehead and encircle to the back of the crown, forming lotus-fists, as if tying a flower-garland. Slowly bring them forward and down, releasing them. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-ratna-māle vaṃ*

"Next, form the Armor Seal. Form lotus-fists and encircle the body front and back as if donning armor. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ abhaya-padma-kavaca-mandhara-rakṣa vaṃ hūṃ haṃ*

"Next, form the Joy Seal. With lotus-palms joined, gently clap three times. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-tuṣya hu*

"Then before oneself, contemplate the world of Sukhāvatī: the ground is lapis lazuli, and there is an ocean of merit-milk. Within that ocean, contemplate the syllable *hrīḥ* transforming into a subtle and wondrous blossoming lotus, which then transforms into Avalokiteśvara-rāja Tathāgata, with colors and adornments as in the previous body-contemplation.

"Next, form the Lotus-Hook Seal. With lotus-palms joined, the forefingers bent like hooks. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-aṃkuśa hūṃ jaḥ*

"Next, form the Lotus-Lasso Seal. With the previous seal, the forefingers bent like rings. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-amogha-pāśa hūṃ*

"Next, form the Lotus-Chain Seal. With lotus-palms joined, the ring-fingers and thumbs pinch each other, the forefingers and little-fingers likewise hook together to form a chain. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-sphoṭa vaṃ*

"Next, form the Lotus-Bell Seal. With lotus-palms joined, the thumbs enter the center and the forefingers form rings. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-veśa aḥ*

"Next, recite the Lotus Division's Hundred-Syllable Mantra. Hold an argha vessel containing fragrant water mixed with saffron and camphor, and make offerings. Recite the hundred-syllable mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-sattva samayam anupālaya padma-sattva tvenopatiṣṭha dṛḍho me bhava sutoṣyo me bhava anurakto me bhava supoṣyo me bhava sarva-siddhiṃ me prayaccha sarva-karma su-ca me cittaṃ śrīyaḥ kuru hūṃ ha ha ha ha hoḥ bhagavan sarva-tathāgata-padma mā me muñca padma-me bhava mahā-samaya-sattva aḥ*

"Next, form the Lotus Inner Four Offerings, the Play Seal. With lotus-palms joined, the thumbs raised upright. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-lāsye hu*

"Next, form the Lotus-Garland Seal. With the previous seal, extend both arms. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-māle trāṭ*

"Next, form the Lotus-Song Seal. With lotus-palms joined, bend and touch each finger, moving from the navel upward to the mouth, then release. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-gīte gī*

"Next, form the Lotus-Dance Seal. With lotus-palms joined, rotate left and right, then release above the crown. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-nṛtye kṛṭa*

"Next, form the Lotus Outer Four Offerings, the Incense Seal. With lotus-palms joined, release downward as if burning incense. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-dhūpe aḥ*

"Next, form the Lotus Flower-Scattering Seal. With lotus-palms joined, scatter forward and upward as if scattering flowers. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-puṣpe oṃ*

"Next, form the Lotus Lamp Seal. With lotus-palms joined, the thumbs raised close together. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-dīpe nī*

"Next, form the Lotus Unguent Seal. With all ten fingers in lotus-palms joined at the heart, part and scatter as if applying unguent. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ padma-gandha-nyak*

"Next, form the Root Seal. With the firm binding, the ring-fingers press together like lotus-petal form. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ lokeśvara-rāja hrīḥ*

"Having formed the root seal and recited the mantra seven times, then take up lotus-seed beads and perform the corresponding recitation. During the four daily periods, practice according to one's capacity. When one wishes to stop, again form the root seal, recite the root dhāraṇī seven more times, then form the eight offering seals, and afterwards dismiss the principal deity.

"The Dismissal Seal: with the previous root seal, move from the navel to the face and release, joining the palms above the crown. Visualize Avalokiteśvara-rāja Tathāgata returning to his original palace. Recite the mantra:

"*Oṃ vajra-padma muḥ*

"Having dismissed the principal deity, make vows as one wishes. Then with the armor seal, protect the body. With the Hayagrīva Vidyārāja boundary-seal, rotate to the left to release the boundary. Walk about freely to any pure place and read the great Mahāyāna sūtras: the *Mahāprajñāpāramitā*, the *Avataṃsaka*, the *Nirvāṇa*, and the *Laṅkāvatāra*. In walking, standing, sitting, and lying down, constantly recollect the principal deity without interruption."
