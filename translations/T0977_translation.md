# 佛說大白傘蓋總持陀羅尼經
## The Great White Umbrella Canopy Dhāraṇī Sūtra Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0977

Translated from the Chinese. Yuan dynasty, translated by Zhēnzhì (真智) and others.

---

Homage to all most excellent supreme masters!

### Practice Instructions

One who wishes to practice the White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother should, in a quiet room, sit upon a soft and comfortable cushion. Then generate the aspiration: "For the sake of all sentient beings in the six realms of existence, that they may be liberated from the cycle of rebirth, may I attain perfect, complete enlightenment." Having generated this aspiration, visualize the White Umbrella Canopy buddha-assembly in the empty space before you. With a sincere mind, recite the three refuges before them. The buddha-assembly then dissolves into light that merges into oneself, and oneself becomes light.

Then recite the *svabhāva* mantra and visualize that all is empty. Within that emptiness, upon a flower and moon disk, visualize a white syllable *oṃ*. The syllable *oṃ* emits light, and the light returns. The seed-syllable transforms into a white umbrella with a golden handle. Upon the handle is adorned the syllable *oṃ*, which emits light and returns. The seed-syllable transforms into the White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother. She has one face, two arms, and three eyes. She sits in the vajra cross-legged posture. Her right hand forms the fearlessness mudrā. Her left hand holds a white umbrella at her chest. She is adorned with various necklaces. Her body is as white as sunlight shining upon a snow mountain. She displays a joyful countenance, manifesting the nature of emptiness. One should contemplate her like an image in a mirror.

When about to recite the dhāraṇī, visualize upon the lotus and sun disk in one's own heart the syllable *oṃ*, surrounded by the heart-dhāraṇī and the long and short dhāraṇīs, all circling there. They emit light that dispels all karmic obstructions of oneself and others. Having thus visualized, including the intervals, then proceed to recite.

When fatigued and wishing to make a food offering, place the offering food before you. Recite the three-syllable mantra *oṃ āḥ hūṃ* to consecrate it, transforming it into nectar. In the empty space before you, invoke the White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother as chief, together with the twenty-two mound-dwelling ḍākinīs, the seven buddhas, the perfectly enlightened ones of the ten directions, the three beautiful pure ones with Brahmā and Śakra as their retinue. Visualize the tongue transforming into a vajra light-tube, and recite the food-offering mantra:

*Oṃ sarva tathāgatoṣṇīṣa-sitātapatre hūṃ phaṭ dāṃ māri khā khā khahi khahi*

Recite three or five times, then recite the praise and prayer. When the petition and vow-requests are complete, send off the buddha-assembly. Discard the offering food in a clean place and dedicate the merit.

*Ārya tathāgatoṣṇīṣa-sitātapatre namāḥ amṛta-cittarāṇi*

### Sūtra

Homage to the Holy One Emerging from the Uṣṇīṣa of All Tathāgatas, the White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother, She Whom None Can Rival. Homage to the Most Supreme Three Treasures.

Thus have I heard:

At one time the Bhagavān was dwelling in the Heaven of the Thirty-Three, in the place of excellent dharma where the virtuous devas reside, together with a great assembly of monks, great bodhisattva heroes, and the retinue of Śakra, lord of the devas.

At that time the Bhagavān, seated upon a lotus seat, entered the samādhi of the universal-observing uṣṇīṣa. Instantly, from his uṣṇīṣa, the Bhagavān produced this complete dhāraṇī, the secret mantra practice:

Homage to the Perfectly Enlightened One and all the bodhisattva heroes. Homage to the Perfectly Enlightened One. Homage to the Wondrous Dharma. Homage to the Great Assembly. Homage to the seven koṭis of truly, perfectly enlightened ones and their śrāvaka assemblies. Homage to all the world's conquerors of enemies. Homage to all stream-enterers. Homage to all once-returners. Homage to all non-returners. Homage to all who have truly transcended the world. Homage to all who have entered reality. Homage to the celestial ṛṣis, the mantra-wielders, and those endowed with the power of blessing. Homage to all who recite and hold the vidyā-mantras and have attained accomplishment. Homage to Brahmā. Homage to Śakra. Homage to the lord of fierce beauty who can subdue and defeat ascetic practice. Homage to the one of beautiful form with five mudrā seals, the refuge of the Sonless One. Homage to the one of beautiful form who can destroy the three-layered fortress, dwelling in the charnel grounds, the refuge of all the ḍākinīs. Homage to the Bhagavān, the Tathāgata-family Buddha. Homage to the Lotus-family Buddha. Homage to the Vajra-family Buddha. Homage to the Jewel-family Buddha. Homage to the Great Elephant-family Buddha. Homage to the Youth-family Buddha. Homage to the Nāga-family Buddha. Homage to the Heroic-Firm-Division Weapon-King Buddha. Homage to Amitābha Buddha. Homage to Akṣobhya Buddha. Homage to Bhaiṣajyaguru Vaiḍūrya-prabha-rāja Buddha. Homage to the Śāla-Lord King of Fully Ripened Flowers and Fruit Buddha. Homage to Śākyamuni Buddha. Homage to Ratnottama-rāja Buddha. Homage to the Most Excellent Samantabhadra Buddha. Homage to Vidyādhipati Buddha. Homage to the Full-Eyed Utpala-Fragrance Supreme King Buddha.

Having paid homage at their places, the Bhagavatī, the White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother emerging from the uṣṇīṣa of all tathāgatas, She Whom None Can Rival, the Great Averter: by this she decisively defeats all invading evil māras. She can also decisively defeat all other vidyā-mantras. She can also avert untimely death. She can also liberate sentient beings from all bondage. She can also avert all hostile and malicious evil dreams. She can also crush the eighty-four thousand evil māras. She can also gladden the twenty-eight lunar mansions. She can also subdue the eight great asterisms. She can also avert all enemies. She can also crush the most extremely violent and malicious evil dreams. She can also rescue from the calamities of poison, weapons, water, and fire.

> She Whom None Can Rival, Great Firm Mother,
> Great Stalwart Mother, Great Power Mother,
> Great Blazing Mother, Great Majestic Mother,
> Great White Canopy Mother, Great Strength Mother,
> Blazing Garland White-Robed Mother, Holy Tārā Wrathful-Browed,
> Victorious Vajra Named Rosary, Lotus Radiance Vajra Named,
> She Whom None Can Rival with Rosary, Vajra Wall and Crusher Mother,
> Gentle Buddha Offering Mother, Gentle Form Majestic Great Mother,
> Holy Tārā Great Power Mother, Deathless Vajra Iron-Chain Mother,
> Vajra Youth Seed-Holding Mother, Vajra-Hand Lineage Golden Rosary,
> Great Red and Jewel-Pearl Mother, Lineage Vidyā Vajra Named Uṣṇīṣa,
> Lineage-Mark Graceful Vajra Mother, Golden-Light-Hued Eyed Mother,
> Vajra Torch and White-Hued Mother, Lotus-Eyed and Moonlight Mother.

By the power of all their assembled mudrās, may they protect me! Protect me!

*Oṃ ṛṣi-gaṇa-pra-śasta ya tathāgatoṣṇīṣa-sitātapatre hūṃ ṭhrūṃ jambha-nadhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ sitātapatra-nadhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ mahā-vidya samaśagandhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ para-vidyā samaśagandhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ sarva-śastra-nistāra-nadhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ sarva-yakṣa-rākṣasa-graha-na-vi saṇḍhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ cakra-stena-graha-sahasrā-vi saṇḍhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ aṣṭa-jñāte na-sahuddha-na-māprasataṇḍhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ aśita-namā-graha-na-saṇḍhakara hūṃ ṭhrūṃ rākṣa rākṣa* protect me!

The Bhagavatī, the White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother emerging from the uṣṇīṣa of all tathāgatas, the Great Averter of the vajra-uṣṇīṣa, she of a thousand great arms, a thousand great heads, ten million koṭi eyes, non-dual, blazing, endowed with all marks, the vajra vast great white one, sovereign ruler of the three realms: at all times protect me! Protect me!

*Oṃ:* From the calamity of kings, the calamity of thieves, of fire, water, poison, weapons, foreign armies, famine, enemies, epidemics, thunderbolts, untimely death, earthquakes, shooting stars, royal punishment, devas, nāgas, lightning, flying spirits, and enraged wild beasts;

And from the māras of devas, nāgas, asuras, wind spirits, flying spirits, gandharvas, kumbhāṇḍas, great-bellied serpents, vighnas, yakṣas, hungry ghosts, ḍākinīs, flesh-eating spirits, vetāḍas, bottle-spirits, foul-smelling spirits, body-odor spirits, emaciation spirits, forgetfulness spirits, madness spirits, nightmare spirits, subjugation spirits, the Kṛttikā-asterism spirits, from all of these may I obtain safety and auspiciousness!

And from the glory-stealing ghosts, beauty-stealing ghosts, womb-eating ghosts, blood-drinking ghosts, afterbirth-eating ghosts, flesh-eating ghosts, fat-eating ghosts, marrow-eating ghosts, membrane-eating ghosts, life-stealing ghosts, vomit-eating ghosts, excrement-eating ghosts, urine-eating ghosts, discharge-eating ghosts, remnant-eating ghosts, saliva-eating ghosts, mucus-eating ghosts, drool-eating ghosts, pus-eating ghosts, offering-eating ghosts, garland-eating ghosts, fragrance-eating ghosts, incense-eating ghosts, mind-stealing ghosts, flower-eating ghosts, fruit-eating ghosts, crop-eating ghosts, and fire-offering-eating ghosts, from among all these may I obtain safety and auspiciousness!

All of these and all the vidyā-mantras created by all māras, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by wandering spirits, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by ḍākas and ḍākinīs, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by Maheśvara, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by the Sonless One, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by flying spirits and truth-practitioners, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by Mahākāla and the ḍākinīs, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by skull-bowl bearers, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by victory-givers, honey-makers, and fulfillers of purpose, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by the four sisters, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by Piśācī, the Joy-Lord, and the Assembly-Lord, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by the Impure One, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by the Enemy-Destroyer, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by the Passionless One, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra! The vidyā-mantras created by the Secret Lord Vajrapāṇi, decisively defeat and strike them with the vajra!

Homage to the Bhagavatī, the White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother emerging from the uṣṇīṣa of all tathāgatas. Protect me! Protect me!

[Main dhāraṇī]

*Oṃ asitānalarkha-pra-masiputañjā-satasmāpatra ṭhrūṃ ṭhrūṃ khā khā ra ra mi mi jina jina mina mina hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ phaṭ svāhā hin hin phaṭ ho ho phaṭ amukīṃ phaṭ apratihata phaṭ pramārata phaṭ asura-vidrapramākha phaṭ sarva-deva-bhaṭ sarva-deva-bījña-bhaṭ sarva-asurī-bhaṭ sarva-marunī-bhaṭ sarva-garuḍī-bhaṭ sarva-kinnara-bhaṭ sarva-siddhira-bhaṭ sarva-mahoranī-bhaṭ sarva-yakṣīṃ-bhaṭ sarva-rākṣasa-sīṃ-bhaṭ sarva-bhūtī-bhaṭ sarva-piśācī-bhaṭ sarva-kumbhāṇḍī-bhaṭ sarva-pūtanī-bhaṭ sarva-kaṭapūtanī-bhaṭ sarva-skandanī-bhaṭ sarva-unmādanī-bhaṭ sarva-cchāyī-bhaṭ sarva-apasmārī-bhaṭ sarva-śākinī-bhaṭ sarva-ḍākinī-bhaṭ sarva-revatī-bhaṭ sarva-jāmikī-bhaṭ sarva-kṛtya-kārmāṇi-kagoṭī-bhaṭ graha-dviṣṭa-bhaṭ cit-sañ-prajāka-saṇḍegoṭī-bhaṭ darpaguṭī-bhaṭ sarva-tīrthika-bhaṭ sarva-śramaṇī-bhaṭ sarva-jñātya-bhaṭ jaya-karmākaraya-bhaṭ sarva-adhā-sataṃjña-bhaṭ vicāri-bhaṭ jamemavaganīṃ-bhaṭ sarva-gaumārīṃ-bhaṭ jñātrāṇīṃ-bhaṭ mahā-pratyagīrī-bhaṭ vajra-śaṅkhalaya-pratyagīra-rājaya-bhaṭ mahā-kālaya-mātrī-gaṇḍa-namaskṛtaṃ-bhaṭ*

*jñāja-bīṃ-bhaṭ prahevāniya-bhaṭ agaiṇīṃ-bhaṭ mahā-kārīṃ-bhaṭ kālatīṃ-bhaṭ patrīṃ-bhaṭ dātrīṃ-bhaṭ gaumīrīṃ-bhaṭ praṇe-bhaṭ camatīṃ-bhaṭ raḍatrīṃ-bhaṭ kāla-raḍatrīṃ-bhaṭ yamadattīṃ-bhaṭ kapārīṃ-bhaṭ atimavati-sma-jñāna-māṣināṃ-bhaṭ*

May all sentient beings who harbor hostile minds toward me, who harbor violent minds, who steal vital force, and also the beauty-stealing ghosts, womb-eating ghosts, blood-drinking ghosts, fat-drinking ghosts, flesh-eating ghosts, fat-eating ghosts, marrow-eating ghosts, afterbirth-eating ghosts, life-stealing ghosts, vomit-eating ghosts, excrement-eating ghosts, urine-eating ghosts, discharge-eating ghosts, remnant-eating ghosts, drool-eating ghosts, mucus-eating ghosts, saliva-craving ghosts, pus-eating ghosts, offering-eating ghosts, garland-eating ghosts, fragrance-eating ghosts, incense-eating ghosts, flower-eating ghosts, fruit-eating ghosts, crop-eating ghosts, fire-offering-eating ghosts, those with sinful minds, those with hostile minds, those with violent minds; and also all the māras of devas, nāgas, asuras, wind-spirits, flying spirits, gandharvas, kumbhāṇḍas, great-bellied serpents, vighnas, yakṣas, hungry ghosts, flesh-eating spirits, vetāḍas, bottle-spirits, foul-smelling spirits, body-odor spirits, emaciation spirits, madness spirits, enchantment spirits, forgetfulness spirits, subjugation spirits, ḍākinī spirits, Kṛttikā spirits, Yama spirits, ḍākinī delight spirits, wandering garland spirits, khaṇḍa spirits; and also one-day fevers and all two-day, three-day, four-day, seven-day fevers, perpetual fevers, unending fevers, skin diseases, diseases arising from wind, from bile, from phlegm, from their combination, and all bodily diseases: may they all be dispelled!

And also diseases of the limbs, loss of appetite, eye diseases, nose diseases, mouth diseases, neck diseases, heart diseases, throat diseases, ear diseases, tooth diseases, heart-burning diseases, brain diseases, rib diseases, spinal diseases, abdominal diseases, waist diseases, rectal diseases, thigh diseases, shin diseases, hand diseases, foot diseases, joint diseases, and multi-limb diseases: may they all be dispelled! May they all be protected!

The Great White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother, the Great Averter of the vajra-uṣṇīṣa, within twelve yojanas, all vetāḍas, reanimated corpses, ḍākinīs, and also epidemics, scabies, itching sores, pox, leprosy, chapping sores, hemorrhoids, burns, boils, cysts; and also the terror of emaciation, jewel-poison, compound poisons, enchantment-poison, and the dangers of fire and water; and also fighting, enmity, harm, and untimely death; and also trimāpukā worms, trirāta worms, vipers, scorpions, lizards, snakes, rats, wolves, lions, tigers, bears, wild wolves, water beasts, gadflies, and biting flies that take the lives of others: the vidyā-mantras of all these are bound! All their ritual actions are bound! The vidyā-mantras of all others are bound! The vidyā-mantras of all māras are all bound!

*Tadyathā oṃ anale anale viṣade viṣade vīra vajrini-māmā vajrapāṇi phaṭ hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ phaṭ*

*Hūṃ ṭhrūṃ bandha phaṭ svāhā*

### Benefits

Any practitioner who writes this White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother Whom None Can Rival, the Great Averter emerging from the uṣṇīṣa of all tathāgatas, on birch bark, white cotton, or tree bark, and either wears it on their body or around their neck, shall for their entire life be unharmed by poison, uninjured by weapons, unburned by fire, unswept away by water, unaffected by jewel-poison, unharmed by compound poisons, undamaged by mantra-poison, and untouched by untimely death. All enemy māras and all malicious companions, wherever they may be, shall feel love and reverence. Furthermore, eighty-four thousand vajra-lineage beings as numerous as the sands of the Ganges shall also protect, rescue, and shelter such a person, holding them in loving reverence.

Furthermore, for eighty-four thousand great kalpas one will attain the knowledge of past lives. In every birth, one will not receive the body of a vighna, rākṣasa, hungry ghost, foul-smelling spirit, or body-odor spirit. One will not receive a body of poverty in the human realm. One will be endowed with the merit and fortune of immeasurable, countless buddhas as numerous as the sands of the Ganges.

Furthermore, one who constantly upholds this White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother Whom None Can Rival, the Great Averter emerging from the uṣṇīṣa of all tathāgatas: even without practicing celibacy, celibacy will be accomplished; even without patience, patience will be accomplished; even without purity, purity will be established; without the upavāsa precepts, the upavāsa precepts will be obtained; without observing fasting precepts, fasting precepts will be accomplished. Even if one has committed the five heinous sins, they will be completely purified without remainder, and all past karmic obstructions will be entirely eliminated.

If a woman who desires a child can uphold this White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother Whom None Can Rival, she will obtain a child endowed with long life, merit, and power. After death, she will be reborn in the Land of Ultimate Bliss.

Furthermore, for human illness, cattle illness, livestock epidemics, harm, obstructions, fighting, and warfare with any and all armies: by placing this White Umbrella Canopy Buddha-Mother Whom None Can Rival, the Great Averter emerging from the uṣṇīṣa of all tathāgatas, upon a banner-pole, making extensive offerings, and setting the banner at the gate of a great city, within a palace, within a village quarter, within a settlement, upon a plain, or in a secluded place, and making extensive offerings before She Whom None Can Rival, the Great Averter, one can swiftly bring peace to the realm and subdue epidemics, obstructions, harm, fighting, and all enemy armies.

### Closing

One should practice the full-moon meditation. All the nāga kings and others will send timely rains. The perfectly enlightened ones, the bodhisattva heroes, the devas, asuras, humans, gandharvas, and all beings of the world greatly rejoiced, and praised what the Bhagavān had spoken.
