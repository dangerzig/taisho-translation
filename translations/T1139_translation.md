# 護命法門神呪經
## Sūtra of the Divine Spells of the Dharma-Gate for Protecting Life

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1139

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Bodhiruci (菩提流志).

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time, the Bhagavān was at the great caitya where he attained perfect enlightenment beneath the bodhi tree, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus. Among them were the Elder Śāriputra, the Elder Mahāmaudgalyāyana, and the Elder Ānanda; these great śrāvakas served as the foremost. He was also together with a great assembly of bodhisattvas: Vajradhvaja Bodhisattva, Vajragarbha Bodhisattva, Vajrapāṇi Bodhisattva, and Maitreya Bodhisattva; these and all the bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the Bhadrakalpa served as the foremost.

At that time, Vajrapāṇi, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! May the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, for the sake of all sentient beings, teach the sublime dharma-gate for protecting life. Through this dharma-gate, may it serve as a great illumination and a great sheltering protection for all sentient beings, so that they may attain fearlessness. May all enemies and evil companions, whether devas, nāgas, yakṣas, rākṣasas, bhūtas, ghosts, kumbhāṇḍas, humans, or non-humans, be forever unable to give rise to evil intentions, to cause disturbance, or to steal their vital essence. If they encounter swords, poisoned food or drink, sorcerous curses, or other such afflictions, these shall be unable to cause harm. For all beings, whether walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, intoxicated, or waking, in all places I shall provide protection.

"May the World-Honored One, for the benefit of all sentient beings, teach these various divine spells. Just as I always attend upon and serve the Tathāgata without ever departing, so too shall I protect all sentient beings in the same manner: at all times, for their welfare, for their happiness, for their benefit. Therefore I now request the Tathāgata to protect all good men and good women who abide in the bodhisattva vehicle, so that they may be free from quarrels, insults, slander, and grudges in their hearts, and from all illnesses and untimely, premature death."

After Vajrapāṇi, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, had made this request, the Bhagavān addressed Vajrapāṇi Bodhisattva, saying: "Excellent, excellent! Good man! You have requested the Tathāgata for the benefit of all sentient beings. Listen now attentively and reflect well upon it. I shall now teach you the dharma-gate for protecting life, called the Gate of Goodness:

*Tadyathā: uku maku ukumā-bhinī bandhanī bhāra-camanī bhā-hurī bhā-hurī hu hu hurī bhaṃdreya svāhā.*

At that time, after the Tathāgata had spoken this Abhiṣeka Dhāraṇī Spell, the World-Honored One further spoke the Dhāraṇī Spell of the Longevity Dharma-Gate Protected by All Buddhas:

*Tadyathā: vajre vajre vajre vajre vajra-dhārī vajra-bhāti vajra-gati vajra-dāte cakra-vajre cakra-dāte made dāte dāte bhide bhide made cide huhu de bhaṅga-bhibhide śide de de śi-jujude ruru-ji-bhāde cari-jude made cade maṇḍade maṇḍanī sarva-pāpa-bhināśanī sarva-rūga-praśamanī ākāra-mṛju-pratiṣṭhādanī yābhaju-bhāva-puna-punā-tiṣṭhādanī.*

*Tadyathā: maṇḍanī gagāranī modhanī saṃpūrṇa-modhanī nāśanī saṃpra-nāśanī sarva-vidhi-pūrṇa-śamanī yābhadu-svabhāva-puna ākāra-mṛju-pūrṇa-tiṣṭhādanī.*

*Tadyathā: nāśanī nāśanī bandhanī bandhanī chinda chinda bhide bhide sade ha-tadne bhade bhāde bhāde vide vide vide vide uṣāde śānde bhārya-bharūne bhārya-kaṭhaye bhārya-harmā-cāriṇī indrā-bhati dhi-dhira-yanī nadmo-mahīśvara rāye nī hṛṣī-bha-jaḍanī pāpa-jālanī kāra-bhādinī bhūta-bhādinī sattva-gati suma-bhatī suma-pūrṇa-bhī svāhā.*

*Tadyathā: hara hara hirī svāhā.*

*Tadyathā: gajita-pajita āyena ade cambhadane aikā-kaḍane putra-ḍahanī mārga-bhirūhanī husa-bhati husa-bhati pusa-bhati pusa-bhati hirī hirī yathā jaya yathā agni yathā pāñcaya yathā bhakaṃ yathā bhajarāṃ yathā haridakaṃ.*

The Buddha told Vajrapāṇi, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva: "This Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this divine dhāraṇī spell, was previously taught by past tathāgatas, arhats, and perfectly enlightened ones, who previously provided protection and established a great vow of truth. I now also, for the benefit and compassion of all sentient beings, establish a great vow of truth. Therefore I teach this dhāraṇī spell."

At that time, the Bhagavān emitted the light of the great person's mark from between his brows, and this light illuminated all buddha-lands everywhere. Through the Buddha's spiritual transformation, all the tathāgatas in those lands, seeing this light, each simultaneously set forth from their own lands toward the Sahā world and came to the presence of Śākyamuni Buddha. They praised him, saying: "Excellent, excellent, Śākyamuni Buddha! You are able, for the benefit and protection of all sentient beings, to teach this dharma-gate dhāraṇī spell, which can serve as a great illumination, bringing joy and right mindfulness. Therefore we now offer praise and commendation.

"If any good man or good woman, at the morning hour, upholds and recites the meanings and phrases of this dhāraṇī divine spell, they shall obtain twenty kinds of merit:

1. They will be embraced by all buddhas.
2. They will always be remembered by all buddhas.
3. They will permanently leave behind the karma of evil destinies.
4. They will attain long life and wealth.
5. They will attain widespread fame.
6. They will attain courageous and mighty power.
7. They will always be free from illness and furthermore be diligent.
8. They will always be sheltered and protected by all buddhas.
9. They will furthermore be remembered by all devas.
10. They will be diligent in cultivating virtuous conduct.
11. They will attain radiance, joy, and right mindfulness.
12. They will attain the complete marks.
13. They will attain fearlessness.
14. They will attain the full perfection of śīla.
15. They will attain the accomplishment of all good roots.
16. They will be guarded by all devas.
17. They will be guarded by all nāgas.
18. They will be guarded by all yakṣas.
19. They will be honored and loved by all in the world.
20. They will swiftly attain anuttarā-samyak-saṃbodhi."

The Buddha told Vajrapāṇi: "This dhāraṇī spell that I have now taught is the secret treasury of all the Buddha-Tathāgatas, the Arhats, the Perfectly Enlightened Ones, and is guarded by all buddhas."

At that time, Vajrapāṇi, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this dhāraṇī spell, rising at the morning hour to recite it, World-Honored One, I shall always guard such persons and keep them free from all harm.

*Tadyathā: śikhi śikhi śikhi śikhi ji ji ji ji bha bha bha bha hulu lulu lulu dhi dhi dhi dhi ghara ghara ghara ghara huḍi huḍi huḍi huḍi hā hā hā hā ma ma ma manā manā hānā hā me sarva-pratyarthika-dahā-dahā me sarva-śatru-bha-pacca-pacca me sarvāhite śiṇā svāhā.*

At that time, the Bhagavān praised Vajrapāṇi, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, saying: "Excellent, excellent, good man! You have now spoken this dhāraṇī divine spell, which accomplishes the supreme and is of great benefit. For the protection of all sentient beings, I bestow my radiant power within every one of their pores to bring them comfort and happiness."

At that time, the lord of the Sahā world, Brahmā, rose from his seat, joined his palms toward the Buddha in reverence, and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the benefit and protection of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī divine spell:

*Tadyathā: hide miri jirī svāhā. Brahma puḍe svāhā. Brahma svāre svāhā. Brahma bhe sūga bhe puṣpa-saṃsāre svāhā.*

"World-Honored One! These divine spell phrases are for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women. At the morning hour, one should rise and recite them."

At that time, Śakra Devānām-indra addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, may the Tathāgata graciously grant protection and remembrance. I shall speak a dhāraṇī. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, at the morning hour, they should recite and remember it.

*Tadyathā: bhini bhārṇi danḍa-madha-jitinī guḍe gandha dṛḍe sāre mahānī bajrāṃda-runī jurā-mālinī cakra-bhāgī śā-bhāre śā-māre-pramālatī hāna-ma-hantānī śambhāre svāhā.*

At that time, Vaiśravaṇa the Heavenly King of Much Learning, Dhṛtarāṣṭra the Heavenly King Who Upholds the Realm, Virūḍhaka the Heavenly King of Increase, and Virūpākṣa the Heavenly King of Terrible Eyes joined their palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! We also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī divine spell. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: puṣpī-supuṣpī duma-pāde hāre ārya-pūrṇa-sidhi-śāntī-nirmukti muṃgale hiraṇi garbhe stutī satā-bhitī svāhā.*

At that time, Hārītī, the Mother of Ghost-Children, addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this divine-spell dharma-gate dhāraṇī, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: sirī-si sirī sirī-si sirī birī mahā-bi-birī simi-rī si-kurunī kurunī kurunī mahā-kurunī phu svāhā.*

At that time, the five great mountain yakṣa generals joined their palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! We now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī divine spell. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this divine-spell dharma-gate dhāraṇī, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: aṭṭa-pu bhaṭṭa-pu ṇaṭṭa-pu kuṭṭa-pu maṭṭa-pu haramūṇḍa hāte pu śrī pu paṅki pu mā-carī pu svāhā.*

At that time, Vajrapāṇi, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, out of compassion embracing and bringing them all peace, ease, and good fortune, shall establish a boundary to free them from weapons and eliminate all evil poisons. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: ha-vajre ha-vajre hihi hihi bhī bhī si-mahā-bhīsi pu svāhā.*

At that time, Maheśvara Devaputra joined his palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī divine spell. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: huru huru huru huru ruru ruru ruru ruru sarva-bhūta-pūrṇa-tiṣṭhādanāṃ kāramī svāhā.*

At that time, the Kumāra General, joining his palms toward the Buddha in reverence while standing, addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this divine-spell dhāraṇī dharma-gate, at the morning hour, they should cleanse their body and rise to recite it.

*Tadyathā: hirī hirī hirī rī rī rī rī rī rī sarva-bhūta-pūrṇa-pratiṣṭhādanāṃ kārumī svāhā.*

At that time, Candraprabha Devaputra joined his palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this dhāraṇī divine spell, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: aṅgī paṅgī jaṅgī hinī dhi dhi dhi dhi jī jī pu svāhā.*

At that time, the Noble Avalokiteśvara, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: hāte vihāte nihāte suhāte sarva-pūrṇa-dārthike pūrṇa-dā-mitre śudhi-mukti vimārī nimālī pūrṇa-bha-svāre pūrṇa-bha-skāre pūrṇa-bha-skāre pu svāhā.*

At that time, Maitreya Bodhisattva addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: jvalī jvalī mahā-jvalī jvalī jvalirīnī ukti mukti śame pūrṇa-śame mahā-śame śamī-samādhi mahā-samādhi samāpati mahā-samāpati jvalī mahā-jvalī jvalā-bhame pu svāhā.*

At that time, the Snow Mountain Great Yakṣa Generals joined their palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! We now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī divine spell. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: udambhāre jambhāre pūrṇa-jambhārī vibhāti māye samādhi mahā-samādhi samāpati mahā-samāpati nirī nirī nirā-ke śeke śinī kulī kulī mahā-kulī nirā-kulī siddhi-siddhā-manurate. Aṭṭa-bhaṭṭa-ṭṭa-kuṭṭa-samantī bhāra-kuce dhantī nirā-keśe kulī mahā-kulī nīrā-kantī caṃgurī huśe-muhuśe bhāra-caṃgurī pharā-kuce pharā-muñce pu svāhā.*

At that time, the Seven Mountain Great Yakṣa Generals joined their palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! We now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak a dhāraṇī. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this dhāraṇī spell, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: amārī vimārī nimārī muṃgale hiraṇya garbhe sarvārtha-sādhanī makā-śica.*

*Niyukti virāśe kaṭṭa-ke ju-gambhārī brahme brahma-svāre brahma-siddhi-siddhā-manurate pu svāhā.*

At that time, Vajra-Śaṅkarā, the Great Goddess, joined her palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit, compassion, and embrace of all good men and good women, shall speak dhāraṇī phrases, so that they may all be at peace and attain good fortune. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this divine-spell dharma-gate, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: vajre vajrā vajre mahā-vajre vajra-māti buddha-vajre buddha-adhiṣṭhita vajre udbhūtinī buddhī saṃbuddhi bāhu-buddhi viśiṣṭa-buddhi buddhi buddhi buddhi buddhi buddhi buddhi dhi-śaṅga-mīnī caṃga-me ārāme tirī-me tirī tirī tirī māgī mā-gandha gāminī de pu svāhā.*

At that time, Vajrasena, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, joined his palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, shall speak dhāraṇī phrases. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this divine-spell dharma-gate dhāraṇī, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: hihi vajra hihi vajra hehe vajra yā bhakiri kiri vajra svāhā.*

*Tadyathā: andade paṇḍade śivete paṇḍara-bhāsinī kiṃre kāra-ḍaki yule iṭa-buddhi tatrā-buddhi bhūje-bhūja-game bhūja-game bhūja-ga-bhadī bhāye bhāyāṃgī bhāya-ghara-bhatī bhinī bhinī śide pāte śimā-pāte ādittya-pāte deje deje pāte ākāśa pāte pu svāhā.*

At that time, the Vajra Messenger joined his palms in reverence and addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I now also, for the protection and benefit of all good men and good women, and for the elimination of all their obstacles, shall speak. If any person can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this dhāraṇī divine spell, at the morning hour, they should rise and recite it.

*Tadyathā: kuṭṭa kuṭṭa kucinī kuṭa kuṭa re svāhā. Nirī nirī nirā-keśe svāhā. Bhīti bhītā bhīta-keśe svāhā. Ruhite ruhite ruhita-keśe svāhā. Abhādate abhādate abhādata-keśe svāhā. Mandiṣṭa mandiṣṭa mandiṣṭa-keśe svāhā. Cakre cakre cakra-cakre vajra-cakre svāhā.*

At that time, the Bhagavān Śākyamuni Buddha extended his right arm of true golden color and praised Śakra, Brahmā, the Four Heavenly Kings, and the others, saying: "Excellent, excellent! You devas have compassionately protected all sentient beings. I shall, with my spiritual power, protect you and teach these dhāraṇī spells."

At that time, the Bhagavān further addressed Vajrapāṇi, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, saying: "If any good man or good woman can uphold this Dharma-Gate of Protecting Life, this dhāraṇī divine spell, reciting and upholding it at the morning hour, that person shall be protected by all buddhas and all bodhisattvas, and by all devas, nāgas, yakṣas, asuras, garuḍas, kiṃnaras, and mahoragas."

When the Bhagavān had finished teaching this sūtra, Vajrapāṇi, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, together with Śakra, Brahmā, the World-Protecting Heavenly Kings, humans, asuras, gandharvas, and all the great assembly, having heard what the Buddha taught, joyfully received it and put it into practice.
