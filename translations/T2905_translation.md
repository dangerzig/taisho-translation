# Names of the Fifteen Hundred Presently Existing Buddhas of the Ten Directions, Together with Miscellaneous Buddhas Sharing the Same Title

## *Xiànzài Shífāng Qiānwǔbǎi Fómíng Bìng Zá Fó Tónghào* (現在十方千五百佛名並雜佛同號)

Taishō 2905

Translator unknown.

*Note: This is a liturgical compilation text that gathers buddha-names from several canonical sources for use in repentance and devotional rituals. It draws from four named scriptures: (1) the Sūtra of Medicine King and Medicine Lord (Yàowáng Yàoshàng Jīng), providing fifty-three buddha-names; (2) the Sūtra of Definitive Vinaya (Juédìng Pínì Jīng), providing thirty-five buddha-names used in the famous thirty-five buddhas repentance liturgy; (3) the Sūtra of Amitābha's Praise of All Buddhas (Āmítuó Zàn Yīqiè Zhūfó Suǒchí Zhī Fǎ Jīng), providing thirty-eight buddha-names from six directions; and (4) the Sūtra of Praising the Merits of All Buddhas (Chēngyáng Zhūfó Gōngdé Jīng), providing one hundred and thirty-nine buddha-names from five directions. The text concludes with an extensive catalog of groups of buddhas sharing identical names, drawn from the Buddhāvataṃsaka and other sources, listing how many buddhas in each of the ten directions share a given title. Such compilations were widely used in Chinese Buddhist liturgical practice.*

---

[The text opens with instructions for prostration practice:]

... able to injure. If one can, even once, cast the five limbs to the ground in prostration, speaking aloud: "I now universally bow to all buddhas of the three times in the ten directions," repeating this up to a thousand times before rising, the merit obtained is as described above.

## The Fifty-Three Buddhas from the Sūtra of Medicine King and Medicine Lord

If any good men or good women, or any other sentient beings, hear the names of these fifty-three buddhas, those persons for a hundred thousand myriad koṭi asaṃkhyeya kalpas will not fall into evil destinies. If furthermore any person can recite the names of these fifty-three buddhas, in life after life they will always encounter the buddhas of the ten directions. If furthermore any person can with utmost sincerity reverently bow to the fifty-three buddhas, they will eliminate the four grave offenses, the five heinous acts, and slander of the Vaipulya teachings, all becoming completely purified. Because of the original vows of these buddhas, in thought after thought such sins as the above are immediately eliminated.

## The Thirty-Five Buddhas from the Sūtra of Definitive Vinaya

If any bodhisattva has committed the five heinous acts, or has violated a woman or a man, or has transgressed against a stūpa or the saṃgha, or committed other such offenses, that bodhisattva should, before these thirty-five buddhas, day and night in solitude, with utmost sincerity repent of the grave sins committed. The method of repentance is: take refuge in the Buddha, take refuge in the Dharma, take refuge in the Saṃgha.

Homage to the Tathāgata of Jewel-Lotus Well-Abiding Śāla-Tree King.

All such world-honored buddhas in all the worlds are constantly abiding. May these world-honored ones be mindful of me with compassion. Whether in this life or in former lives, from beginningless saṃsāra onward, whatever sins I have committed, caused others to commit, or rejoiced at seeing committed, whether against stūpas, the saṃgha, or the four-directional saṃgha properties, whether taken by myself, caused others to take, or rejoiced at seeing taken; whether the five heinous grave sins, committed by myself, caused others to commit, or rejoiced at seeing committed; the ten unwholesome courses of action, committed by myself, caused by others, or rejoiced at seeing committed; whatever karmic obstructions, whether concealed or unconcealed, that should lead to falling into hell, hungry ghost, animal, or other evil destinies, borderlands, low births, or barbarian peoples: all such karmic obstructions I now fully repent. May all the buddha world-honored ones bear witness to me and be mindful of me. Furthermore, before the buddha world-honored ones, I make this declaration: whatever acts of giving I may have practiced in this or other lives, whatever pure precepts I may have upheld, even giving a morsel of food to an animal, whatever roots of goodness from pure conduct, whatever roots of goodness from bringing beings to maturity, whatever roots of goodness from cultivating bodhi, whatever roots of goodness of unsurpassed wisdom: all of these I gather together, calculate and reckon, and wholly dedicate to supreme, perfect awakening. Just as the buddhas of past, future, and present have made such dedications, I too dedicate them thus.

## The Thirty-Eight Buddhas of Six Directions from the Sūtra of Amitābha's Praise

Furthermore, Śāriputra: In the east there are the buddhas Akṣobhya Tathāgata, Sumeru-Banner Tathāgata, Great Sumeru Tathāgata, Sumeru Radiance Tathāgata, and Compassionate Joy Tathāgata.

In the south: Sun-Moon-Lamp Brightness Tathāgata, Radiance-and-Renown Tathāgata, Great Flame Lineage Tathāgata, Sumeru-Lamp Tathāgata, and Infinite Vigor Tathāgata.

In the west: Infinite Life Tathāgata, Infinite Banner Tathāgata, Infinite Pennant Tathāgata, Great Radiance Tathāgata, Great Radiance Universal Pervasion Tathāgata, Jewel-Banner Tathāgata, and Pure Radiance Tathāgata.

In the north: Great Flame Lineage Tathāgata, Joyful Sound Tathāgata, Invincible Tathāgata, Arisen from the Sun Tathāgata, and Jewel-Net Radiance Tathāgata.

Below: Lion Tathāgata, Far-Famed Tathāgata, Famous Radiance Tathāgata, Dharma-Name Tathāgata, Dharma-Banner Tathāgata, and Dharma-Upholding Tathāgata.

Above: Brahmā-Sound Tathāgata, Supreme Fragrance Tathāgata, Star King Tathāgata, Fragrant Radiance Tathāgata, Great Flame Lineage Tathāgata, Marvelous Jewel-Colored Flower-Body Tathāgata, Wondrous Fragrance Tree King Tathāgata, Jewel-Lotus Tathāgata, Teaching All Matters Tathāgata, and Sumeru-Mind Tathāgata.

If any good men or good women hear the names of these buddhas and the names of this sūtra, all such beings shall be guarded and upheld by all these buddhas, and shall surely attain non-retrogression, and shall accomplish the unsurpassed, true Way. Therefore, Śāriputra, with single-minded faith and joy, one should widely proclaim and sincerely practice this. If any person holds my name and embraces the names of all the above buddhas, the merit of holding these names, with utmost aspiration to be born in Amitābha Buddha's land: whether they vowed in the past or vow now, wishing to be born in Amitābha Buddha's land, all such beings shall surely abide in non-retrogression, and at the end of their lives shall all be born in Amitābha Buddha's land.

## The One Hundred and Thirty-Nine Buddhas of Five Directions from the Sūtra of Praising the Merits of All Buddhas

This section notes the sūtra's physical dimensions: two fascicles totaling fifty-five and a half sheets, with twenty-five lines per sheet and seventeen characters per line.

**Eastern Direction (53 buddhas):** Jewel-Ocean Tathāgata, Jewel-Blossom Tathāgata, Jewel-Accomplishment Tathāgata, Jewel-Radiance Tathāgata, Jewel-Banner Tathāgata, Jewel-Radiance Tathāgata, Akṣobhya Tathāgata, Great Radiance Tathāgata, Infinite Sound Tathāgata, [and many more, totaling 53].

**Southern Direction (38 buddhas):** Sun-Moon-Lamp Brightness Tathāgata, Sumeru Tathāgata, Great Sumeru Tathāgata, Transcending Sumeru Tathāgata, [and more, totaling 38].

**Western Direction (15 buddhas):** Amitābha Tathāgata, Most Excellent Tathāgata, Gathered Sound Tathāgata, [and more, totaling 15].

**Northern Direction (6 buddhas):** Virtue Richly Adorned King Tathāgata, Vajra Firm Subduing and Dispersing Tathāgata, [and more, totaling 6].

**Upper Direction (27 buddhas):** Golden Jewel Radiance Tathāgata, Infinite Honored Abundance Tathāgata, [and more, totaling 27].

**Lower Direction (7 buddhas):** Cutting Doubt, Uprooting Desire, Removing Darkness Buddha, [and more, totaling 7].

**Intermediate Directions:** Northeast (4), Southeast (3), Southwest (4), Northwest (3).
