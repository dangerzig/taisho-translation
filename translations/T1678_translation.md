# 佛三身讚
## Praise of the Buddha's Three Bodies

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1678

Translated from the Chinese. Composed by sages of the Western Lands (西土賢聖撰). Song dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢)

---

**Dharmakāya**

> I now bow my head to the Dharmakāya Buddha,
> Beyond analogy, inconceivable, all-pervading wisdom,
> Filling the Dharmadhātu without obstruction,
> Serene and tranquil, without equal or peer,
> Neither existent nor non-existent, its nature is truly real,
> Neither many nor few, transcending all enumeration,
> Equal and formless, like empty space,
> Benefiting oneself and others is likewise so.

**Sambhogakāya**

> I now bow my head to the Sambhogakāya Buddha,
> Serenely abiding, the Great Muni,
> Compassionately transforming and guiding the assembly of bodhisattvas,
> Presiding over the assembly like the sun, universally illuminating,
> Accumulating all merit over three asaṃkhyeya kalpas,
> Only then able to perfect the tranquil path,
> With a great voice expounding the wondrous Dharma,
> Universally enabling all to attain the fruit of equality.

**Nirmāṇakāya**

> I now bow my head to the Nirmāṇakāya Buddha,
> Who attained perfect enlightenment beneath the Bodhi tree,
> Sometimes manifesting transformations, sometimes abiding in stillness,
> Or going forth to transform beings throughout the ten directions,
> Or turning the Wheel of the Dharma at Mṛgadāva,
> Or manifesting great radiance like a blazing mass of fire,
> Able to eliminate all the suffering of the three evil destinies,
> Incomparable throughout the three realms, the Great Muni.

**Dedication of Merit**

> Such is the Buddha's body endowed with undefiled wisdom;
> I constantly hold faith and understanding, purifying the three karmas,
> With immeasurable wisdom and great meritorious conduct,
> Single-mindedly extending compassion to all sentient beings.
> By these verses offered in praise of the Three-Body Buddha,
> May the undefiled seeds of merit thus obtained
> Enable me to swiftly realize the Buddha's bodhi,
> And guide all sentient beings back to the right path.
