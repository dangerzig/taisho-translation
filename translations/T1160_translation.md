# 日光菩薩月光菩薩陀羅尼
## Dhāraṇī of Bodhisattva Sūryaprabha and Bodhisattva Candraprabha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1160

Translated from the Chinese. Translator unknown (失譯)

---

Bodhisattva Sūryaprabha, for the sake of those who uphold the Dhāraṇī of the Great Compassionate Heart, proclaims a great dhāraṇī-spell to protect them:

> *Namo buddha-guṇa-me namo dharma-mahā-te namo saṃgha-tāyane tri-bhu-visat-stāṃ namo svāhā*

"Reciting this spell extinguishes all sins, and also repels demons and dispels heavenly calamities. If one recites it once and bows once to the Buddha, and in this manner recites the spell and pays obeisance to the Buddha three times each day, then in future lives, in every body one receives and every place one dwells, one shall obtain the karmic reward of a complete and perfectly upright appearance in every feature."

Bodhisattva Candraprabha likewise proclaims a great dhāraṇī-spell for those who recite and uphold the Dhāraṇī of the Great Compassionate Heart, and speaks a dhāraṇī-spell for all practitioners in order to protect them. The spell is spoken thus:

> *Śīte tuṣita ajñā-mite udvata śīṃkita parādhita-yami jñāta udvata kulādhita kīmata svāhā*

"Recite this spell five times, take threads of five colors to fashion a spell-cord, and bind it at the place of pain. This spell was proclaimed by all the Buddhas of the past, as numerous as the sands of forty Ganges Rivers; I now likewise proclaim it: in order to protect all practitioners, to remove all obstructions and difficulties, to eliminate all evil diseases and suffering, to accomplish all wholesome dharmas, and to depart far from all fears. If anyone should slander this spell, they thereby slander all those Buddhas."

---

*Collated once on the evening of the twenty-second day of the ninth month, Jōkyō 3 [1686], year bingyin.*

Jōgon
