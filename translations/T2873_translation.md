# Sūtra of the Bhikṣu Śūra

## *Shǒuluó Bǐqiū Jīng* (首羅比丘經)

Taishō 2873

Translator unknown.

*Note: This is an apocryphal Chinese Buddhist text of a millenarian and apocalyptic character, placed in the "suspected apocrypha" section of the Taishō canon. The text takes the form of a dialogue between the bhikṣu Śūra (Shǒuluó) and a "Great Immortal" (dàxiān), and later between Śūra and a figure called "Moonlight Child" (Yuèguāng Tóngzǐ), concerning the coming age of calamities and the eventual appearance of Moonlight as a messianic savior-king. The text describes three great disasters (water, plague, and demonic invasion), the appearance of thirty-six great demons riding dragon-horses, seven days of darkness with flesh-eating yakṣas, and the establishment of a golden city. It names specific "worthies" and "sages" with Chinese surnames who are said to be secretly present in the world, identifies specific Chinese geographic locations (Penglai Mountain, Hailing Mountain, etc.), and includes a dhāraṇī for protection. The text explicitly threatens severe karmic punishment for anyone who conceals or fails to circulate it. Its Chinese geographic and political references, use of Chinese personal names, and millenarian tone clearly mark it as a Chinese composition. Multiple Dunhuang manuscripts survive.*

---

[The text opens mid-dialogue, with Śūra questioning the Great Immortal:]

... "Is there yet another method to escape this?" The Great Immortal replied to Śūra: "Reverently believe in the Three [Treasures], observe the fast, perform prostrations, with devoted and unremitting reverence, and you shall be saved from the disaster of great flood."

Śūra again asked the Great Immortal about the disaster of illness. The Great Immortal said: "Bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas [and upāsikās], from now on, uphold the precepts and observe the fast, keeping all pure and clean. Act with knowledge, speak with propriety, receive the precepts as I command. Do not act according to your ordinary inclinations, and you shall be able to cross over."

Śūra asked: "What further method is there to escape the disaster of demons and evil spirits?" The Great Immortal replied: "Demons and evil spirits are of many kinds. Those who receive my command should be careful not to believe in them."

### The Coming of Moonlight and the Golden City

"When Moonlight (*Yuèguāng*, 月光) appears in the world, only those who are good will see him. Those of the five heinous sins and great evil will never see him."

Śūra asked about the cities and their quarters. The Great Immortal answered: "The city is over seven hundred leagues in breadth, a thousand feet high, with a base of a thousand feet and battlements of over five hundred feet. It has seventy-two gates and is made of purple-polished gold. Within it is the Tuṣita City, also a thousand feet high with a thousand-foot base and five-hundred-foot battlements, also of purple-polished gold. Within it are eight cities, each over thirty leagues wide, also of purple-polished gold, each with a thousand lanes, lane aligned with lane, gate facing gate, all looking out upon the Dharma-King."

### The Eight Worthies and the Sixteen Sages

The Great Immortal said: "I shall speak their names." He then named: Shí Xián, Dé Yán, Xián Míng, Sūn Xián, Qí Huā Xián, Dé Wú Xián, Shǐ Zhèng Xián, Dāng Guān Xián, and Bǎo Zhào Xián. "These are the names of the eight worthies. You should go and seek them out. They are your true guides, able to carry you across birth-and-death."

"Besides these eight, there are more worthies: Qín Chāoshì, Pān Dàochéng, Lú Huìyuàn, Bǎn Guóxīng, Fú Nányáng, Liú Dàoguì, Wáng Yánshòu, Zhào Xiǎnzōng, Zhāng Dàobǎn, Gù Shì'ān, and Lǐ Luóchà. All such worthies wander throughout the world. With your fleshly eyes you cannot distinguish them."

"Receiving my command, you should practice equanimity toward all. Why? Because the great worthies are difficult to distinguish. They may appear mad, foolish, dull, poor, lowly, or humble. Some may drink wine and eat meat, or eat at night and break the fast. Manifesting in such ways, who can recognize them? They appear to possess the three poisons and the marks of [worldly] views, conforming to worldly ways, difficult to know. Only through equanimity can one encounter the worthies and sages."

### The Three Great Disasters

Moonlight Child said: "In coming years there will surely be a flood disaster. The water will rise over forty leagues above flat ground. When the water comes, it will surge from the northwest and flow to the southeast, with great waves roaring louder than thunder and lightning, beyond any comparison."

"Only those who uphold the precepts with purity and diligently seek to cross over the world [shall be saved]. The Moonlight Child will command the Great Dragon King to lead people broadly to the floating mountains."

"Furthermore, the three disasters of plague will spread. Of ten, nine will perish, with all manner of strange illnesses bringing untimely death. The king should believe in this. Command everyone in the land, from one year old and above, who can walk, speak, and understand, to receive the three refuges and five precepts."

"Whether old or young, all should be encouraged to receive the three refuges and five precepts and practice the wholesome Dharma. Such persons will all cross over the evil age."

### The Dhāraṇī

"If one reads this dhāraṇī, lions, tigers, wolves, evil spirits, and all demons will naturally depart."

*Yōuqiūní yōuqiūní dànpó dànpó yùlí yùlí wūhū wūhū nísàhū sàhū dànchā dànchā chā āyóuchí chíhūní yàotā yàotā suǒyóu fùnúní fùnúní.*

"When reading this dhāraṇī, wash your hands clean, rinse your mouth, and read it. It will cause people to be peaceful and at ease day and night, and they will immediately see the Bodhisattva. Reading this dhāraṇī one hundred times, one will see the Bodhisattva emitting great radiance, manifesting before the person."

### Exhortation to Circulate the Sūtra

Śūra told the four assemblies: "The great disaster is approaching! Do not act according to your ordinary inclinations. If you have a root teacher, parents, sovereign, patrons, friends, acquaintances, or relatives, give this sūtra to all of them. Whether near or far, all who obtain this sūtra should diligently circulate and distribute it so that everyone may hear it. Anyone who conceals and hides this sūtra will in future lives surely fall into the evil paths."

"Suppose a person were to give away the elephants, horses, seven treasures, people, grain, silk, and wealth of the three-thousand great-thousand world-system as an act of generosity. It would not equal the merit of a person who circulates this sūtra. The merit is a hundredfold greater."

"If one obtains this sūtra, diligently distribute it throughout cities, towns, and villages so that all men and women, great and small, may hear it. Anyone who conceals this sūtra will in the present life be unfortunate and in future lives will contract illness."

"The wise will believe it; the foolish will discard it."

Śūra said: "My sūtra shall in the future be fulfilled. Do not disbelieve it. All sentient beings should receive and practice it with joy. This sūtra is like the ocean, possessing great moisture and benefit."

At that time, the king of the Land of the Noble, together with his great ministers, chief officers, and all officials, numbering more than three thousand, each heard that at the Great Tranquility Monastery above there were five hundred immortals, and were overjoyed. Each prepared their carriages and went to the Great Tranquility Monastery to visit the immortals. They bowed and asked: "Great Immortals, from whence have you come, and where do you intend to go? Please tell us your purpose."

The Great Immortal said: "I heard that the Moonlight Child has appeared in the world, and so I have come here wishing to go to that place."

When the king and great ministers heard these words, they were overjoyed and gazed longingly to the west without cease. "The true sages of the Western Land, what merit did they cultivate to be able to enter into goodness? What wholesome karma did they perform to see the Moonlight One appear in the world? And our people are so far away that we cannot see him."

The Great Immortal replied: "When the Moonlight One appears, all people everywhere shall see him. Why does the king give rise to lamentation?"

When the king heard that all would universally see him, he and the great ministers and all the people were delighted. Each prepared their carriages, saying: "Let us go now."

The king asked the Great Immortal: "Where is the Moonlight Child at present?"

The Great Immortal replied: "Excellent, excellent, great king! Listen well to what I say. The Moonlight Bright King is now with three thousand followers at Penglai Mountain, at the foot of Hǎilíng Mountain, at the Cave of Mǐnzǐ. He abides there in contemplation, awaiting the appointed time to manifest."

The king of the Land of the Noble, along with his ministers, officials, and people, all addressed the Great Immortal: "May we follow the Great Immortal to the place of the Moonlight One to see him?"

The Great Immortal said: "The Moonlight Bright King is like the great ocean, like the great earth. He would never give rise to doubt or create obstacles."

The king and his ministers, together with the people, then followed the Great Immortal. There were fifty-seven thousand people. After traveling more than seven thousand li from the Land of the Noble, they arrived at Penglai Mountain, at the foot of Hǎilíng Mountain, at the Cave of Mǐnzǐ. They saw the Moonlight Child. Three thousand followers and all the sages were assembled there.

The Moonlight Child asked: "All you Great Immortals and the great king, from where have you come, and where do you intend to go? The terrain here is perilous and difficult, with no human habitation. How were you able to come here?"

The king said: "We heard that the World-Honored One is about to appear in the world, and so we have come to pay our respects."

The Moonlight Child said: "You should simply cultivate goodness and practice with diligent effort. Do not act according to your ordinary inclinations. I have already completed my task. Why should I manifest again?"

The great king said: "I heard that the Holy Sovereign would appear in the world, but I do not know the proper procedures. Please explain them so that I may apply my mind to cultivating goodness."

The Moonlight Child said: "If you do not know, I shall explain it for you." The Moonlight One said: "Listen well, and reflect upon it well. Take it deeply to heart and do not act according to your ordinary inclinations. When I have seen and explained it, in the coming year there will surely be a flood disaster. The waters will rise more than forty li above flat ground. When the great waters come, they will surge from the northwest and flow toward the southeast. The great waters will roar and churn with sounds of thunder and lightning beyond description. The churning waves will cry out and roar. At that time, people will be terrified and many will die of fright. Only those who maintain the precepts with purity and diligently strive to transcend the world will be saved. The Moonlight Child will command the Great Dragon King to lead people in great numbers to a floating mountain. If someone composes a single fascicle of the Avalokiteśvara Sūtra; if someone abides in single-minded meditation; if someone, in this evil age, diligently urges and transforms others; if someone circulates this sūtra without concealing its words and phrases and diligently encourages others: all such persons will transcend the world and not be swept away by the flood disaster. Excepted are those who are not sincere or whose lifespan has reached its end."

The Moonlight One further told the great king: "In the coming three disasters, epidemic illness will spread. Nine out of ten will perish, and all manner of strange diseases will claim their lives. The king should believe this. Command everyone within the kingdom, from one year of age up to those who can walk and speak, to receive the three refuges and the five precepts. Whether old or young, all should be urged to receive the three refuges and five precepts, and to practice the wholesome Dharma. Such persons will all transcend the world, excepting those who are not wholesome, not sincere, or whose lifespan has reached its end."

"I now teach you these words in secret so that you may know. In the evil age, when epidemics spread and the good Dharma later leads to untimely death, do not give rise to regret. I tell you all: as the world approaches its end, evil will gradually arise. The coming year will be hard to survive. Practice goodness diligently and do not act according to your ordinary inclinations. When I appear in the world, the disasters too will not be wholesome. Simply exert yourselves, diligently practice the wholesome Dharma, and do not act according to your ordinary inclinations. Demons and evil omens, heroic warriors arising: they will naturally be worn down and extinguished, and in the end they will not see me appear in the world."

"You all, excellent! Do not act according to your ordinary inclinations. Those who receive my teaching should recite the Avalokiteśvara one thousand times to protect their bodies, transcend the world, and attain the fruit of goodness. Furthermore, each person should receive the three refuges and five precepts. Now put them into practice and do not act according to your ordinary inclinations. Diligently circulate this sūtra. Whether old or young, cause all to hear it, and they shall transcend the evil age. If someone circulates this sūtra in all places so that people hear and know it, they shall transcend and preserve their lives. The merit of those who diligently circulate this sūtra: even if I were to calculate that person's reward through a thousand kalpas, I could never exhaust it. If someone gives rise to doubt and disbelief: even if I were to calculate that person's offenses through a thousand kalpas, I could never exhaust them. Moreover, if one conceals the written words and phrases, even a single sheet, preventing people from hearing them, one covers the eye of wisdom, and in every lifetime one will be perpetually blind and unable to see. Even upon attaining arhatship, one will never be free from blindness, because of covering the eye of wisdom and obstructing the Dharma, thus receiving such retribution."

"Those who receive my teaching: in cities and villages, kings and great ministers, and all the people should hear it. Those who have faith and revere this sūtra, whether near or far, should go and circulate it so that people may hear it. In a thousand cities and a hundred countries, cause all to hear it."

"When I appear in the world, north of the Yellow River and south of the Ruò River, within that region I shall reign in the land of China."

The great king addressed the Bright King: "From what source does this sūtra come?"

The Moonlight One told the great king: "The Great Sage of Hǎilíng and more than three thousand others composed it in council."

The Moonlight One told the great king: "You and the great assembly should each disperse and go forth to transform all under heaven; there is no need to return."

At that time, the great king received the teaching, practiced it joyfully, and departed. The Moonlight Child said: "What I have said above, if you do not believe it, look at the stone image of Kāśyapa. That is the record of my appearance in the world."

"Excellent! When the cord is severed and the threads are joined, the wholesome sentient beings who follow should not be frightened. When I appear, all shall attain the unconditioned."

The king and the great assembly joyfully received and practiced the teaching, continuing to transform others without slacking.

"I tell you all: this year is upon us. You should exert yourselves with diligence and not act according to your ordinary inclinations. Each should make vows to transcend the evil age."

The Great Immortals, the king, and all the ministers and people joyfully received the teaching, bowed, and departed.

The five hundred immortals were at Great Tranquility Mountain. They beheld the Moonlight Child and the single fascicle of this sūtra. In the Golden Dragon City, they saw a bodhisattva beneath the Dragon-Flower Tree. They saw a practitioner bringing down this sūtra, creating it for one sentient being and thereby generating the mind of all sentient beings.

The king said: "I have analyzed and explained this for you. The Dharma King awaits the Holy Sovereign's descent. For all young men and women, he dispatches one hundred and twenty worthy sovereigns. In the years of *shēn* and *yǒu*, he will teach the Dharma for sentient beings and bring my young men and women to the attainment of the Way."

"When reading this sūtra, reflect well and grasp these words. Men should take 'immeasurable'; women should take 'Ganges-sands.' Men need not take wives."

"The Moonlight Child is about to appear as a sage and manifest to bring all sentient beings to the Way. If one reads the words of this sūtra, one can escape this calamity."

"The Moonlight Bodhisattva is about to descend to teach that maintaining the precepts enables one to see the Bright Sovereign."

If one wishes to recite this dhāraṇī, lions, tigers, wolves, and all evil spells of the hundred spirits will naturally depart. All sentient beings who die untimely deaths are many. For all sentient beings, revere the Buddha's correct Dharma.

[Dhāraṇī]

When reciting this dhāraṇī, wash your hands cleanly and rinse your mouth. Recite this dhāraṇī, and it will bring safety and peace by day and by night, and you will see the bodhisattva. Recite this dhāraṇī one hundred times, and you will see the bodhisattva emitting great radiance and appearing before you, with a staff on one side, the color of gold, a golden flower on the head as large as a cartwheel, and hands one and a half feet long. When you first see this, do not be frightened. Take refuge in the Buddha, take refuge in the Dharma, take refuge in the Sangha, and in the dharma-realm of the ten directions. Read this sūtra three times, and you shall attain liberation.

The World-Honored One, with his deep gaze, sees that the offenses of sentient beings are not few. He has analyzed and explained so that they may escape and be freed from calamity. When the Bright Sovereign appears, hold this sūtra before him. The Bright Sovereign will surely see you; be careful not to be frightened. Maintain correct thoughts, correct intentions, and correct bodily conduct to see my body. Some may encounter difficulties. Such persons may appear as lions, tigers, or wolves, holding golden staffs and striking people without limit. Follow the words of this sūtra and practice the bodhisattva path, and you can see the bodhisattva teach the Dharma and manifest, wearing celestial garments.

Be careful not to speak, for this person will then attain the Way. Seeing flowers and fragrance appearing of themselves, do not let the mind be moved. Speak to this person, and they will surely depart from the evil age. The foremost application of intention for one hundred days: during this time, do not be foolish or greedy. In the years of *shēn* and *yǒu*, a father will not recognize his son, a mother will not recognize her daughter. Remember the words of this sūtra, and you can change your mind. The foremost change of mind: first, no sexual desire; first, no evil glances.

The Buddha teaches the Dharma for sentient beings so that they may regain their original mind. The Buddha is about to appear in the world; diligently repent with sincerity. Seeing the Moonlight Bodhisattva, a single worthy person can save ten people. This sūtra must not be slandered. When dark rulers arise and demons flourish, those who diligently strive with effort shall escape this calamity.

All the practitioners of the Way, for all sentient beings, teach the Dharma accordingly. If you understand my words, you shall see the Dharma King. Even if practitioners die without abandoning the practice of the Way, the dead are many, and practitioners' offenses are not few. Because of engaging in trade for a living, the nation is not at peace. When practitioners die, there will be disasters from brigands, and many children will die. The intention of practitioner-monks is to esteem the bhikṣuṇī sangha. Of a thousand, only ten are selected. The foremost application of practice: the sage is about to begin, heroes are about to arise, the season is about to arrive. Yellow robes will be a zhang and two feet long. Milled rice will grow everywhere. Diligently create merit, and you shall see these things. Reflect on the words of this sūtra, and you shall see my body.

On the fourteenth day of the seventh month, there will be a strange sign. On the fifteenth day, you will see the Buddha and the earth will shake; do not be frightened. When you see the practitioner, all will gradually be at peace.

Those who disparage the words of this sūtra will be stricken with great illness. You will also see an old bird with legs a zhang long. You will also see a worthy one who has cultivated the Way for more than seven hundred years in the Purple Crag Mountain. You will also see a practitioner wearing celestial garments. The worthy one, seeing him, will then make offerings for seven days. The practitioner's teacher and followers have been together for more than three hundred years. The practitioner's spoken words: "Reciting the sūtra, one can escape calamity."

"May the Buddha, beneath the Pāṭali Tree, turn and recite for all sentient beings." Looking out, he sees that sentient beings are dying in great numbers.

The Moonlight Child again addressed the World-Honored One: "Sentient beings can be transformed."

The Buddha told the Moonlight Child: "Go ahead according to your wish, and then desist. Those who have committed the five heinous offenses are very many; how can they be saved?"

The Buddha again told the Moonlight Child. The Buddha again told the Four Heavenly Dragon Kings: "Sentient beings are pitiable. They arise according to causes and conditions."

He further dispatched one hundred and twenty worthy sovereigns to remove the good and evil of sentient beings, distinguishing them. The Buddha-Radiance Bodhisattva, the Universal-Radiance Bodhisattva, and the Jeweled Canopy Samantabhadra's merit is not small. Throughout all buddha-lands, Bhaiṣajyarāja Bodhisattva and Steadfast Bodhisattva are present in the human world. Of a thousand villages, only one village remains; for ten thousand li, there is a single long stretch.

The Buddha is about to appear; sentient beings, strive with diligence, and you shall see the Buddha. Those who slander will have their entire household destroyed, and they will assuredly fall into the hells, never to see the Buddha. The Buddha is about to appear in the world; strive diligently.

"Vimalakīrti and his companions, Dīpaṃkara, are among the people. Vimalakīrti takes a wife and moves among women so that people do not recognize him. He acts as a worldly ruler, descending fragrance to transform people. Vimalakīrti's great extent stretches forty-five li in each direction. Vimalakīrti has three world-children; Vimalakīrti saves people without limit. He pretends to engage in desire and licentiousness in the human world, yet has no licentious conduct. Sentient beings can dare to behold this practice. When Vimalakīrti's season arrives, immeasurable sentient beings will repent their regrets. Vimalakīrti's saving of people assuredly leads to the attainment of buddhahood. Vimalakīrti appears greedy and given to theft and false speech."

All sentient beings, strive with diligent effort, and you shall see Vimalakīrti and all the practitioners of the Way. A certain bird by the seaside brought down this sūtra. When the monk Qìng and the traveling band of seven saw this bird, they burned incense, were overjoyed, and did not eat for seven days.

If all sentient beings hear the sound of my words, strive with diligent effort, and be careful not to waver. The evil will die out and great happiness will come; ancestral wealth will not be lost.
