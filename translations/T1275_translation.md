# 聖歡喜天式法
## Ritual Method of the Holy Joyous Deva

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1275

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, composed by Prajñā-cakra (般若惹羯羅).

---

If one wishes to open the spiritual efficacy of the Holy Deva's method, first construct the heaven-disc and earth-disc. The heaven-disc is three *cun* or four *cun* long; the earth-disc is seven *cun* long and square. Use milk-sap wood or fragrant wood, and complete them within a single day. Both heaven-disc and earth-disc should be made of white wood. When making and painting them, they must not be seen by lowly persons. One must practice with utmost diligence and ritual purity, selecting an auspicious day according to the standard method.

On one end, the heaven-disc is made round. Contemplating the eastern direction, place the syllable *oṃ* upon a petal. It transforms into Sun-Disc Joyous Deva, with a sun-disc above the head, in a worldly form with an angry countenance, reddish in color. The right hand holds a vaiḍūrya jewel pressed to the chest; the left hand holds a single-pronged vajra gathered at the left hip. He is the lord of the Crusher division, named Great General Without-Sorrow, surrounded by seven asaṃkhyeya retinues, seated upon a blue stone.

On the southern petal, contemplate the syllable *ja*. It transforms into Love-King Joyous Deva, appearing like a dharma-master, with the trunk below the chest like the eyes' direction. The right hand holds a round cake placed upon the crossed-leg lap; the left hand holds a radish root raised beside the left shoulder. He is the lord of the Jackal division, named Great General Elephant-Head, surrounded by eighteen koṭi retinues, seated upon a red stone.

On the western petal, contemplate the syllable *hrīḥ*. It transforms into Moon-Love Joyous Deva, whose form is a beautiful woman with a flower garland on the head. The right hand holds a jeweled mirror; the left hand supports the cheek. A jeweled casket serves as a footrest. She is of surpassing beauty and wondrous color. She is the lord of the One-Tusk division, named Great General Adorned-Topknot, surrounded by 140,000 koṭi retinues, seated upon tiger skin.

On the northern petal, contemplate the syllable *maṃ*. It transforms into议特 (议特, Gītaka) Joyous Deva, whose form is blue like Kauśika Deva. Both left and right hands each form the sword-mudrā, gathered at each side of the hip. He is the lord of the Nāga-Elephant division, named Great General Crown-Walker, surrounded by one koṭi-nayuta retinues, seated upon a black stone.

Next, the earth-disc assembly. In the east, install Indra Deva; in the southeast, Agni Deva; in the south, Yama Deva; in the southwest, Rākṣasa Deva; in the west, Varuṇa Deva; in the northwest, Vāyu Deva; in the north, Vaiśravaṇa Deva; in the northeast, Maheśvara Deva. Install the twenty-eight lunar mansions and the thirty-six animal spirits, established in this sequence. The four devas on the heaven-disc are all one body, not different bodies. It is also acceptable to install the Dhvaja Deva in the center.

Next, the invitation mudrā: both little fingers and ring fingers hooked inward toward each other. Both middle fingers erect and crossed. Both index fingers each erect alongside the middle fingers. Both thumbs also erect alongside the index fingers; the thumbs move back and forth. The mantra:

*Oṃ bhāgara-junadai-bhava-tāya.*

Next, the universal mudrā with accompanying mantra:

*Oṃ bhāgara-saṃman retinues from the Ganges-sands, guard self and others. Pray, pray. Certainly, certainly. Urgently, urgently, as commanded!*

Next, the invitation mudrā for the earth-disc assembly. Form the Indra-bestowing mudrā: both little fingers and ring fingers crossed within the palm. Both middle fingers erect with tips pinching. Both index fingers each placed below the half-joint on the back of the middle fingers. The thumbs move back and forth. The mantra:

*Oṃ śāṭuru-paramā-tha-nī ye svāhā.*

Next, the invitation mudrā and mantra for the twenty-eight mansions and thirty-six animal spirits:

*Oṃ ciri-aṃ-giru-nī ye svāhā.*

Next, the mudrā and mantra for inviting all deva classes: vajra joined palms, standing. Both thumbs move back and forth. The mantra:

*Wealth, compliance, love-spirits joined. Divisions and families seated in assembly. Led in with retinue-clans, one hundred million, two hundred million, all classes, each chief, come! Enter, enter, enter! Svāhā.*

Next, the offering mudrā and seal are to be found within the various categories.

### Divination Procedures with Talisman Combinations

**Secret Capturing Talisman**

If one desires official rank and emolument: combine Sun-Disc Deva with Indra Deva.

If one wishes to cause another person to have a fever: combine Sun-Disc Deva with Agni Deva-King.

If one desires to obtain merit and virtue: combine Moon-Love Deva with Vaiśravaṇa Deva and pray.

If one desires to gain another's affection: combine Moon-Love Deva with a messenger of Vaiśravaṇa Deva, and call upon the twenty-eight mansions.

If one desires to win in military battle: combine Sun-Disc Deva with Maheśvara Deva, and call upon ten thousand retinues.

If one desires to win people's hearts and have them follow one's will: combine Gītaka Deva with Yama Deva.

If one desires to be loved and respected by all: combine Love-King Deva with Vaiśravaṇa Deva.

If one desires to obtain another's wealth and treasures: combine Moon-Love Deva with Maheśvara Deva.

If one desires to cause love between husband and wife: combine Moon-Love Deva with Indra Deva.

If one desires to cause husband and wife to separate: combine Gītaka Deva with Varuṇa Deva-King.

If one desires the sudden death of an enemy or hostile person: combine Love-King Deva with Rākṣasa Deva.

If a servant has run away and one wishes them to return: combine Love-King Deva with Maheśvara Deva, and call the servant's name.

If a pregnant woman wishes for a safe and speedy delivery: combine Moon-Love Deva with Yama Deva; this works well.

If one has been cursed and wishes to reverse it: combine Sun-Disc Deva with Rākṣasa Deva and pray.

If one wishes to cause another to have a wind-disease: combine Gītaka Deva with Vāyu Deva and pray.

If one wishes to stop skin sores, abscesses, jaundice, or any other affliction: combine Love-King Deva with Yama Deva and pray.

If one wishes to stop excessive bleeding or bloody dysentery: combine Sun-Disc Deva with Indra Deva and pray.

If one wishes to stop rain or flooding: combine Love-King Deva with Indra Deva.

If one wishes to stop drought and bring rain: combine Gītaka Deva with Varuṇa Deva-King.

If one wishes to stop stomach or head ailments: combine Sun-Disc Deva with Yama Deva.

If one wishes to prevail in debate and argument: combine Sun-Disc Deva with Maheśvara, and call upon the twenty-eight mansions.

If one wishes to succeed in public or private affairs: combine Love-King Deva with Indra Deva.

If one wishes for a good grain harvest: combine Gītaka Deva with Indra Deva.

If one wishes for peace within the household: combine Love-King Deva with Vaiśravaṇa Deva.

If one wishes to stop quarrels and slander: combine Love-King Deva with Maheśvara Deva.

If one wishes to stop hauntings and evil dreams: combine Sun-Disc Deva with Yama Deva.

If one wishes a messenger sent far away to return quickly: combine Gītaka Deva with Vāyu Deva; they will return at once.

In this manner, matching each combination and praying accordingly, whatever matter one contemplates will certainly be accomplished. At the time of the practice, recite the root mantra and mudrā.

Next, form the three-division body-protection mudrā and mantra according to the standard mudrās.

The comprehensive capturing-hook mudrā: both hands in internal bond, placed atop the crown, then scattered. The mantra:

*Oṃ sara sara heaven-earth comprehensive binding! Binding! Hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ.*

Next, the counter-mudrā: the Acala Aga mudrā. The mantra:

*Hūṃ hūṃ kini kini hūṃ phaṭ svāhā.*

Next, the ten-directional boundary mudrā: the earth-boundary, directional wall, and space-boundary mudrās and mantras are the same as before.

Next, use thread to make the pairing. The mantra:

*Oṃ kye ye ye hūṃ.*

This method was transmitted at Bodhi-ji temple in the country of Heaven (India). The ritual implements needed are not many, and people of the time all called it the "verified method."

### Sequential Procedure for the Holy Deva (one fascicle)

First, purify the three karmas with mudrā and mantra. Next, body-protection mantra and mudrā. Next, face the altar, receive it three times, and swallow three times. Next, the celestial drum, twenty-one times. Next, bow to the presiding deity three times. Next, take one's seat. Next, paste-incense to purify the hands. Next, empower the sprinkling-water and scatter it over the altar, offerings, and entire room to make all pure. Next, state the matter and reasons. Next, the spirit-portion prayer. Next, the Mahācakra Vajra mantra and mudrā. Next, the wheel-altar mantra and mudrā. Next, the supreme-siddhi mantra and mudrā. Next, the three-division samaya heart-mantra and mudrā. Next, the three-sword verse. Next, the Tathāgata-fist mudrā. Next, the practice-hall contemplation. Next, the invitation mantra and mudrā for the heaven-disc holy assembly. Next, the accompanying deva mudrā for the heaven-disc. Next, the invitation mantra and mudrā for the earth-disc assembly. Next, the accompanying deva mudrā for the earth-disc. Next, the mantra and mudrā for the twenty-eight mansions and thirty-six animal spirits. Next, the earth-statement mantra and mudrā. Next, the obstacle-removal boundary mantra and mudrā. Next, the Jade-Maiden mantra and mudrā. Next, the samaya-revelation mantra and mudrā. Next, the arghya mantra and mudrā. Next, the flower-seat mantra and mudrā. Next, the welcome verse. Next, arghya mantra and mudrā. Next, paste-incense mantra and mudrā. Next, flower-offering mudrā. Next, burning-incense mantra and mudrā. Next, food-and-lamp mudrā. Next, universal-offering mantra and mudrā. Next, Mahācakra Vajra mantra and mudrā. Next, joined palms while reciting the names of the heaven-disc assembly, twenty-eight mansions, and thirty-six animal spirits three times. Next, recite the names of the seven stars and nine heavens twice. Next, three-division samaya heart-mantra and mudrā. Next, earth-boundary mantra and mudrā. Next, directional boundary mantra and mudrā. Next, upper boundary mantra and mudrā. Next, the pairing mudrā and mantra (whether using thread to pair the discs or not). Next, body-protection mantra and mudrā. Next, take the rosary, circle the incense-disc three times, and return it to its place. Next, form the root mudrā, take the rosary, and recite the desired deva's mantra as many times as one wishes. Next, when recitation is finished, return the rosary to its place. Next, apply paste-incense to the left. Next, flowers, paste-incense, burning-incense, food-and-drink, and lamps. Next, universal offering. Next, prayer. Next, arghya. Next, dedication of merit. Next, boundary. Next, farewell and sending off. Next, three-division body-protection mudrā and mantra. Next, exit the hall.

*Holy Deva Sequential Procedure*, one fascicle.
