# 摩利支天一印法
## The Single-Seal Method of Māricī Deva

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1259

Translated from the Chinese. Translator unknown (失譯)

---

There are two seals: first, the body seal; second, the invisibility seal.

First, form the body seal. Recite the dhāraṇī seven times at the heart and consecrate five places on the body. The five places are: first, the heart; second, the forehead; third, the left shoulder; fourth, the right shoulder; fifth, the crown of the head.

The form of the body seal: join both hands into hollow-heart fists, bending the tips of the two middle fingers to entwine them around the tips of the two index fingers. This is the Great Vajra Wheel Seal.

The dhāraṇī:

> *Oṃ Māricī svāhā*

Next, form the invisibility seal and recite it mentally one hundred and eight times at the heart. By the power of the consecration of this seal and dhāraṇī, all celestial demons, malevolent spirits, inauspicious evil demons, and heterodox practitioners cannot find their opportunity, nor can they perceive the practitioner's body and form. Even if they seek and search, exhausting all their malign power, they cannot obtain sight of the body and form of the vidyādhara practitioner. Therefore, at each of the seven occasions of daily activity, one should consecrate oneself with the seal and dhāraṇī.

The seven occasions of activity are: first, during sleep; second, upon waking; third, during bathing; fourth, during distant travel; fifth, when encountering guests; sixth, during eating and drinking; seventh, when going to the privy.

Consecrate the five places with the body seal, and with the body seal visualize oneself as hidden and entered into the fortress city of the Indestructible Vajra Wisdom-Fist. As for the invisibility seal: form the left hand into a hollow-palm fist; spread the five fingers of the right palm and cover the hollow-palm fist of the left hand, leaving no gap.

The dhāraṇī of the mind and body:

> *Oṃ ārdhyā Māricī svāhā*
