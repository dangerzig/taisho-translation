# 佛說大威德金輪佛頂熾盛光如來消除一切災難陀羅尼經
## Sūtra of the Dhāraṇī of the Great Awesome Virtue Golden Wheel Buddha-Uṣṇīṣa Blazing Radiance Tathāgata for Eliminating All Calamities, Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0964

Translated from the Chinese. Translator unknown

---

At that time, Śākyamuni Buddha was dwelling in the Pure Abode Heaven. He addressed Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva-Mahāsattva, the four assemblies, the eight divisions, the great celestial beings who traverse empty space, the nine planetary lords, the seven luminaries, the twelve zodiacal deities, the twenty-eight asterisms, and the sun, moon, and all the constellations: "In the past, at the place of Sāla-vṛkṣa-rāja Buddha, I received this Dharma of the Dhāraṇī of the Great Awesome Virtue Golden Wheel Buddha-Uṣṇīṣa Blazing Radiance Tathāgata for Eliminating All Calamities. In the future world, if in any national realm the sun, moon, five planets, Rāhu, Ketu, comets, ominous portents, or baleful stars shine upon one's natal mansion, the ruling constellation, or the planetary positions, or approach the imperial throne, and in the nation, in the household, and in the territorial domains, there is encroachment, whether advancing or entering, creating various calamities, one should set up a practice-hall in a clean place and recite this dhāraṇī sūtra with utmost sincerity one hundred and eight times or one thousand and eighty times. Whether for one day, two days, or up to seven days, one should adorn the altar according to the Dharma, receive, uphold, read, and recite it, and all calamities will naturally be eliminated and unable to cause harm. If Venus or Mars enters the Southern Dipper, creating calamities for the nation, the household, and the territorial domains, one should set up before an image of a wrathful deity, draw the form of the offending planet, and recite this true word to empower it. That calamity will immediately disperse, transferred to the body of one who is disobedient and rebellious against the king's command." Then he spoke the dhāraṇī true word:

"*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ apratihata-śāsanānāṃ oṃ kha kha khahi khahi huṃ huṃ jvala jvala prajvāla prajvāla tiṣṭha tiṣṭha sthiri sthiri saphala saphala śāntika-śrīye svāhā*"

The Buddha said: "If in any national realm, territorial domain, or upon the person of any man or woman, the celestial stars and asterisms are exercising their influence, one need only write out this sūtra and receive, uphold, read, and recite it with utmost sincerity, always maintaining purity. This dhāraṇī was proclaimed in unison by all the Tathāgatas. It can accomplish eighty thousand kinds of great auspicious matters and can further eliminate eighty thousand kinds of great inauspicious matters. If there are national kings, great ministers and their retinues, and all the common people, and they are encroached upon by the five planets, Rāhu, Ketu, comets, portents, or baleful constellations approaching the imperial throne; if in the nation, the household, and the territorial domains, the ruling mansions are beset by calamities arising in succession; if Saturn invades, whether advancing or retreating; if enemies from past lives seek to harm them; if various evil and untoward events, slander, hostile spells, curses, or talismanic writings create calamities: have all sentient beings receive and uphold this according to the Dharma, and all calamities will be unable to cause harm. Calamity will be transformed into fortune, and all will obtain auspiciousness. I teach this true word of inconceivable and incomparable merit. Receive and uphold it in secret, and do not recklessly transmit it." The Buddha told the great assembly: "If there are national realms that are unsettled and calamities arise, or if any man or woman suffers calamitous omens and misfortune, simply invite the saṃgha and establish a practice-hall according to the Dharma. Install a Buddha image, maintain pure precepts and protection, and make offerings of incense, flowers, lamps, and candles according to one's ability. This will cause all sentient beings to obtain immeasurable merit, and the calamities will immediately be eliminated."

At that time, the Tathāgata again told the great assembly: "If a person's current year is afflicted by the five planets of Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth, or by Rāhu, Ketu, the sun, the moon, and the various constellations influencing their person, with calamities arising in succession, I have a great auspicious true word called 'Destroyer of Planetary Influences.' If one can receive, uphold, and recollect it with utmost sincerity, those calamities will naturally be extinguished and misfortune will be transformed into fortune." Then he spoke the true word:

"*Oṃ sarva-nakṣatra-samaye śrīye śāntika kuru svāhā*"

At that time, when the Tathāgata was teaching this sūtra, Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva-Mahāsattva, the four assemblies, the great celestial beings who traverse empty space, the asterisms, and the entire holy assembly, all following the Buddha's command, bowed their heads in reverence, received and upheld it, and each returned to their own celestial palace. The devas, nāgas, the eight divisions, and all the rest, having heard what the Buddha taught, were all greatly delighted, faithfully received it, and practiced accordingly.

The true words of the nine planetary lords:

Metal [Venus]:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ mahā-śaṃ-vīya svāhā*

Wood [Jupiter]:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ indrāya svāhā*

Water [Mercury]:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ varuṇāya svāhā*

Fire [Mars]:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ agnaye svāhā*

Earth [Saturn]:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ pratiye svāhā*

Rāhu:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ rāhu-na asura-rājāya huṃ svāhā*

Ketu:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ vajra-ketu nakṣatra-rājāya huṃ svāhā*

Sun:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ ādityāya svāhā*

Moon:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ oṃ somāya svāhā*
