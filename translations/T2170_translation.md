# 福州溫州台州求得經律論疏記外書等目錄
## Catalog of Sūtras, Vinaya, Abhidharma Texts, Commentaries, Records, and Other Works Obtained in Fuzhou, Wenzhou, and Taizhou

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 2170

Compiled during the Tang Dynasty by Enchin (圓珍) of Japan

---

**Catalog of Enchin, Dharma-Seeking Monk from Japan**

Passing through Fuzhou, Wenzhou, and Taizhou, he obtained sūtras, Vinaya texts, Abhidharma texts, commentaries, records, and other works, totaling 458 fascicles.

The above were copied.

The above were donated by the Brahmin Tripiṭaka master Āsāryamañjuśīdhara at Kaiyuan Monastery in Fuzhou.

The above were obtained at Kaiyuan Monastery and Dazhong Monastery in Fuzhou.

The above were obtained at the residence of Zhang Dezhen in Hengyang County, Wenzhou.

The above were obtained in Yongjia Commandery, Wenzhou.

The above were copied during the sea voyage from Fuzhou to Wenzhou from the Daoist Anxi edition.

The above were copied at the residence of Qu Zonggu at Jiaoding Ridge in Taizhou.

The above were donated by the Venerable Qingling of Anning Monastery in Huangyan County, Taizhou.

The above were donated by the Venerable Zhijian of Kaiyuan Monastery in Taizhou.

The above were sought and obtained in the Taizhou region.

The above were copied at Guoqing Monastery on Mount Tiantai.

The above six fascicles were brought from the capital by Abbot Guan and copied on the twentieth day of the eighth month of the eighth year.

The above were donated by Abbot Guan for permanent use as temple offerings.

---

**Translator's note:** This text is a bibliographic catalog rather than a translated sūtra. The Brahmin master's name 阿娑阿哩耶曼蘇悉坦羅 is a phonetic transcription of a Sanskrit name, here rendered as Āsāryamañjuśīdhara; the precise Sanskrit original remains uncertain. "The eighth year" refers to a reign-period year that cannot be determined without additional context; given Enchin's time in China (853–858 CE), it likely falls within that span.
