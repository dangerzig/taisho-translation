# 聖閻曼德迦威怒王立成大神驗念誦法
## Recitation Method for the Immediate Accomplishment of Great Spiritual Power of the Holy Yamāntaka Wrathful King

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1214

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Amoghavajra (不空).

---

At that time, Śākyamuni Buddha observed the bodhisattvas, devas, nāgas, and the eightfold assembly in the Pure Abode Heaven and addressed Mañjuśrī: "In the past, throughout ten asaṃkhyeya koṭis of tathāgatas, all generated the aspiration for supreme bodhi through the Great Holy Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva and proclaimed the teaching of Mañjuśrī's true words, desiring the Buddhadharma to abide long in the world, empowering kings to protect and maintain the boundaries of their kingdoms, and guiding sentient beings through the ten wholesome actions. Now is the proper time; you should proclaim it."

The Holy Yamāntaka Wrathful King: his body rides a dark-blue water buffalo, he holds various implements, wears a necklace and crown of skulls, and a skirt of tiger skin. His body is immeasurably vast, his entire body ablaze with flames like the conflagration at the end of a kalpa. He glares in all directions like a lion charging with fierce valor. He proclaimed the supreme root true word:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ apratihata-śāsanānāṃ oṃ kara-kara kuru-kuru mama-kāri yambhañja-bhañja sarva-vighnān dahā-dahā sarva-vajra-vināyaka khamūra-dhaṭṭha-kaja-vivitanta-kara mahā-vikṛta-rūpiṇī paca-paca sarva-duṣṭa-mahā-gaṇapati-jīvitanta-kara bandha-bandha sarva-graha-śamukha-śambhuja-śacaraṇa-rudra-mānaya viṣṇu-mānaya brahma-nideva-nānaya ma-vilaṃva ma-vilaṃva rahu-rahu maṇḍala-mathi praveśaya samaya manusmara hūṃ hūṃ hūṃ hūṃ hūṃ hūṃ sphaṭa-sphaṭa svāhā*

The moment this true word was spoken, the great trichiliocosm quaked in six ways. All the palaces where Maheśvara and other great sovereign power-kings dwelt were shaken and could find no peace. They all gathered like clouds and bowed to the Great Awesome Virtue Lord, saying: "We beseech your compassion; grant us fearlessness."

At that time the Wrathful King addressed the Buddha: "This true word that I have now proclaimed: even tenth-bhūmi bodhisattvas who hear it and do not follow its teaching can be melted away, how much more so the devas, nāgas, eightfold assembly, and other obstacle-makers."

"First recite this true word a full ten thousand times, and then one can perform all manner of fierce subjugation methods.

"If there is an evil person or enemy who bears malicious intent toward a good person and seeks to harm them, one should cast a single bronze image of the Wrathful King of spiritual power, of whatever size desired. In a pure room, construct a triangular altar and place the image within it. Beneath the altar, draw that person's form or write their name. The image faces north. The practitioner wears black garments, sits facing south, forms the lance-seal, and with wrathful, forceful voice recites three times daily, twenty-one recitations each time, for a full seven days. The evil person will either contract a terrible illness, break out in terrible sores, or their body will perish.

"Another method: draw the enemy upon a pattra leaf or birch bark. Having completed the drawing, form the lance-seal and press it upon their heart. Recite the true word with empowerment one hundred and eight times, then place it beneath the image's seat. Perform this on the day of Mars at noon. Also, each day at midnight, press the lance-seal upon the image's heart and recite the true word twenty-one times. Visualize the enemy beneath the image, and their retinue and dependents will all decline and perish.

"Another method: first draw a triangular altar. Within the altar, draw the enemy's form. Take khadira wood and make a stake about four inches long, with the tip in the shape of a single-pronged vajra. Empower it with the true word one hundred and eight times, inserting that person's name within the mantra phrases, then add it below that phrase. Drive it into their heart. For one month, recite before the image three times daily, one hundred and eight recitations each time, and according to your ability make offerings and burn agaru incense. When the month is complete, the enemy will find no rest whether sitting or lying, will flee far away, and eventually will sicken unto death. If the reciter arises compassion and performs a pacification homa rite, the person will recover as before.

"Next, the great heart true word method. The true word:

*Oṃ hṛṣṭi-kāra vikṛta-dāna hūṃ sarva-śatrūn nāśaya stambhaya stambhaya sphaṭa-sphaṭa svāhā*

"Before the image of the Great Holy One, construct a triangular altar and recite this true word ten thousand times; the spiritual practice is then accomplished. Then take black mud and mold it into the form of the enemy lying supine. Place donkey dung in the belly. Take five donkey-bone stakes, each six inches long. Empower each stake individually with the true word one hundred and eight times. Drive two stakes into the left and right shoulders, two into the two thighs, and one into the heart. Sit facing south, burn agaru incense, and recite the true word ten thousand times. The enemy will fall ill, vomit blood, and die.

"Another method: before the image, construct a triangular fire-altar. Perform homa for seven nights, kindling the fire with thorny bramble-wood. Take neem leaves, write the names of two people who love each other on the leaves, placing them back to back. Wrap them with snake skin and bind the leaves with civet-hair cord. Use one hundred and eight bundles. Within the mantra phrases recite their names: one recitation, one casting into the fire. The two will come to hate and resent each other and will no longer be in harmony.

"Another method: if one wishes to cause an evil person to flee far away, take one hundred and eight crow feathers, dip them in mustard-seed oil, and burn them in a triangular furnace. Kindle the fire with thorny bramble-wood. Perform homa for one night. Within the mantra phrases recite that person's name: one recitation, one casting into the fire. That person, not of their own will, will immediately depart to a distant place.

"Another method: if one wishes to be victorious on the battlefield, take one hundred and eight blades of cogon grass, twelve finger-widths long, dip them in sesame oil, and burn them in a triangular furnace. Kindle the fire with thorny bramble-wood. Perform homa for seven nights, reciting the true word one hundred and eight times. Within the mantra phrases recite the enemy commander's name: one recitation, one casting into the fire. The enemy's battle formation will break and you will be victorious.

"Another method: take six ounces of iron powder. Recite the true word with the enemy's name within the mantra phrases. Then take one pinch, empower it with the true word, and with one recitation cast one pinch into the triangular furnace. Perform homa for seven nights. The enemy will naturally decline and perish.

"Another method: take a khadira-wood stake four finger-widths long. Within a triangular altar, draw the enemy's form using cremation-ground ash. Empower the true word one hundred and eight times, and drive the stake into the enemy's heart. Visualize your own body as the Great Awesome Virtue Wrathful Lord, and with the left foot tread upon the enemy's heart. Recite the true word one thousand times with the enemy's name in the mantra phrases. That person will be destroyed.

"Next, the heart-within-the-heart method. The true word:

*Oṃ stri-kāra-rūpa hūṃ khaṃ svāhā*

"When performing the recitation and this method, recite this heart true word and seal the five points of the body for self-protection, expulsion, and boundary-establishment. If one recites it one thousand times each day, all evil persons and enemies will have no advantage, their evil methods will fail, and all curses and sorcery will be utterly destroyed. If one has an evil dream, recite seven times and the evil dream will not come to pass.

"Another method: form the heart-within-the-heart seal and wave it in the direction of the enemy. That person's homeland will experience calamities, epidemics, drought, and floods. If one wishes to resolve this, arise the mind of great compassion and from afar visualize the people of that realm generating mutual loving-kindness toward one another, regarding each other as father, mother, son, or daughter. Recite the true word ten thousand times and the calamities will be quelled.

"Next, the root-seal method. Interlock both hands inwardly to form fists, with both middle fingers standing upright, their tips touching. This seal possesses great awesome power and great spiritual efficacy. The moment one forms this seal and recites the true word, one becomes identical to the body of the Great Awesome Virtue Wrathful Vidyārāja, without any difference.

"Next, the heart-seal method. Form the root seal as before, but extend both index fingers bent like a trident. Forming this seal and reciting the true word, all undertakings will be accomplished.

"Next, the heart-within-the-heart seal. Form the heart seal as before, but with both index fingers standing straight upright. Forming this seal and reciting the true word, one can accomplish all matters.

"Another method: if one wishes to crush an adversary, one should construct a triangular altar. Within it, paint the Great Awesome Virtue Vidyārāja, body the color of dark clouds, entire body ablaze with flames, holding various colored implements. Place three syllables on the body: *Oṃ* at the crown, *Aḥ* at the mouth, *Hūṃ* at the heart. Having completed the honored body, beneath the image place the various enemies with their names. Before this image, arrange one's own body identically to the image in the altar; the dharma of awesome spiritual power is entirely the same. Then form the seal and recite the preceding great heart true word one thousand and eighty times. Within the mantra phrases, beneath the word for 'enemy,' insert that person's name. Their followers will naturally scatter, or they will develop illness and vomit blood unto death.

"Also, paint that image and visualize one's own body as dark blue-black in color, with six feet, six heads, and six arms. The purpose is to purify the six destinies, fulfill the six pāramitās, and accomplish the six supernatural powers.

"Next, recite the three-syllable vidyā and distribute the true word upon each location accordingly:

*Oṃ Aḥ Hūṃ*

"Having accomplished this empowered body, recite the praise of the Great Awesome Virtue Vidyārāja:

*Namo varada-vajra-garbha Mañjughoṣa mahā-māla sahasa-ravaidhū vijayī vighna-rāṣṭra-rapadaka*

"Next, the twenty-three kinds of offerings, which cause the methods performed to be swiftly accomplished.

"Next, the accompanying heart true word:

*Oṃ stri-kāra-rūpa hūṃ khaṃ svāhā*

"Next, the heart-crown-mouth-heart true word:

*Oṃ Aḥ Hūṃ*

"Recitation method of the Holy Yamāntaka Wrathful King:

"Next, the twenty-three kinds of offerings. First, the summary verse:

> "Begin from sweeping the ground and reciting,
> Summoning, then arghya,
> Offering a seat, bathing, bell,
> Garments, unguent, incense, flowers,
> Empowering five-colored powder
> To encircle the altar while reciting the true word.
> Next recite for jeweled canopy and banner,
> Jeweled pavilion, jeweled tower,
> Fan and jeweled adornments,
> Lamp true word, food offering,
> And offering the recitation count.
> Sending off the Buddha is last.
> Having finished reciting, follow the painted image;
> The sequence is entirely thus.
> This verse the wise keep secret;
> The practitioner knows it of themselves.
> These are the true words,
> Counted as twenty-three.
> Now beginning from sweeping the ground.

"Sweeping the ground true word:

*Oṃ nimatha nimatha sarva-bhūtan*

"Summoning true word:

*Oṃ śamukha-śambhuja-śacaraṇa adhimusalapara-śupaśa-hasta*

"Arghya true word:

*Oṃ mahā-vighna-gataka*

"Offering a seat true word:

*Oṃ hūṃ hūṃ misphaṭa misphaṭa*

"Bathing true word:

*Oṃ sarva-bhūta-bhaya-ṭhākara*

"Vajra-bell true word:

*Oṃ aṭaṭa-hāsana-dinī*

"Offering garments true word:

*Oṃ vyāghara-camaṇi-vasana*

"Unguent true word:

*Oṃ kuru-kuru sarva-karmaṇi*

"Incense true word:

*Oṃ chinda-chinda sarva-mantra*

"Flowers true word:

*Oṃ bhinda-bhinda para-mudraṃ*

"Empowering five-colored powder to encircle the altar true word:

*Oṃ sarva-duṣṭān praveśaya praveśaya maṇḍala-mathi*

"Jeweled canopy true word:

*Oṃ ma-vilaṃva ma-vilaṃva*

"Banner true word:

*Oṃ vaivāsvata-ṇi-vidantakara*

"Pennant true word:

*Oṃ kuru-kuru mamakāri-yam*

"Jeweled pavilion true word:

*Oṃ sarva-śaparipūraka*

"Jeweled tower true word:

*Oṃ samaya manusmara*

"Fan true word:

*Oṃ sphoṭaya sphoṭaya*

"Adornment true word:

*Oṃ ākarṣaya ākarṣaya*

"Lamp true word:

*Oṃ daha daha paca paca*

"Food offering true word:

*Oṃ kha-kha-kha-hi kha-hi duṣṭa-sattvān namaka*

"Offering the recitation count true word:

*Oṃ he he bhagavan*

"Sending-off true word:

*Oṃ kiñcirāya si*

"Whisk true word:

*Oṃ vikṛta-nāna*

"For all the above true words, the practitioner need only recite three or seven times each, and the twenty-three kinds of offerings are fully accomplished. Whatever siddhi is sought will be swiftly attained.

"Among the above twenty-three vidyās, the five-colored powder vidyā should be used after the altar is constructed: empower the powder with this true word and use it to encircle the altar. The sending-off true word: after the recitation is complete and one is about to leave the practice-hall, recite this vidyā to dispatch the holy assembly.

"Another method: if one wishes the holy one to quickly descend, swiftly remove obstacles, and fulfill one's original vow, construct a triangular fire-altar as before. Take agaru incense, white mustard seeds, and human blood, mix with various poisons, red mustard seeds, and oil. Take one hundred and eight single-stemmed leaves and one hundred and eight neem branches, sharpened at both ends to twelve finger-widths, and dip them in oil. Recite the true word:

*Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ apratihata-śāsanānāṃ tadyathā he he mahā-krodha śamukha-śambhuja-śacaraṇa sarva-vighna-gataka hūṃ hūṃ kiñcirāya-si mahā-vināyaka ṇi-vidantakara nu svapananmi nāśaya rahu rahu samaya manusmara sphaṭa-sphaṭa svāhā*

"Having recited once, cast all the incense and medicines together into the fire. When all one hundred and eight are completed, all wishes shall be accomplished. At that time the holy one, not transgressing his original vow, will swiftly arrive at the practice-hall and grant empowered protection. If there are evil dreams, through the power of empowerment they will all cease to appear and will ultimately be extinguished.

"When the practitioner has completed the recitation for all the preceding methods, one should leave the practice-hall, arise the mind of compassion, and not generate thoughts of enmity or harm. Walk about at leisure and read the Mahāyāna scriptures. Abide in the stage of non-retrogression."
