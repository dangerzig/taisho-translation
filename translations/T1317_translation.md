# 甘露陀羅尼呪
## Amṛta Dhāraṇī

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1317

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Śikṣānanda (實叉難陀)

---

[Dhāraṇī]

*namo bhagavate amitābhāya tathāgatāya arhate samyaksaṃbuddhāya / tadyathā / oṃ amṛte amṛtodbhave amṛtasaṃbhave amṛtagarbhe amṛtasiddhe amṛtatejas amṛtavikrānte amṛtavikrāntagāminī amṛtagaganakīrtikare amṛtadundubhe sarvārthasādhane sarvatathāgatādhiṣṭhite sarvakleśakṣayaṃkare svāhā*

---

**Translator's note:** This text consists entirely of a phonetic transcription (Chinese characters used to approximate Sanskrit sounds) of an Amṛta dhāraṇī associated with Amitābha Tathāgata. The reconstruction above follows standard forms of this dhāraṇī attested elsewhere in the Taishō corpus. The second section heading, *Fóshuō Gānlù Tuóluóní Jīng* ("Sūtra of the Amṛta Dhāraṇī Spoken by the Buddha"), appears in the Taishō as a colophon title without additional body text.
