# 曼殊室利菩薩呪藏中一字呪王經
## Sūtra of the One-Syllable Mantra King from the Mantra Treasury of Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1182

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Yijing (義淨).

---

At that time Śākyamuni Buddha was in the Śuddhāvāsa heaven, seated in full lotus among the devas with right mindfulness, surveying the great assembly and addressing them: "You devas! Listen with utmost sincerity to the subtle and wondrous practice of Mañjuśrī the Youth, namely the altar, divine mantra, and ritual procedures, and the foremost secret and supreme mantra-heart needed for healing illness and protecting the body. It has been proclaimed and praised in unison by all tathāgatas. It is the great mantra-king. If good men or good women can recite this mantra, it is as if they have received and upheld all divine mantras.

"You devas! No one can violate this great mantra-king. Even Mañjuśrī the Youth, Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, can be summoned and drawn to come at will by this mantra, to appear before the practitioner and perform various deeds; how much more so other bodhisattvas. All worldly and supramundane divine mantras, all hindrances, are entirely dispelled by it. It possesses great valor and great awesome power, is first in divine efficacy, and is supreme among all mantras: one syllable, the most honored. This single syllable can accomplish all undertakings, can bring all wholesome dharmas to perfect fulfillment, and stands forever above all worldly and supramundane mantra arts. It is indestructible. It is the heart of all buddhas and can fulfill all wishes.

"You devas! Listen with one mind to this inconceivable mantra of greatest awesome power, the supreme divine mantra."

He then spoke the mantra:

*Oṃ ṭirahīyaṃ*

"You devas! This is the supreme one-syllable mantra-king among all secrets. No sentient being dares violate it, no ghost or spirit dares approach, all buddhas use it for auspicious deeds, and among all mantras it is the most able to accomplish whatever one desires. It is supremely exalted among all worldly things. For those seeking wealth, it possesses great sovereign power and can bring prosperity. Amid all anger, it brings loving-kindness. Toward all sentient beings, its nature is compassion. All who are rebellious will become obedient. In brief, whatever is undertaken will all be accomplished.

"Even without performing a ritual or entering an altar, by simply reciting and upholding it and touching someone with the hand, things immediately follow one's wishes. If one empowers clothing and puts it on, all people will be delighted and joyful upon seeing one. If one empowers a tooth-cleaning stick and rubs the teeth with it, toothache will immediately cease.

"If one has an eye ailment, take saindhava salt, grind it to powder, empower it seven times, and place a small amount in the eye; the pain will immediately stop.

"If one has an ear ailment, take a mushroom from an elephant or horse dung heap, together with lettuce oil and a small amount of saindhava salt. Empower these seven times, grind them together until fine, strain and extract the juice, warm it, and drip it into the ear canal; the pain will immediately stop.

"If a woman is about to give birth and is tormented by the fetus, with knotting pain in the abdomen and inability to deliver quickly, take aṭaruṣā root or ox-knee root, crush it with insect-free water. Empower it seven times and apply it below the navel, and delivery will come easily.

"If a person has been shot and an arrowhead is lodged in the body and cannot be extracted, take aged ghee, empower it one hundred and eight times, and have the patient drink it; the arrowhead will come out.

"If one suffers from indigestion with abdominal pain, vomiting and diarrhea with cholera-like symptoms and fear of death, take dark salt, saindhava salt, or any mixed salt, empower it seven times, grind it fine, and have the patient take it with warm water; they will immediately recover.

"If one suffers from persistent dysentery that will not stop, take the root of citron or quince, grind and crush it, empower it seven times, mix with water and have the patient drink it; they will immediately recover.

"If a barren woman who has never conceived wishes for children, take aśagandhā root, fry it until cooked in ghee, crush it fine, and mix with yellow cow's milk. Empower it twenty-five times. When that woman's body is clean, have her drink the medicine. The wife must not touch another man, and the husband must not touch another woman. Before long she will conceive.

"If a woman's fertility has ceased and she has had no children for three, five, or many years, whether due to others' binding spells, or from sorcery, or because of various illnesses, or from another's interference, or from having encountered poison, and through such evil conditions has no offspring: take a small amount of peacock tail feather, place it in aged ghee, and boil it several times. Grind until well-mixed, add rock sugar in the amount of a jujube, and empower it twenty-seven times. Have her take the full dose. For the next seven days, each day mix rock sugar with milk, empower it seven times each time and have her drink it. Her body will be purified, all illnesses will be cured, and she will conceive.

"If a woman suffers from unceasing menstrual bleeding, take a handful of ālambuka root or indigo root, crush it, mix with milk, and boil until cooked. Empower it one hundred and eight times and have her drink it; she will immediately recover.

"If one suffers from headache, take a crow's feather, empower it seven times, and sweep it over the painful area; the ailment will be permanently cured.

"If one suffers from malaria, whether recurring every one, two, three, or four days, or chronic heat, or temporary fever: cook milk porridge with ghee, empower it one hundred and eight times, and feed it to the patient; they will immediately recover.

"If a person has been bewitched by sorcery, cursed, or poisoned, one should form the reverse-fate mind, empower one's own face one hundred and eight times, and gaze upon the patient; their affliction will be cured.

"If a person is possessed by evil ghosts, kaṭapūtana, or other spirits, or if a child suffers from any illness caused by non-human beings who torment them: empower one's own hand one hundred and eight times and rub the patient's head, and all ailments will be cured.

"If a person is bitten by a snake or scorpion, or wounded by a mad dog, blow urgently upon the wound while reciting the mantra forty-nine times, and it will be cured.

"If a person suffers from leprosy or wasting disease, they should bathe and be clean, and in a quiet secluded place constantly recite this mantra; all will be healed.

"Anyone who recites this mantra must always stay far from evil people and unclean, foul-smelling places, and must not consume alcohol, meat, or the five pungent vegetables. If one upholds it with single-minded concentration, there will be no failure of efficacy.

"If one recites it once each day, it can protect oneself. If one recites it twice, it can protect one's companions. If one recites it three times, it can protect one household. If one recites it four times, it can protect one village. If one recites it five times, it can protect one city. If one recites it a hundred times, it can protect one country. If one recites it a thousand times, it can protect the four continents.

"In brief, what has been described here is but a summary of healing illness and protecting the body, providing timely benefit. If there are other things one wishes to accomplish, do them all as one desires; nothing will fail to be achieved."

At that time all the devas and the great assembly, hearing the Buddha proclaim this Sūtra of the One-Syllable Mantra King, joyfully accepted and put it into practice.
