## The Esoteric Seal Shared by Thirty Thousand Buddhas, Together with the Dharma of Dragon-Lineage Supreme Honored King Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 2906

Translated from the Chinese. Author unknown.

Its purple-colored light, after a thousand years of transformation, becomes a shooting star. A shooting star, after a thousand years, becomes floating water. Gold placed in water does not diminish but rather benefits the Great Purity. This is the method of the flying immortals.

Take two sheng of ritual wine and combine with poria. The immortal formula for abstaining from grain is the method of sesame. Its first name is "the three luminaries' noble glory"; second name is "prosperity"; third name is "embracing radiance"; fourth name is "blue canopy." Take its leaves and, when well ripened, pound and remove the earthy impurities. Use as much as desired and dry in broth; afterward steam it until a slight vapor emerges and the distillation fully passes through, then stop. Dry it again in the sun. Each time you steam, you must have fine clear weather. On cloudy days, you must not warm and re-steam. When entirely finished, pound it and mix with white honey. When taking it, take one pill per dose, three times daily. This enables one to live without dying.

In the eleventh month, on the first hai day, collect well-ripened Chinese scholar-tree seeds. Wear yellow clothing and take them. After a hundred days, one sees the celestial patterns above. After two hundred days, take one pill at first. After ten months, on the first jian day, collect three sheng of Chinese scholar-tree seeds. One pill for one year; after two years, all ten thousand illnesses are eliminated. Taking them for three: the faculties become keen.

Take thirty jin of pokeweed root, remove the impurities, and dry in the shade for sixty days. Grind to a powder and sift to a square-inch measure. To obtain treasures immediately without difficulty, and to walk a thousand li in a day: this formula is not to be transmitted.

The method for eliminating the three corpse-worms: constantly take orpiment and realgar in equal parts, grind to powder, and wrap in cotton. The dead ones come out. This extends the lifespan by five hundred years. Mix also with wine. On the day of destruction in the eighth month, mix the medicine. All hundred illnesses are cured. After taking three sessions, ears and eyes become keen and bright, sharing the same radiance. The upper corpse-worm departs after a hundred days, the middle after sixty days, and the lower is like an egg. The upper corpse-worm is black, the middle is blue, and the lower watches for a person's transgressions and reports them above to the Celestial Elder. Because of this, its medicinal power causes a person's hundred illnesses to remain uncured, and the five colors to be impaired. If one does not eliminate the three corpse-worms, one merely becomes an immortal sharing radiance with the sun and moon. Do not look down upon it.

Another method: on the seventeenth day of the seventh month, take the fingernails and toenails of one's hands and feet and swallow them. The three corpse-worms in the abdomen will be eliminated.

Another method: take five sheng of torreya seeds, four liang of dry firewood, four parts of lycium root, and take with water once daily. After five days, the three corpse-worms will emerge from the lower body. Guard against them. After three days, all will be well without further precaution.

The Bodhisattva Avalokiteśvara's method: take eight crabs. With half a sheng of fine wine, grind and mix with the flesh. The body can walk ten thousand li in a day. A galloping horse cannot keep up. Since innumerable kalpas, this has never been seen. Explaining it causes people to be doubtful and confused.

Take yellow-essence root, wash and remove the bad skin and hairs, finely slice, and boil one stone of water until it turns yellow. Grind and make into cakes. Take daily an amount the size of an egg. One will not age. Intelligence increases ten-thousandfold. One becomes coterminous with heaven and earth.

Pound it thoroughly, boil with one stone of water, then add sesame. It can be made into pills the size of an egg. Take three pills daily. After ten days, continue taking for a hundred days. All ten thousand illnesses are eliminated. Within two or three intervals, one then ascends to heaven.

Wash the fresh root, pound it, and mix with one sheng of raw soybean flour. Combine and make into cakes as usual. Take in a furnace, one cake at first. Afterward, three cakes daily, and gradually one will not feel hunger. After ten days, sleep will naturally cease, and there will be no need for food. After one year, one ascends. Yellow-essence's second name is "multi-storied tower"; its third name is "the immortal's surplus provisions from the Celestial Elder Sun." It is a grass produced by heaven and earth. Eating it grants the name "yellow-essence." Nourishing and eating it enables one to transcend the world. If one puts it in the mouth, the dead come back to life. If one truly believes and breathes upon wounds and injuries, those wounded are healed. This grass's essence has leaves resembling bamboo. It should be gathered in the second and eighth months, and in the third month. It is forbidden to eat plums.

The formula for abstaining from grain without hunger: roast soybeans. Together with the above six ingredients, five ge of each, pound and sift to powder. One jin of white honey can make pills the size of a plum. Swallow and take them. This enables a person to never experience hunger or thirst. The weather must be clear and bright. One should first purify the mind, observe the precepts, and bathe in fragrant water before compounding the immortal medicine.

Another method for treating the three corpse-worms: take wolfberry root, dry it, and take it, one square-inch measure, three times daily. The worms will descend clearly.

Take two sheng of hemp seeds and one sheng of soybeans. Also roast the hemp seeds, mix and pound to powder. Every day take one pinch. After one year, one can walk a thousand li daily.

The five-zhi recipe: half a jin of bird-poria, four liang of pepper, half a portion of tin, and two ge of honey. Take three pills daily, at sunrise, noon, and the hour of the monkey. White hair turns black again. Strength reaches through the void. Walking a thousand li. Wangqiao's recipe: five jin of water, three stone, in the cauldron, gradually adding water and boiling. It can be reduced to one sheng. Taste it. Then add one sheng of white honey, and further cook in a double-boiler. Five sheng: one can leap and move freely.

### The Esoteric Seal Shared by Thirty Thousand Buddhas, Together with the Dharma

Thus have I heard. At one time, the Buddha was in the Paranirmita-vaśavartin Heaven, together with great bodhisattvas who surpassed the world by a hundredfold. Their names were: Dhāraṇī Heaven Bodhisattva, Ākāśa Bodhisattva, Resplendent Light Bodhisattva, Immeasurable Banner Bodhisattva, Immeasurable Body Bodhisattva, Vajra Bodhisattva, Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, Great Resonance Bodhisattva, Vasubandhu Bodhisattva, Heavenly Ford Bodhisattva, Ocean-Eye Bodhisattva, Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva, and others. There were great bodhisattva-mahāsattvas and immeasurable śrāvakas, pratyekabuddhas, faithful men and faithful women, and others who circumambulated the Buddha a hundred times and were silent.

At that time, the Adorned World Dragon-Lineage Supreme Honored King Buddha, leading 320,000 koṭi of dharmapāla devas and their retinues, surrounded before and behind, scattered flowers. Fragrant winds blew through the groves, pervading everywhere. Radiance shone brilliantly. The earth quaked in six ways. He visited the lands and came to the heaven above. All the twenty-eight heavens, together with the sun, moon, and stars, all vanished from sight. And upon this Vaśavartin Heaven, everything was transformed to golden color. The bodhisattvas' awesome radiance was all eclipsed.

At that time, that Dragon-Lineage Supreme Honored King Buddha arrived at the Tathāgata's place and made immeasurable inquiries, then rose to face him.

At that time, the Buddha addressed him: "World-Honored One, I have through immeasurable kalpas cultivated and assembled these methods. Now I wish to exalt the great Dharma. I pray the Buddha will compassionately speak."

The voice said: "Excellent! Excellent! You are able to arouse the mind. Now quickly speak. I and the great tathāgatas of the ten directions will assist you in propagating and transforming."

Then the Supreme King Buddha again addressed him: "There is a divine seal. The buddhas of the past and the buddhas of the future, more than thirty thousand persons, have all attained the unsurpassed Way and ultimate nirvāṇa through this seal. World-Honored One, those who have not yet attained arhatship or pratyekabuddhahood, as well as ordinary beings of the lower classes who have first aroused the mind: if they seek great magical arts and sovereign mastery, they should first take a piece of rūyāsā wood one inch and eight-tenths square." This means red willow wood. "Carve the seal and carry it. A thousand numinous powers will all manifest, and ten thousand arts will naturally penetrate."

### The Dharma of Dragon-Lineage Supreme Honored King Buddha

First, establish an altar. For three days, go to the latrine only once and bathe once. Then carve the seal. While holding fragrant water, carve the seal. The mantra is:

*Viyati-ma-vidha-dhu svāhā.*

Think of Dragon-Lineage Supreme Honored King and universally make vows for swift attainment.

### Testing the Seal's Efficacy and the Signs of the Boundary

"World-Honored One, if one wishes to wear it," he said briefly, "the seal manifests many holy signs and few test-effects." Having spoken thus, he lay down. If there is an upper-class person of sharp faculties among ordinary beings, at the head of the bed there will immediately appear three pills of black maṇi heart-jewel medicine. If there is a lower-class ordinary being, at the middle of the night there will be the sound of a bell. If an ordinary being, there will be an extraordinary fragrance coming from before them. If one has sharp faculties, these things happen immediately. If one has middling faculties, it takes three days to occur. When one obtains the medicine, do not let outsiders notice. Swallow it in one breath. That person will then share the same rank as a dhāraṇī bodhisattva. After obtaining these signs, when the required days are fulfilled, one may then test the seal's arts and methods.

### The Methods for Testing the Seal's Efficacy

"World-Honored One, when one wears the seal and it has power, when wishing to test it: take seven cloves, and with the seal, stamp them, one impression each, crushing them. Mix with water and swallow. In a moment, one attains wisdom."

"If one wishes to obtain the knowledge of others' minds, also take two cloves, dry-swallow them, and with the seal, stamp the mouth three times. One immediately attains the knowledge of others' minds, foreknowing the events of three lifetimes."

"Another method: take one skull of a deceased person. Without questioning it, stamp it seven times with the seal. All the evil ghosts in the world will all come and submit."

"Another method: if one wishes to command hundreds of thousands of tens of thousands of kinds of spirits, vajra beings, and the like, take one jin of mulberry-root bark, boil until soft, remove the bad bark, and stamp it with the seal. Eat it, then repeat. Ten thousand ghosts will all come and submit, and messages will also be foreknown."

"Another method: take one sheng of clean sand, stamp it with the seal, and in the four directions, all will be freed."

"Another method: take three sheng of well-flower water, stamp it with the seal, and scatter it in the four directions. All withered things will be moistened and benefit."

"Another method: take three sheng of yellow sand, stamp it with the seal, and bury it in the earth. In one day, it will transform into gold."

"Another method: take seven hairs of the person wearing the seal, stamp them with the seal, and throw them upon the ground. They will transform into black earth."

"Another method: the person wearing the seal takes a snake, stamps it with the seal, one impression each. The snake dies from the stamping. Take it for treating leprosy, fistulas, and incurable ailments. Give a small amount of the snake to the patient to take, and they will be cured."

"Another method: if the person with the seal seeks to transcend the world, take three measures of water, boil them down, and wash the face and feet with it. In a moment, from the ground there will spring up a hundred-jeweled lotus flower to support the person with the seal."

"If seeking to fly through the air, take three sheng of water, stamp it a hundred times with the seal, and wash oneself. One will immediately levitate and depart."

"If one arouses a vast mind and seeks mastery of ten thousand Dharma-arts, take one liang of pepper and three sheng of water, boil them down, and stamp three times with the seal. Divide into three portions. At dawn, take one portion. At noon, take one portion. At night, take one portion. The signs of awakening will be similar. Afterward, one attains penetration of ten thousand dharmas. Above, one knows the thirty-three heavens. Below, one knows the suffering beings of ninety-two hundred million worlds, and can rescue them all."

"Another method: take three liang of frankincense, stamp it seven times with the seal. Yakṣas, rākṣasas, and all evil ghosts will all give rise to wholesome minds, serve the preceptor, and also take refuge."

"Another method: take one jin of cow's ghee, refine it, and place it in a white porcelain bowl. In any amount, stamp it with the seal. Take three liang daily. Taking it, one will become weary of this body. 'Because I am encroached upon by evil bandits, I relinquish this body. In the next life, I shall be reborn before the Buddha.'"

"Another method: take seven white stones, stamp them with the seal, and throw them into the water. The river spirit and all will leap out, and all poisonous nāgas will all take refuge."

"Another method: take the seal and create a jeweled pavilion."

"World-Honored One, before and after this seal, two hundred million koṭi of buddhas have all attained liberation through it. If good men and good women merely obtain this seal and carry it, when the days are fulfilled, they will be the same as a buddha."

"Another method: simply take the seal and stamp whatever place is needed. It will immediately become ash. If the seal is applied to the mouth of a deceased person whose body is still intact, stamp it with the seal, and it transforms into ash and earth."

"Another method: take a pine tree, stamp it with the seal, and it immediately transforms into a flower tree."

"Another method: take a six-foot piece of peach wood, stamp it with the seal, and carry it wherever one wishes to go, as one pleases."

"World-Honored One, the seal: I shall in my own transformation-body entrust it to the aforesaid person."

"World-Honored One, if this seal remains in any world, it is constantly known to exist only by bodhisattvas. Those who have attained sovereign mastery obtain it. Others do not see it."

"World-Honored One, if a person carries and wears this seal, the 200,000 kinds of merit of the seal-bearer cannot be fully described."

"First: from beginningless time, revolving in the six destinies, the heavy obstructions of the three kinds of karma, upon seeing this seal, are immediately extinguished. One attains the supreme reward of coolness and purity."

"Second: from immeasurable kalpas of unfilial conduct, shedding the Buddha's blood, killing arhats, slandering the Three Treasures, and other such grave transgressions, this seal permanently severs and eliminates the immeasurable heavy obstructions. One attains pure abiding."

"Third: for those constantly in the hells, hungry ghost, and animal realms, one attains a body adorned with immeasurable hundreds of merits."

"Fourth: upon hearing of this seal, one attains the severing and elimination of immeasurable obstructions."

"Fifth: from immeasurable time, having studied various dharmas and magical arts, all are perfected by this one seal's reward."

"Sixth: from immeasurable time, seeking causes and conditions, this seal enables penetration of the boundless gate of wisdom, and one accomplishes the unsurpassed way of the Dharma."

"World-Honored One, the seal: the awakened one, I also shall not grasp correct awakening. It manifests of itself, without seeking afar."

"Another method: take one sheng of ash. This world extends to other realms."

"Another method: take the seal, stamp it, and consume."

"World-Honored One, all good men: those who have the conditions, whether one person, three persons, or a hundred persons, should with effort carry and wear it. I lead immeasurable mantra-arts. The great immortals will always protect."

"Excellent! Excellent! Great being! You possess such inconceivable power. This Dharma, together with all things, shall also enable the great bodhisattvas of the ten directions to hear of it and carry this seal. In the five turbid evil world, they shall attain purification. The assembly shall constantly have arts to assist. When the mantra is used to make the seal and carry it, it surpasses the Buddha as the foremost. All attain liberation through this seal in the present. They also follow this seal and attain the great. Through this seal they attain liberation."

"World-Honored One, all good practitioners who cultivate this seal, whether sitting or lying down, need only focus the mind, and success is immediate."

When these words were spoken, all rose from their seats and addressed the Buddha: "Now, if there are good practitioners, take one liang of fine incense, crush to powder, mix with honey, and burn. Stamp with the seal. This Dharma enables us to be born together in one place and attain liberation. With immeasurable great vows, the immortals shall serve as foremost." And the solitary immortals and the great sages, with Aṃbhavā as foremost, and the three-knowledge supernatural nāgas, the eight classes, the great ghost kings, the yakṣa kings, the beast kings, the Iron Mountain King, the Great Iron Mountain King, Mucilinda Mountain King, and the six desire-heaven kings all spoke thus: "World-Honored One, we shall constantly protect with arts, never abandoning them for a moment."

The entire assembly greatly rejoiced, single-mindedly cultivating and upholding. Each made offerings to the Buddha, raising their voices on all four sides in praise of the Buddha, scattering flowers in offering.

### Dragon-Lineage Supreme Honored King Buddha's Instructions for Commanding Ten Thousand Spirits

Whenever one wishes to make this seal, ten thousand ghosts come to assemble and a thousand spirits all gather. When this seal is used in sitting or lying down, for those of immeasurable capacity, one should immediately close the eyes and enter concentration. When obtaining the result, whenever one wishes to perform a method, first press the thumb against the ring finger. Do not let the eyes see the transmission, or it will not succeed. There are also external methods of practice. When performing this method, one must not make another one separately. Anything that delights can all be accomplished by it. If the person cultivating the seal wishes to sit somewhere, simply stamp the seal immediately.
