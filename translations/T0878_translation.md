# 金剛頂經金剛界大道場毘盧遮那如來自受用身內證智眷屬法身異名佛最上乘祕密三摩地禮懺文
## Liturgy of Homage and Confession for the Secret Samādhi of the Supreme Vehicle of the Vajradhātu

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0878

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Amoghavajra (不空)

---

Opening homage: requesting the Buddhas with Brahmā-praises, following the constant [liturgy]:

"Homage to the eternally abiding, pure and wondrous Dharma-body throughout the three times: the Great Compassionate Vairocana Buddha of the Vajradhātu.

"Homage to the Vajra-Firm Self-Nature Body: Akṣobhya Buddha.

"Homage to the Merit-Adorned Accumulation Body: Ratnasambhava Buddha.

"Homage to the Enjoyment-Wisdom Body: Amitābha Buddha.

"Homage to the Transformation Body: Amoghasiddhi Buddha.

"Homage to the Great Perfect Mirror Wisdom, Vajra-Pāramitā, giving rise throughout all of empty space and the entire Dharma-realm to dust-mote worlds of pāramitās and all the Buddhas.

"Homage to the Equality-Nature Wisdom, Ratna-Pāramitā, giving rise throughout all of empty space and the entire Dharma-realm to dust-mote worlds of pāramitās and all the Buddhas.

"Homage to the Wondrous Contemplation Wisdom, Dharma-Pāramitā, giving rise throughout all of empty space and the entire Dharma-realm to dust-mote worlds of pāramitās and all the Buddhas.

"Homage to the Accomplishment-of-Action Wisdom, Karma-Pāramitā, giving rise throughout all of empty space and the entire Dharma-realm to dust-mote worlds of pāramitās and all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Bodhicitta, Vajrasattva and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Bodhicitta, Vajrarāja and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Bodhicitta, Vajrarāga and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Bodhicitta, Vajrasādhu and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Merit-Accumulation, Vajraratna and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Merit-Accumulation, Vajratejas and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Merit-Accumulation, Vajraketu and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Merit-Accumulation, Vajrahāsa and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Wisdom-Gate, Vajradharma and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Wisdom-Gate, Vajratīkṣṇa and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Wisdom-Gate, Vajrahetu and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Wisdom-Gate, Vajrabhāṣa and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Great Vigor, Vajrakarma and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Great Vigor, Vajrarakṣa and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Great Vigor, Vajrayakṣa and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Great Vigor, Vajramuṣṭi and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, of one and the same nature, born of the Vajradhātu body, all the Buddhas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Delight, Vajralāsyā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the cloud-ocean of offerings.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Great Awakening, Vajramālā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the cloud-ocean of offerings.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Wondrous Dharma-Sound, Vajragītā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the cloud-ocean of offerings.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Supernatural Action, Vajranṛtyā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the cloud-ocean of offerings.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Suchness-Perfuming, Vajradhūpā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the cloud-ocean of offerings.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Supreme Adornment, Vajrapuṣpā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the cloud-ocean of offerings.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Constant Universal Illumination, Vajrālokā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the cloud-ocean of offerings.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Constant Offering, Vajragandhā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all bodhisattva mahāsattvas of the cloud-ocean of offerings.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Fourfold Gathering Wisdom, the Bodhisattva Vajrāṅkuśa and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all accomplishing bodhisattva mahāsattvas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Skillful Wisdom, the Bodhisattva Vajrapāśa and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all obedient bodhisattva mahāsattvas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Firm Wisdom, the Bodhisattva Vajrasphoṭa and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all messenger bodhisattva mahāsattvas.

"Homage to all the Tathāgatas' Joyful Wisdom, the Bodhisattva Vajraghaṇṭā and all throughout empty space and the entire Dharma-realm, all compliant bodhisattva mahāsattvas."

The above thirty-seven deities of the great maṇḍala of the Vajradhātu are all the inner retinue of the Dharma-Buddha's direct realization of bodhi, mutually embodied with Vairocana.

"Homage to the eternally abiding, equally Three Jewels of the three times throughout all the inconceivable, inconceivable dust-mote world-oceans and assemblies of the lotus-treasury world-systems of the ten directions. Universally for the five classes: all the world-lords of the devas, parents of the nine realms both worldly and transcendent, all good teachers, donors to the place of practice, and all sentient beings throughout the realm without remainder, may all sever their obstructions. Taking refuge with devotion, I confess; with utmost sincerity I confess:

> "Throughout beginningless cyclic existence in all states of being,
> The transgressions born of karma of body, speech, and mind:
> As all the Buddhas and bodhisattvas have confessed,
> So now do I confess likewise."

Having confessed, with utmost sincerity take refuge and bow to the Great Compassionate Vairocana Buddha. With utmost sincerity, rejoice:

> "One should further deeply generate a mind of joy,
> Rejoicing in all accumulations of merit and wisdom.
> Among the practices and vows of all the Buddhas and bodhisattvas,
> The merit born of the vajra three karmas,
> And the good roots amassed by pratyekabuddhas, śrāvakas, and sentient beings:
> In all of these I rejoice."

Having rejoiced, with utmost sincerity take refuge and bow to the Great Compassionate Vairocana Buddha. With utmost sincerity, exhort and request:

> "Contemplate all the Buddhas seated beneath the bodhi-tree,
> And with my own body request each to turn the Dharma-wheel.
> All the lamps of the world seated in the place of practice,
> Whose awakened eyes open and illuminate the three realms of existence:
> All the Tathāgatas, lords of the three realms,
> Who are about to enter the parinirvāṇa without remainder,
> I exhort and request to remain long in the world,
> Not to abandon their compassionate vows, and to rescue the world."

Having exhorted and requested, with utmost sincerity take refuge and bow to the Great Compassionate Vairocana Buddha. With utmost sincerity, transfer merit:

> "The merit of confession, rejoicing, and exhortation:
> May I never lose the mind of bodhi.
> Among the wondrous assemblies of all the Buddhas and bodhisattvas,
> May I always be a good friend, never abandoned.
> Far from the eight difficulties, born without hindrance,
> With the knowledge of past lives, my body adorned with marks.
> Far from foolish delusion, endowed with compassion and wisdom,
> May I fully perfect all the pāramitās.
> Wealthy and prosperous, born in a noble family,
> With a vast and ever-flourishing retinue.
> The four unobstructed wisdoms, the ten sovereign powers,
> The six penetrations and all dhyānas, all perfectly complete:
> As Vajraketu and Samantabhadra,
> So do I praise and transfer merit likewise."

Having transferred merit and made vows, with utmost sincerity take refuge and bow to the Great Compassionate Vairocana Buddha. What follows proceeds in the regular sequence.

Address the assembly: each should recite this verse of purity:

> "All dharmas are like reflected images,
> Pure and without blemish.
> They cannot be grasped through apprehension or speech;
> All arise from karmic causes."
