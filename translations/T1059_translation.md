# 千手千眼觀世音菩薩治病合藥經
## Thousand-Handed Thousand-Eyed Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva's Sūtra on Curing Diseases and Compounding Medicines

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1059

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Bhagavaddharma (伽梵達摩).

---

At that time the Buddha told Ānanda: "The dhāraṇī of the Great Compassionate Heart of Complete and Perfect Unobstructed Great Compassion spoken by this Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva is true and not false. If any person has a wish to make and desires to beseech Avalokiteśvara, the Sovereign Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, take guggula incense, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and burn it. Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva will assuredly come at once.

"If any person is afflicted by a malicious ghost, take mṛttikā, burn it to ash, mix it with clean clay, and form it into the shape of the malicious ghost. Before the image of the Thousand Eyes, take a steel knife, empower it with the spell one hundred and eight times, and cut the figure into one hundred and eight pieces, calling out the person's name. The person will immediately recover, permanently cured, and will never again be afflicted.

"If any person has been harmed by gu-poison sorcery, take karpūra incense and guggula incense in equal parts, mix them with one measure of fresh well-water, and boil them down to half a measure. Strain out the dregs and take the juice. Before the image of the Thousand Eyes, empower it with the spell one hundred times; the person will immediately recover.

"If any person has been stung by a venomous snake or scorpion, take dried ginger, grind it fine, empower it with the spell seven times, and apply it to the wound. The person will immediately be cured.

"If any person has a wicked enemy who with malicious words plots to do them harm, take clean earth, or flour, or wax, and form it into the likeness of that wicked person. Before the image of the Thousand Eyes, take a steel knife, empower it with the spell one hundred and eight times, making one cut and calling the person's name with each recitation. Burn all one hundred and eight pieces. That person will then become delighted, cultivate self-discipline, treat one with warmth and respect, and love and revere one. All enmity will be gone.

"If any person suffers from damage to the eyes, whether from clear blindness, dark blindness, white cataracts, red films, or loss of light, take one each of harītakī fruit, āmalakī fruit, and vibhītakī fruit, crush them, strain through a sieve with oil, and while grinding, keep the preparation strictly clean. Do not let a woman who has recently given birth or a dog see it. Continuously recite the Buddha's name in the mouth. Mix with white honey or human milk and seal into the eyes. The human milk must be from a mother who has borne a son; if it is from a mother of a daughter, it will still be effective for compounding the medicine. When the mixing is complete, before the image of the Thousand Eyes empower it with the spell one thousand and eight times, then apply it to the eyes. For a full seven days remain in a dark room, avoiding wind, the bedchamber, the five pungent foods, and all impure substances. The eyesight will be restored to bright, clear, and vigorous clarity.

"If any person suffers from malarial ghost-disease, take the skin of a tiger, leopard, jackal, or wolf, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and have the person wear it on their body. They will immediately recover. Lion skin is the most efficacious.

"If any person has been bitten by a snake, take a knot of cloth from the bitten person's garment, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and place it on the wound. The person will immediately recover.

"If any person suffers from a severe case of tetanus entering the heart and is about to die from unconsciousness, take one ball of peach resin, as large as a peach fruit, mix it with one measure of clear water and boil it. Take half a measure, empower it with the spell seven times, and have the person drink it all at once. They will immediately recover. Do not let a woman boil this medicine; it must be prepared under clean conditions. In general, whenever medicines are compounded, if they are not prepared by the proper person, they will have no efficacy.

"If any person suffers from the transmitted-corpse disease, ghost-qi, or persistent wasting illness, take guggula incense, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and burn it to fumigate the person's nostrils. Also take seven pills the size of rabbit droppings, empower them with the spell fourteen times, and have the person swallow them. They will immediately recover. Avoid alcohol, meat, the five pungent foods, and abusive language. Also take manāśilā mixed with white mustard seed and salt formed into cakes, empower them with the spell twenty-one times, and burn them beneath the sick person's bed. The ghost causing the illness will have its body split apart, and will flee in retreat and not dare remain.

"If any person suffers from hemiplegia on one side, with ears and nose blocked and hands and feet impaired, take sesame oil and boil it with costus root, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and rub it on the body. The person will be permanently cured. Also take pure cow ghee, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and rub it on the body; the person will recover.

"If any woman suffers from difficult childbirth, take sesame oil, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and rub it on the laboring woman's navel and birth canal, or have her swallow it to ease the delivery.

"If any woman has a dead fetus in her womb, take one large measure of ābhāmṛtā grass, mix it with two measures of water and boil it, strain out the dregs, and take one measure of juice. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and have her drink it. The fetus will immediately come out, without any pain. If the afterbirth does not come out, drinking this medicine will also cause it to be expelled.

"If any person suffers sudden, unbearable heart pain, which is caused by corpse-lodging disease, take one ball of turūṣka incense of the nipple-shaped variety, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and chew and swallow it. The amount does not matter; have the person vomit and they will immediately recover. Avoid the five pungent foods, alcohol, meat, oily substances, and all impure things, as well as sexual activity.

"If any person has been severely burned, take warm gomaya, empower it with the spell fourteen times, and apply it to the wound. The person will immediately recover.

"If any person suffers from roundworms gnawing at the heart causing pain, take half a measure of black pepper, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and have the person take it. They will immediately recover. In severe cases, take a full measure; the worms will come out twisted together like a rope, and the person will recover.

"If any person suffers from sores on the lower body, take the leaves of lingxiao, pound and squeeze out the juice, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and apply it to the eyes at night before bed. The person will immediately recover. If needed, one may also use the juice from white stallion's dung, empowered with the spell twenty-one times; apply as above and the person will recover.

"If any person suffers from abdominal illness and pain, take well-water and mix in two lumps of formed salt, empower them with the spell twenty-one times, and have the person drink half a measure. They will immediately recover.

"If any person suffers from red eyes, has a fleshy growth in the eye, or has cataracts, take śamī leaves, pound them, and squeeze out the juice. Empower it with the spell seven times, soak a copper coin in it overnight, then empower it another seven times, and apply it to the eyes. The person will immediately recover.

"If any person suffers from nocturnal anxiety and restlessness, going out and coming in with fright and dread, take a white thread and make it into a cord. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times, tying twenty-one knots, and fasten it around the neck. The terror will be removed. Not only does it remove fear, but it also generates merit, extinguishes transgressions, and extends one's lifespan.

"If any person's household is beset by sudden calamities and disasters, take pomegranate branches and cut them into one thousand segments. Smear both ends with ghee, curds, and honey. With each recitation of the spell, burn one segment, completing one thousand and eight recitations. All calamities, disasters, and afflictions will be entirely extinguished. This must be performed before the image of the Thousand Eyes.

"If one takes white acorus root, empowers it with the spell one hundred and eight times, and ties it to the right arm, one will prevail in all debates and discussions one encounters.

"If one takes śamī leaves or their branch-stems cut to the length of one inch, smears both ends with pure cow ghee, white honey, and cow curds, and with each recitation of the spell burns one piece, completing one thousand and eight pieces, performing this three times each day, one hundred recitations each time, for a full seven days, the spell-master will naturally awaken to penetrating wisdom.

"If any practitioner wishes to subdue great and powerful ghosts and spirits, take ariṣṭaka dye, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and burn it in a fire, adding ghee, curds, and honey. This must be performed before the image of the Compassionate One.

"If one takes one large measure of gorocanā, places it in a crystal vessel, sets it before the image of the Great Compassion, and empowers it with the spell one thousand and eight times, then anoints one's body and marks one's forehead, all devas, nāgas, ghosts, spirits, humans, and non-humans will all be delighted.

"If any person is shackled in manacles, fetters, cangues, or chains, take white ceruse, empower it with the spell one hundred and eight times, rub it on the hands, and use those hands to touch the manacles, fetters, cangue, or chains. They will unlock of themselves.

"If a husband and wife are in discord, quarreling like water and fire, take the tail-feathers of a mandarin duck and before the image of the Great Compassionate Heart empower them with the spell one thousand and eight times. If the person wears them, they will rejoice in mutual love and respect for the rest of their lives.

"If any person's fields and crops or five kinds of fruit trees are being eaten by insects, take clean ash and sand, empower it with the spell one thousand and eight times, and scatter it around the four borders of the fields. The insects will retreat and scatter. For fruit trees, additionally empower water with the spell one hundred and eight times or up to one thousand and eight times, and sprinkle it on the trees. The insects will not dare to eat and harm the fruit.

"If any person encounters a ghost that seals their mouth, causes them to foam and spit, lose consciousness, change color, and is about to die, take fine filings of amber and jade, empower them with the spell one hundred and eight times, and have the person take them with wine. Also take the squeezed juice of kuravaka, empower it with the spell fourteen times, and have the person drink it. Also mark the ghost-sigil on the person's forehead, empower it with the spell one hundred and eight times, and the person will immediately recover.

"If any person has been scalded and burned, take wood ash mixed with water, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and plaster it on the wound three times a day. Also take warm gomaya, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and apply it to the wound. The person will immediately recover.

"If any person has consumed the flesh of various livestock and is afflicted by calamitous poison, with the heart troubled and on the verge of death, take one measure of clean earth, boil it with three measures of water, and take one measure of the juice. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and have the person drink it. They will immediately recover.

"If any person suddenly develops various tumors and swellings, take turnip leaves, pound them, and mix them with clear wine. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and apply it to the swelling. The person will immediately recover.

"If any person has a malignant swelling that has entered the abdomen and they are about to die, take gomaya, burn it, and mix it with wine. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and apply it to the swelling. Also have the person drink some. They will immediately recover.

"If any person suffers from a great nosebleed and is about to die, take fresh penglai herb, boil it with water and take the juice. The amount does not matter. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and have the person swallow it. Their life will be saved.

"If any person suffers from bloody dysentery or bloody stool, take peach resin the size of a chicken egg, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and have the person swallow it. They will immediately recover.

"If any person suffers from coughing and wheezing, take one measure of peach kernels, heat them on a fire and mix them with malt sugar. Empower them with the spell one hundred and eight times and have the person take them all at once. If necessary, prepare three or four doses; the illness will be cured.

"If any person suffers from itching of the anus, take the fruit of the cāṃraka vine, grind it to a fine powder, and mix it with sugar. Empower it with the spell one hundred and eight times and apply it to the anus three times a day. The person will immediately recover.

"If any person suffers from constipation, take two measures of mallow seeds, boil them with four measures of water, and take one measure of the juice. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and have the person drink it repeatedly. The bowels will immediately move.

"If any person suffers from urinary retention, take the juice squeezed from cow-calf dung, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and have the person drink it. They will immediately recover.

"If any person suffers from urinary retention, take dust sifted from the beam inside the house, pinch a three-finger measure, and mix it with clear water. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and have the person drink it. They will recover.

"If any person suffers from frequent and excessive urination, with copious output, take one measure of trichosanthes root, boil it with three measures of clear water, and squeeze out half the juice. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times or one hundred and eight times. Have the person drink it all at once. They will immediately recover.

"If any woman suddenly falls ill during pregnancy, boil five measures of red beans and three measures of fermented soybean with one large measure of clear water. Take three measures of the juice, empower it with the spell one hundred and eight times, and divide it into two doses. The illness will immediately be cured, and the delivery will be peaceful and easy.

"If any woman suffers from a breech presentation causing difficult delivery and is about to die, take one measure of penglai herb, boil it with three measures of water, and take one measure of the juice. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and have her drink it. She will immediately give birth without illness.

"If any infant cries at night and cannot sleep peacefully, write the character for 'ghost' below the child's eyes, empower it with the spell twenty-one times, and spit three times. The crying will immediately cease.

"If any infant develops various sores on the head, take gomaya mixed with ox-horn scrapings, burn them to powder, and mix with pig fat. Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and apply it to the sores. They will immediately heal.

"If any infant's tongue is swollen and they cannot nurse, take juice pressed from the eastern direction, empower it with the spell one hundred and eight times, and apply it to the tongue. The infant will immediately recover.

"If any infant develops sores in the mouth and cannot eat, take coptis root, pound it finely and sift it, and mix it with a nursing mother's milk (from a mother who has borne a son). Empower it with the spell twenty-one times and apply it to the mouth sores. They will immediately heal."

At that time Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva told the great assembly: "After the Tathāgata's parinirvāṇa, the sentient beings of the final age will suffer from innumerable diseases of the mouth and all kinds of afflictions. Therefore I now teach these methods of cure, which are likewise as described. If any bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, upāsikās, good men, or good women are able to receive, uphold, and practice these methods of cure to save sentient beings from suffering, they should know that such a person is my transformation body. Because with a great compassionate heart they save sentient beings, I shall certainly go to their place and make these dharma-methods yield supreme efficacy. Such a person, who upholds the Great Compassionate Heart's Wish-Fulfilling Sovereign Dhāraṇī's divine and wondrous formulas that can save the world from suffering, is like me, no different from my own body.

"If any man or woman of pure faith is able to receive and uphold this suffering-saving dhāraṇī spell, and while applying the worldly medical methods to treat various diseases does so with a compassionate heart, necessarily empowering with the spell one hundred and eight times with utmost sincerity, then by the power of Avalokiteśvara all treatments will be efficacious. If any sentient being upholds my dharma, at the time of that person's death I shall swiftly come to welcome them, and upon a jeweled cloud-chariot with boundless bliss, I shall speedily cause them to be reborn in the World of Peace and Happiness, born from a lotus, and they shall attain buddhahood before long."

At that time Śākyamuni Buddha praised Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, saying: "Excellent, excellent! Great Being! So it is, so it is, just as you have said. If any good man or good woman gives even one portion of medicine to one who is ill, in the present life they will gain merit and extinguish transgressions, and in future lives their reward of merit will be inexhaustible. Life after life, world after world, they will receive a body free from illness, enjoy all manner of happiness, receive supreme benefits among devas and humans, attain immeasurable lifespan among devas and humans, and before long attain the way."

At that time the great assembly in the gathering, the bodhisattva mahāsattvas, Brahmā, Śakra, the four kings, devas, nāgas, yakṣas, all the spirit-kings and ghost-kings, humans, and non-humans, having heard what the Buddha taught, all greatly rejoiced, accepted it with faith, bowed in homage, dedicated the merit, and put it into practice.
