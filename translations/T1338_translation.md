# 呪三首經
## Scripture of Three Dhāraṇīs

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1338

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Divākara (地婆訶羅)

---

## I. The Great Wheel Vajra Dhāraṇī

*Namaḥ sattriyādivikāṇāṃ tathāgatānāṃ oṃ virati virati mahācakravajri satta satta sarate sarate traye traye vidhamani sambhavani tramati siddhāgṛtaṃ svāhā*

Reciting this dhāraṇī twenty-one times, one will immediately be admitted into all maṇḍalas, and all that one undertakes will be accomplished.

## II. The Dhāraṇī of Sūryaprabha Bodhisattva

*Namo buddha-guṇe namo dharma-mahā namo saṃgha-tāya nityatripuṣpa-stutaṃ namaḥ*

## III. The Dhāraṇī of Māricī

*Oṃ māricye sarva-sattvā adhari sari svāhā*

Reciting this dhāraṇī greatly protects a person's body. Each day, at three separate times, recite it one hundred and eight times each.
