# 阿彌陀佛說呪
## Dhāraṇī Spoken by Amitābha Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0369

Translated from the Chinese. Translator unknown (失譯)

---

*[Dhāraṇī:]*

> Namo buddhāya. Namo dharmāya. Namo saṃghāya. Namas Amitābhāya tathāgatāyārhate samyaksaṃbuddhāya. Tadyathā: amṛte amṛtodbhave amṛtasaṃbhave amṛtavikrānte gāmine gaganakīrtike vara-pratyakṣayaṃka svāhā.

---

*Translator's note: This text consists entirely of a phonetically transcribed Sanskrit dhāraṇī. The opening section — the triple refuge (namo buddhāya, namo dharmāya, namo saṃghāya), the homage to Amitābha (namas Amitābhāya tathāgatāyārhate samyaksaṃbuddhāya), and the amṛta- sequence (amṛte, amṛtodbhave, amṛtasaṃbhave, amṛtavikrānte gāmine gagana-) — conforms to the standard Amitābha dhāraṇī known from related sources. The final cluster preceding svāhā (Chinese: 稽唎底迦婆囉皤波跢叉焰迦) is rendered here with a partial reconstruction; the precise Sanskrit underlying these syllables is uncertain.*
