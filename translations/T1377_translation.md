# 佛說寶帶陀羅尼經
## The Buddha's Teaching: Jewel-Belt Dhāraṇī Sūtra

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1377

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dānapāla (施護).

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time the Buddha was at the dwelling place of the Śākya clan, in the city of Kapilavastu, in the Nyagrodha Tree Garden, together with a great assembly. At that time the boy Rāhula was staying in a different place when, during the night watch, he was suddenly frightened by a great evil rākṣasa. That very night, Rāhula, surrounded by a group of boys before and behind him, left the city of Kapilavastu and went to the place of the Buddha. Upon arriving, he bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and then stood weeping before the Buddha.

At that time the World-Honored One, knowing this matter, addressed the Venerable Ānanda: "Ānanda! You should know that I have a great vidyā called the Jewel Belt, which has great merit and can provide protection for the boy Rāhula. I shall now proclaim it, and you should receive and uphold it. This great vidyā: whether the hosts of devas, nāgas, yakṣas, rākṣasas, asuras, garuḍas, gandharvas, kiṃnaras, piśācas, pretas, kumbhāṇḍas, pūtanas, kaṭapūtanas, the hosts of constellations, the twenty-eight great yakṣa generals, the four great yakṣa lords, the four great heavenly kings, the hosts of sages free from desire, those dwelling on the Himalayas, and even all the hosts of bhūtas and rākṣasa-women of the ten directions, and the mother-ghost hosts such as Kāṇirī, Puṣyanārī, Keṭa, Karaṇṇā, Sumanā, and Mahāhārī, and the three great rākṣasas Mudgā and Mikarapā, and the great rākṣasa-woman Hārī of the east, and the great rākṣasa-woman Guṇasī of the south, and the great rākṣasa-woman Keśarī of the west, and the great rākṣasa-woman Jewel-Topknot of the north, each of these four great rākṣasa-women together with their sixty retainers: all such beings, upon hearing the Jewel-Belt great vidyā proclaimed, will in all places provide protection for all sentient beings."

Then the World-Honored One spoke the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Ghaṇi graha-gaṇa-pati gumī graha-guma-pati parikā-gharī-ni-ghara-pati sara-pati dhana-pati sura-pati aripa aripa-pati ma-pati a-pati mahu-pati hura-hīri huru-tīri hīri-cicciri pata-paṭha-ṭha-ṭha-ve oṃ laṃ-vima-vi sarva mahā-hīri hu-hu samanta-sa-sari a-samanta-sara-parikha-sari avarta mahā-varta ghaṇi-mikhari.*

The Buddha said: "Ānanda! This Jewel-Belt great vidyā that I have proclaimed has great merit. If one who upholds this vidyā seeks anything whatsoever, all will be fulfilled. All the hosts of vidyādharas, bhūtas, vetāḍas, great powerful devas, and sages of supernatural powers and vigor will be unable to create obstacles for the upholder of this vidyā. Ānanda! You should know that this Jewel-Belt great vidyā was jointly proclaimed by the buddha world-honored ones and universally provides benefit and happiness for all sentient beings, even secretly providing protection in all places. Ānanda! All the great evil beings on earth who are difficult to tame and who steal others' majesty, and those of extremely evil karma, whether in any direction or any corner, and even in all places, upon hearing this Jewel-Belt great vidyā, are all greatly terrified. Furthermore, within a circumference of ten yojanas, this great vidyā can provide protection for all sentient beings, rescuing them from difficulties, sustaining and guarding them with mindful protection, enabling all sentient beings to have calamities pacified and to be at ease. They will not be beaten by staffs, not be wounded by blades, and not be poisoned; all evil poisons will naturally be destroyed, evil companions will be kept far away, and all evil beings' curses and binding spells will naturally be released. If humans or non-humans wish to cause harm to any sentient being, one should establish an altar according to the method, make offerings to the Tathāgata with various incenses and flowers, set boundaries according to the method, bathe and purify oneself, and recite this Jewel-Belt great vidyā. Those evil beings will naturally be subdued and unable to cause harm, and even all illness and suffering will be eliminated." He further spoke the great vidyā:

*Tathā-nīri-nīri vimalī-tamali huci-huci siri-tiri sari-ra.*

"Furthermore, Ānanda! This great vidyā also has great merit. When this great vidyā is spoken, if the ghost hosts hear it they are terrified and tremble, if the deva hosts hear it they all come to assemble, if the nāga kings or bhūta kings hear it they also all come to gather, and if Brahmā, Śakra, Maheśvara, Kumāra-deva, and others hear this great vidyā, they will all come from their various places to gather here, praising and rejoicing in the Jewel-Belt great vidyā."

Then Maitreya Bodhisattva Mahāsattva addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I too wish to create a protective dharma for the boy Rāhula and all sentient beings, rescuing them from difficulties, sustaining and guarding them with mindful protection so they may have calamities pacified and be at ease. Blades, staffs, and poisons will be unable to harm them, and evil companions and curses will naturally stay far away. For the sake of this benefit, I joyfully proclaim the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:"

*Tathā avana-ti-pana-ni ani-na-nu-juru-juru mṛddha-nandani a-dhana-ni samanta-nandani nirmūni nirmī-mu-ni śānti svastidhye namaḥ mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca rakṣa kuru svāhā.*

Then the lord of the sahā world, Great Brahmā Heavenly King, also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: aṅguri paṅguri a-nurdri-nuṇḍri śikke-śikkhare nuddha-mṛddri-nurdri-nura-yati aca-aca vimuca śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ kuru mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca svāhā.*

Then the Yāma Deva also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: rasthiri panasthiri rudi-rakāraṇi gaṃbhīri-nurdri-nari-muci-śakeya-ra-ha śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ karśami mama rakṣa kuru svāhā.*

Then Śakra, lord of the devas, also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: nandi-dhanuṣi su-nandi su-nandi te-nandini te-nandini nurdri-mī-sumi-dhama-ni-dhami-tiri-miri mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca rakṣa karu-mi śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

After Śakra, lord of the devas, had spoken this great vidyā, he further spoke another great vidyā:

*Satyam: nandi-dhanuṣi-kanda-yuta-kṛmukī-jvālī-dhami vihāri-sarsa-rāvatī śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ-dhūpa-bhavatu svāhā.*

Then Dhṛtarāṣṭra, Heavenly King, also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: dhari-dhari dhāraṇi dhari a-damti puru-muru śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca rakṣa karu-mi yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ-dhūpa-bhavatu svāhā.*

Then Virūḍhaka, Heavenly King, also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: aṇḍari paṇḍari kāraṇi-kāra-pati kāra-rubhi-kari-dhari-kānaka-pati-ri-ruru-mi kṣa-kṣami a-cimi bhakci śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca rakṣa kuru yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ-dha-bhavatu svāhā.*

Then Virūpākṣa, Heavenly King, also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: majara-majara-jaja-mara-dhīri-dhīri-keri-keri samanta-keri śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the Moon Deva also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: oṃ vane vimalī prabhā-sari-śata-raśmi-sahasra-raśmi prati-sarāti śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ kuru mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the hosts of devas also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: kuru-kuru suru-suru puru-puru śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ kuru mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the hosts of constellations also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: tari-tamali-ari-dari ākāśa-vimalī śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ karu-mi mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the hosts of nāgas also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: mali-mali aca-ri-mati aśu-mati puke śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ kuru mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the twenty-eight great yakṣa generals also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: nardha-pati nardha-pati ruru-dhara ruru-dhara śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ karu-mi mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the hosts of pretas, piśācas, and kumbhāṇḍas also jointly and joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: vibhūti saṃvarta-na vibhūti śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the hosts of pūtanas and kaṭapūtanas also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: padmi kāraṇi uddhata-cūraṇḍa samīraṇi cucu-pati śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ karu-mi mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca rakṣa ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the hosts of mother-ghosts also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: keri-makeri nāri-ṇa-mari śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ karu-mi mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

Then the four great rākṣasa-women of the four directions also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: ani-pāni ghaṭi-ghavāṭi ku-kurīri pipari-hari śāntiṃ svastidhye namaḥ karu-mi mama sarva-sattvānāṃ ca yāvac-ciram-mantaṃ karu-mi svāhā.*

At that time there was in the assembly a great rākṣasa-woman named Rāmiṇā, who also joyfully proclaimed the Jewel-Belt great vidyā:

*Satyam: nardhi-nari nardhi-nari ha-ha-ha-ha hī-hī-hī-hī hu-hu-hu-hu śiku-śiku-śiku-śiku ciku vimukhe mahā-jakhake ṭhami-ci-aṭṭa-aṭṭa-ṭhara-ṭha-ṭha-krama-jaku-juru-juru nu-nu-nu-nu ru-nu-ru-nu ce-miri-mi miri-ana-gha-gha-ghanu-mi nu-nu-nu-ti-thiti-mi-ti-nī-thiri-mi ṭha-citi-mi ci-ri-ci pi-ci-pi-tiri-pe-tiri-ni-nu-hī-ni-mu-hī-ni ra-khe-va-khe dapa-ye.*

When the rākṣasa-woman had spoken this great vidyā, she came before the Buddha and said: "This retinue-vidyā within the Jewel-Belt great vidyā that I have spoken well provides protection for all sentient beings, pacifying calamities and bringing ease. Blades, staffs, and evil poisons cannot cause harm, and all evil companions will naturally stay far away, beyond one yojana, two yojanas, and even up to twelve yojanas. Whether devas, nāgas, yakṣas, rākṣasas, gandharvas, asuras, garuḍas, kiṃnaras, mahoragas, kumbhāṇḍas, piśācas, pretas, pūtanas, kaṭapūtanas, skandhas, apasmāras, vetāḍas, one-day fevers, two-day fevers, and even humans or non-humans who seek an opportunity to cause harm, they will be unable to gain any advantage. If they could gain advantage, such a thing is impossible, except for fixed karmic obstructions from past deeds that cannot be prevented. Furthermore, this great vidyā enables all sentient beings in all places to have all fears pacified and to be far removed from all disturbances."

Then the World-Honored One addressed the Venerable Ānanda: "The Jewel-Belt great vidyā that I have spoken, together with the retinue-vidyā phrases of the Jewel Belt spoken by this rākṣasa-woman and others, have great merit. You should receive, uphold, proclaim, and widely disseminate them. Furthermore, this great vidyā can also restrain all evil winds and storms. If one upholds this vidyā, one can well protect oneself and also protect others; whether in cities, towns, villages, or even all places, it can provide complete protection. Furthermore, among all devas, nāgas, ghosts, and spirits, and even among śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, followers of non-Buddhist paths, Nirgrantha ascetics, and the like, if there are those with evil and poisonous minds, those who are untamed, those who cause harm, or those who are inauspicious, all can be tamed and will not cause harm, and even all great evil constellations can be restrained.

"Furthermore, Ānanda! The Jewel-Belt great vidyā is the most supreme and excellent. All the mantra phrases of śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, followers of non-Buddhist paths, Nirgrantha ascetics, and also of Brahmā, Śakra, the four great heavenly kings, the Desire-Freedom Deva, the sun, moon, and constellations, devas, nāgas, ghosts, spirits, yakṣas, rākṣasas, humans and non-humans, those dwelling on mountain peaks, those dwelling at the base of mountains, those dwelling in space, and even all the bhūtas dwelling in every place: this vidyā can sever all of their mantra phrases, for it cannot be severed by others' mantras.

"Furthermore, Ānanda! The Jewel-Belt great vidyā well provides protection, well rescues, and well sustains. It enables sentient beings to have calamities pacified and to be at ease. Blades, staffs, and evil poisons cannot cause harm, and all evil companions will naturally stay far away, beyond one yojana, two yojanas, and even up to twelve yojanas.

"Furthermore, Ānanda! I do not see in all the worlds of devas, humans, māras, Brahmā, and the rest any being that violates this Jewel-Belt great vidyā. If they were to violate it, such a thing is impossible. Why? Ānanda! You should know: all buddhas are true, the Dharma is true, the Saṃgha is true, truthful speech is true, and all the hosts of ṛṣis are true. Therefore, within this secret dharma-gate, there is no violation. Ānanda! These five kinds of truth cannot be violated: the truth of the Buddha cannot be violated, the truth of the Dharma cannot be violated, the truth of the Saṃgha cannot be violated, the truth of truthful speech cannot be violated, and the truth of the hosts of ṛṣis cannot be violated. Therefore, Ānanda, this Jewel-Belt great vidyā: if anyone receives and upholds it, they will be freed from all illness, liberated from all bonds, and have all evil eliminated, attaining great ease and supreme auspiciousness."

When the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the boy Rāhula was freed from his terror. Ānanda and the great assembly, having heard what the Buddha taught, all greatly rejoiced, faithfully received it, and put it into practice.
