# 于瑟抳沙毘左野陀囉尼
## Uṣṇīṣavijaya Dhāraṇī

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0979

Translated from the Chinese. Goryeo dynasty, translated by Dhyānabhadra (指空)

---

*Oṃ bhruṃ svāhā namo bhagavate sarva-trailokya-prativiśiṣṭāya buddhāya te namas tadyathā oṃ bhruṃ bhruṃ śodhaya śodhaya viśodhaya viśodhaya asama-samanta-avabhāsa-asparaṇa gati-gagana-svabhāva-viśuddhe abhiṣiñcatu māṃ sarva-tathāgata-sugata-vara-vacana amṛta-abhiṣeka-mahā-mudrā-mantra-pade āhara āhara mama āyuḥ saṃdhāraṇi śodhaya śodhaya viśodhaya viśodhaya gagana-svabhāva-viśuddhe uṣṇīṣa-vijaya-pariśuddhe sahasra-raśmi-saṃcodite sarva-tathāgata-avalokini ṣaṭ-pāramitā-paripūraṇi sarva-tathāgata-mati-daśa-bhūmi-pratiṣṭhite sarva-tathāgata-hṛdaya-adhiṣṭhāna-adhiṣṭhite oṃ mudrī mudrī mahā-mudrī vajre vajre mahā-vajre vajra-garbhe jayā-garbhe vijayā-garbhe vajra-jvāla-garbhe vajra-ratna-garbhe vajrāṃ-kurya-bhavatu mama śarīraṃ sarva-sattvānāṃ ca kāya-pariśuddhi bhavatu mama sarvā-gati-pariśuddhi-śca sarva-tathāgata-siñcame samāśvāsayantu buddha buddhya bodhaya bodhaya vibodhaya vibodhaya mocaya mocaya vimocaya vimocaya śodhaya śodhaya viśodhaya viśodhaya samanta-rāśmi-pariśuddhe sarva-tathāgata-hṛdaya-adhiṣṭhāna-adhiṣṭhite oṃ mudrī mudrī mahā-mudrī mahā-mudrā-mantra-pade svāhā oṃ bhruṃ svāhā abhitā-āyuta-te svāhā iyam āryā-uṣṇīṣa-vijaya-nāma-dhāraṇī samāptā*
