# 佛說慈氏菩薩誓願陀羅尼經
## Sūtra of the Dhāraṇī of the Vows of Bodhisattva Maitreya, as Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1143

Translated from the Chinese. Song Dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢)

---

At that time, the Buddha addressed Bodhisattva Maitreya, saying: "Listen carefully. There is a dhāraṇī possessed of great spiritual power and supreme merit, capable of liberating sentient beings from the evil destinies, so that in their future rebirths they shall receive supreme and wondrous bliss."

Bodhisattva Maitreya said: "World-Honored One! Please proclaim it."

At that time the World-Honored One forthwith proclaimed the dhāraṇī:

*namo bhagavate śākyamunaye tathāgatāya arhate samyaksaṃbuddhāya | tathāgate agate jaye | vara vara maitrāvalokite | kara kara mahāsamayasiddhe | vara vara bodhimaṇḍa-vigate | smara smara asamagaṃ samāyaṃ | bodhi bodhi mahābodhi svāhā*

At that time, Bodhisattva Maitreya, having heard the World-Honored One the Buddha proclaim the dhāraṇī, said: "World-Honored One! This dhāraṇī is of great benefit, capable of liberating sentient beings from the evil destinies."

Then Bodhisattva Maitreya further made a vow, saying: "If there are sentient beings in the future age of the Declining Dharma who are able to recite and uphold this dhāraṇī—even those who on account of past karma have fallen into the Avīci Hell—when I attain Buddhahood, I shall by the power of the Buddha rescue and deliver them, and furthermore confer upon them the prediction of anuttarā-samyak-saṃbodhi."

Having spoken these words, Bodhisattva Maitreya prostrated at the Buddha's feet and withdrew rejoicing.
