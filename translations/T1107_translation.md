# 聖多羅菩薩梵讚
## Brahmā Hymn to the Holy Bodhisattva Tārā

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1107

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dānapāla (施護).

---

The entire text of this work consists of a Sanskrit devotional hymn (stotra) to the Bodhisattva Tārā, preserved in Chinese phonetic transliteration. The following is a reconstruction of the Sanskrit syllables based on the standard Chinese-Sanskrit transliteration conventions used in Song dynasty esoteric Buddhist texts.
