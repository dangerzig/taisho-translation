# 不空羂索呪心經
## Heart Sūtra of the Amoghapāśa Spell

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1095

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Bodhiruci (菩提流志).

---

Thus have I heard: At one time the Bhagavān was on Mount Potalaka, in the palace of Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva. That place had countless jeweled śāla trees, *tamāla* trees, *campaka* trees, *aśoka* trees, *atimukta* trees, and various other kinds of jeweled trees, all adorning the surroundings. He was together with a great assembly of eight thousand bhikṣus, ninety-nine koṭi-nayuta-hundred-thousand bodhisattva-mahāsattvas, and countless hundred-thousand Pure Abode celestial sons, Īśvara celestial sons, Maheśvara celestial sons, and Mahābrahmā celestial sons at their head, as well as countless hundred-thousand other devas and humans surrounding him on all sides, as the Buddha expounded the dharma. At that time Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, placed his right knee on the ground, joined his palms toward the Buddha, opened his face in a gentle smile, and addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I have a divine spell-heart called Amoghapāśa. In the past, during the ninety-first kalpa, there was a world called Victorious Contemplation. The buddha of that world was called World-Sovereign King Tathāgata. It was from that buddha that I received the Amoghapāśa spell-heart. World-Honored One! Because I received and upheld this divine spell, I taught and transformed countless hundred-thousand Pure Abode celestial sons, Īśvara celestial sons, and Maheśvara celestial sons, causing them to advance toward *anuttarā-samyak-saṃbodhi*. By the power of this merit, I obtained one billion samādhis, with the Amoghapāśa Wondrous Wisdom at their head. World-Honored One! Wherever this spell-heart exists, twelve billion devas headed by Maheśvara will come to provide protection, as if it were a buddha's caitya. World-Honored One! The sentient beings in any place where this divine spell-heart abides should know that they have already planted roots of merit before countless hundred-thousand koṭi-nayuta-hundred-thousand buddhas. World-Honored One! If furthermore a person hears this spell-heart, even one who has previously created evil karma, practiced wrongdoing, insulted the wise and good, slandered the correct dharma, and slandered all buddhas, bodhisattvas, śrāvakas, and pratyekabuddhas, and who should definitely fall into the great Avīci hell: World-Honored One! If that person, upon hearing this divine spell-heart, generates remorse and shame, and for one day and one night observes the precepts and recites this divine spell, they can eliminate all karmic offenses. Or perhaps their karmic offenses will be experienced lightly in this present life: they may contract a febrile illness lasting one to seven days, or pain in the eyes, ears, nose, tongue, lips, teeth, gums, palate, heart, abdomen, navel, ribs, hands, feet, limbs, or joints; or they may contract hemorrhoids, dysentery, constipation, leprosy, major leprosy, scabies, ringworm, black sores, red sores, fistulae, pustules, epilepsy, or seizures; or they may suffer from sorcery, parasitic poison, imprisonment, beating, slander, curses, or other forms of evil afflicting body and mind, including nightmare visions. I now explain that by experiencing these in the present life, the evil karma deserving of the Avīci hell will be eliminated. How much more so for sentient beings of pure faith who receive and uphold this spell: would their karmic offenses not be eliminated? World-Honored One! If any sentient being, even with a deceitful and dishonest mind, hears the Amoghapāśa spell-heart I have spoken, and whether pretending to uphold it, or copying it themselves or having others copy it, or explaining it to others so they may listen and receive it, or even reciting this spell-heart into the ears of animals, and contemplating the verses of the divine spell, because they do not slander it, because of signlessness, because of birthlessness, because of non-discrimination, because of being ultimately established, because of non-action, because of being free from defilement, because of equality, because of non-abandonment, because of being free from the aggregates: practicing concordant skillful means in this way, by this mindfulness of the Buddha's merit and power, a thousand buddhas from each of the ten directions will appear before the practitioner. Having seen them, all karmic offenses will be eliminated. World-Honored One! I now speak briefly: even if a person merely copies this sūtra and places it in their home, paying homage and making offerings, they will obtain immeasurable and boundless merit. How much more so for one who receives, upholds, reads, and recites it? World-Honored One! If any sentient being, whether seeking to surpass others, or fearing a ruler, fearing enemies, fearing evil beasts, fearing danger, following others, seeking honor, or seeking wealth, hears this divine spell-heart sūtra but, though hearing it, does not generate reverence, or even slanders, scorns, and disparages it: by the awesome spiritual power of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, even such a person will generate supreme merit. It is like a person who takes sandalwood, aloeswood, or musk and, while cursing and reviling them, grinds them to powder and applies them to the body. Those fragrances never have the thought: 'Because this person reviled me, I shall withhold my fragrance and not give it.' Rather, sandalwood and the others, by their inherent nature, are fragrant and perform the work of fragrance. World-Honored One! This divine spell-heart is likewise. Even though some may slander and disparage it, or even copy, uphold, and make offerings to it with deceitful intent, it will still create wholesome root-conditions for all of them. In every place of rebirth, they will never be separated from the provisions of precepts, samādhi, wisdom, and merit. In future lives, the fragrance of their precepts will be complete. World-Honored One! If good men, good women, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, or upāsikās, on the eighth day of the month, observe the precepts with focused mind, do not mingle other speech, and recite this Amoghapāśa spell-heart seven times, those persons will obtain twenty kinds of supreme benefit in their present body. What are the twenty? First, the body will be free from illness. Second, any illnesses from prior karma will be swiftly eliminated. Third, the body will be radiant, with skin smooth and fine, and all who see one will be delighted. Fourth, all people will love and respect one, and one's sense faculties will be guarded. Fifth, one will obtain wealth and treasures. Sixth, having obtained wealth, it will not be plundered by bandits. Seventh, it will not be destroyed by water or fire. Eighth, it will not be seized by the power of kings. Ninth, all undertakings will be accomplished well. Tenth, all crops and seedlings will not be damaged by evil wind, violent rain, frost, hail, or insect pests. Eleventh, if one recites this spell-heart seven times and empowers ash and water, scattering and sprinkling them in the eight directions, above and below, to establish a boundary, all calamities will be eliminated. Twelfth, evil ghosts and spirits will not steal one's vital energy. Thirteenth, all sentient beings will love, delight in, and be happy to see one. Fourteenth, one will not fear enemies. Fifteenth, if there are enemies, they will quickly be reconciled. Sixteenth, one will not fear invasion or harm from humans or non-humans; sorcery and evil spirits will be unable to cause injury. Seventeenth, one will be free from fierce afflictions and secondary afflictions. Eighteenth, fire, weapons, and poison will not cause death. Nineteenth, devas and good spirits will always provide protection. Twentieth, wherever one is reborn, one will never be separated from the four immeasurables of loving-kindness, compassion, sympathetic joy, and equanimity. These are the twenty kinds of supreme benefit that one should seek. There are also eight further benefits. What are the eight? First, at the time of death, Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva will appear in the form of a bhikṣu before that person. Second, at the time of death, one will relinquish life in comfort, free from pain and suffering. Third, at the time of death, right mindfulness will be present and the mind will not be confused. Fourth, at the time of death, the hands will not flail about and the feet will not stretch or contract. Fifth, at the time of death, there will be no incontinence of urine or feces. Sixth, even if ill, one will not be bedridden. Seventh, at the time of death, one will not die face-down. Eighth, at the time of death, one will attain inexhaustible eloquence. After death, one will be reborn in the pure lands of the buddhas according to one's vow, and will never be separated from good spiritual friends. World-Honored One, if good men, good women, and others abstain from alcohol, meat, pungent herbs, and all polluting substances, and three times daily, three times at each session, recite this divine spell-heart dharma-gate, supreme merit will increase day and night. One will know whether sentient beings are strong or weak, and according to their capacity, cause them to hear it. A bodhisattva should not withhold it in their mind, but should forever abandon all evil stinginess and jealousy, and constantly for the benefit of all sentient beings, swiftly advance toward enlightenment and enter the bodhisattva stage. The word 'bodhi' means prajñā. The word 'sattva' means skillful means. These two dharmas will bring all sentient beings benefit and ease. World-Honored One! I now wish to benefit, give ease to, and delight the four assemblies and all other sentient beings who have created karmic offenses. May the World-Honored One compassionately grant permission. Before the Tathāgata, I shall speak this spell-heart."

At that time the World-Honored One told Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva: "The purity of sentient beings makes this the right time. I too rejoice in this divine spell-heart. In the future, it shall serve as father and mother for those who practice the bodhisattva vehicle, causing all bodhisattvas' undertakings to be swiftly accomplished." At that time Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva gazed upon the World-Honored One's countenance without looking away for even a moment, and addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! May the Tathāgata permit me to speak this divine spell, to which all bodhisattvas should pay homage. This liberation dharma-gate has compassion for the world and brings benefit and ease to countless sentient beings. Those who wish to receive and uphold it should first pay homage to the buddhas of the three ages and to all bodhisattvas, pratyekabuddhas, and śrāvakas; pay homage to those of correct attainment and correct practice; pay homage to the great wisdom-śrāvakas headed by Śāriputra; pay homage to the bodhisattva-mahāsattva assembly headed by Maitreya; homage to Golden-Light Roaring Sovereign King Tathāgata; homage to Lion Sport King Tathāgata; homage to Amitābha Tathāgata; homage to Well-Abiding Maṇi Jewel-Heap King Tathāgata; homage to Universal Radiance Praising Merit-Heap King Tathāgata; homage to Victorious Contemplation Tathāgata; homage to Jewel-Topknot Tathāgata; homage to World-Manifesting Tathāgata; homage to Abandoning Destruction of the Aggregates Tathāgata; homage to Golden-Body Tranquil Tathāgata; homage to Kāśyapa Tathāgata; homage to Able-to-Still Tathāgata; homage to Well-Named Tathāgata; homage to Universal Radiance Vanquishing-Enemy Merit Tathāgata; homage to Lord-Banner Merit Tathāgata; homage to Jewel-Radiance Sovereign King Tathāgata; homage to Unobstructed Medicine King Tathāgata; homage to Bravely Striding Tathāgata; homage to Well-Abiding Fearless Tathāgata, Arhat, Samyak-saṃbuddha; homage to the Three Treasures; homage to Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, the Great Compassionate One. Having paid homage to these holy ones, one should further recite: 'Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, before the Tathāgata, spoke the divine spell-heart. I now shall also speak this divine spell. May the activities I undertake be swiftly accomplished. May all my fears be eliminated.'" At that time Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva spoke the spell:

"This divine spell-heart: whenever it is recited, efficacy follows, and all activities are accomplished. Three times daily, three times each session, recite it three times. The five heinous crimes will be eliminated and all karmic obstructions will be purified. Burn aloeswood incense, or scatter ash, scatter water, or use white mustard seeds to establish a boundary. Or take *khadira* wood stakes, empower them with twenty-one recitations, and drive them into the four directions. If one suffers from all kinds of chills, febrile illness, or malarial fever, empower a cord with the spell and wear it; the illness will be cured. For anyone who is ill, empower ghee, oil, or water, and have the sick person either drink or apply it; they will immediately recover. If one has been subjected to sorcery, use flour, clay, or wax to form a human figure and cut it with a knife. Further, with a spell-cord, have the afflicted person always wear it on their body. If one suffers from abdominal pain, empower salt water and have them drink it. If one encounters any poison, empower earth or water; whether applied or consumed, it will be eliminated. If one suffers from eye disease, empower white thread and make a cord to tie around the ear. If one suffers from toothache, empower *kāla-vīra* wood and have them chew it. If one wishes to establish a boundary, use *khadira* wood as stakes driven into the four corners, and empower five-colored cord twenty-one times, wrapping it around the stakes. If one wishes to protect oneself or others, empower a cord to wear, or empower water or ash and sprinkle it upon the body. If one suffers from all kinds of ghost illness, empower five-colored cord and make a cord to wear. If one suffers from all kinds of febrile illness, empower white cord and make a cord to wear. If one suffers from any evil sore, swelling, or throat obstruction, mix honey with long pepper, empower it, and take it. If one suffers from eye disease, empower fragrant water, or *parūṣa* water, or licorice water, and use it to wash the eyes. If one suffers from ear pain, empower sesame oil and drip it into the ear. If involved in battles, disputes, or slander, take empowered water and use it to wash the face. If one wishes to protect a royal capital or settlement, take four vases filled with water, set out food, and make great offerings. The spell-reciter should don new clean garments and read this spell; auspiciousness will follow. Further, sprinkle that water throughout the place; it will protect all sentient beings, and all calamities will be eliminated. If one suffers from illness caused by spirits, grind sandalwood with water, empower it twenty-one times, and apply it to the heart. If one has committed the four grave offenses, five heinous crimes, or crimes deserving of the Avīci hell, one should constantly recite this spell, and those sins will be eliminated. If one wishes to protect a house, take one hundred and eight lotuses, empower each one and burn it in the fire. If one wishes all sentient beings to comply with one, take one hundred and eight pieces of sandalwood two inches long, empower each one, and burn them in the fire. If one is afflicted by ghosts or spirits or has fears, take the following medicines: *jayā*, *vijayā*, *nākulī*, *gandha-nākulī*, *bālaṇī*, *abhaya-paṇī*, *indra-paṇī*, *gandha-priyaṅgu*, *tagara*, *cakra*, *mahā-cakra*, *viṣṇu-krānta*, *sumarasi*, and *sunanda*. Pound and sieve all these medicines, mix with water to form pills, and empower them one hundred and eight times. Whether placed on the head, tied to both arms, or on a child's neck, ghost afflictions and fears will be eliminated. If a woman, due to meager merit, is scorned by others and wishes for a son, she should don new clean garments, empower the medicine-water with twenty-one recitations, and bathe herself. She will gain superior merit, evil signs will be eliminated, and wishing for a son, she will obtain a son. All benefits will be obtained: poison and fire will not approach, and calamities will not attach. If one encounters evil wind, violent rain, or destructive hail, empower water with twenty-one recitations and sprinkle it in the four directions. If one empowers a *kāla-vīra* wood staff with twenty-one recitations and points it at the sky, wind and other disturbances will cease. The great divine spell-heart of Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva accomplishes such supreme activities. If accomplishment has not yet been attained, one should paint a buddha image on white cotton cloth. The pigments should be mixed with fragrant glue and no other kind of glue. Beside the buddha image, paint an image of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, yellowish-white in body color, with dark blue hair hanging down, wearing a flower-crown upon the head, draped in an antelope skin in the manner of Maheśvara, with rings and bracelets all adorned with precious jewels. Before the painter begins, they should first receive the eight-precept fast. When the image is complete, before the image use cow dung to make a maṇḍala, sixteen feet square, scattered with white flowers. At the eight directions of the altar, place eight vases of fragrant water. Set out eight or sixty-four portions of food. Make offerings in this way, excluding all pungent herbs. Burn aloeswood incense. For three days and three nights do not eat, or for one day and one night do not eat. When eating, take only the three kinds of daily food. Three times daily, bathe and don new clean garments. Recite the spell one thousand and eight times. When the recitations are complete, the practitioner will see before the image that their own body is blazing with great radiance like fierce flames. Having seen this, the mind will be filled with joy. Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva will then appear before them, and whatever wishes one has will all be fulfilled. If one wishes to become invisible, take orpiment or añjana medicine and empower it one thousand and eight times. One will then become invisible and travel through the sky, attaining the foremost adornment of the Amoghajñāna Supreme Samādhi. All one's aspirations will be accomplished." Having spoken thus, the Bhagavān rejoiced and praised him. At that time Holy Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, the Pure Abode celestial sons, the lord of the Sahā world, the Īśvara and Maheśvara celestial kings, and all the bodhisattvas and great śrāvakas, having received what the Buddha spoke, joyfully put it into practice.
