# 佛說甘露經陀羅尼呪
## The Dhāraṇī Spell of the Amṛta Sūtra, Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1316

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Śikṣānanda (實叉難陀)

---

*[Dhāraṇī]* Namas surūpāya tathāgatāya | tadyathā oṃ suru suru varasuru varasuru svāhā

Take a handful of water in the right hand, recite the spell seven times, and scatter it into the air. Each drop of that water transforms into ten *hu* of amṛta. All hungry ghosts will be able to drink of it; none shall lack or go without, and all will be fully satiated.
