# 番大悲神呪
## The Great Compassion Divine Spell in Barbarian Script

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1063

Translated from the Chinese. Translator unknown.

---

*Namo ratna-trayāya namaḥ ārya-avalokiteśvarāya bodhisattvāya mahāsattvāya mahā-kāruṇikāya tadyathā oṃ sarva-bandhana-ccheda-na-karāya sarva-bhava-samudra-śoṣaṇa-karāya sarva-vyādhi-praśamana-karāya sarveti-tyupa-drāva-vināśana-karāya sarva-bhayeṣu-trāṇa-karāya tasmai namas-kṛtva idaṃ ārya-avalokiteśvara-bhāṣitaṃ nīlakaṇṭha-nāma hṛdayaṃ āvartayiṣyāmi sarvārtha-sādhanaṃ śubhaṃ ajeyaṃ sarva-sattvānāṃ bhava-mārga-viśodhakaṃ tadyathā oṃ āloke āloka-mati lokāti-krānte ehi hare mahā-bodhisattva he bodhisattva he mahā-bodhisattva he priya-bodhisattva he kāruṇika smara hṛdayam ehi hare ārya-avalokiteśvara parama-maitra-citta mahā-kāruṇika kuru kuru karma sādhaya sādhaya vidyāṃ nidhi nidhi me āna-kaṃ-gama-vihaṃgama siddha-yogeśvara dhuru dhuru viyantiṃ mahā-viyantiṃ dhara dhara dharīṇi-śvara cala cala vimala āmala mṛta ārya-avalokiteśvara gṛṣṇa-jaṭā-makuṭa-ālaṃba-kṛta-śrī-rālaṃba-pra-rālaṃba vi-rālaṃba mahā-siddha-vidyā-dhara bāla bāla mahā-bāla māla māla mahā-māla cala cala mahā-cala kṛṣṇa-varṇa nigra-kṛṣṇa-pakṣa nigha-tana he padma-mahā-siddha-cala-cala niśa-carī-śvara kṛṣṇa-sarpa-kṛtāya jivu-pavita ehi he vārāha-mukha-tri-pura-dahana heśvara nārāyaṇa-rūpa-viśeṣṭa-hrī nīlakaṇṭha he mahā-kāla hāla hāla viṣa-nirjita-loka-śyā-rāga-viṣa-vināśana dveṣa-viṣa-vināśana moha-viṣa-vināśana huru huru māra huru hare mahā-padma-nābha-sara-sara siri siri suru suru budhya budhya bodhaya bodhaya maitrī nīlakaṇṭha ehi he mānas-thita siṃha-mukha hasa hasa muñca muñca mahā-ṭaṭṭā-hāsa-nai he bhau mahā-siddha-yogeśvara bhaṇa bhaṇa vāci sādhaya sādhaya vidyāṃ smara smara hiva-gavaṃ-luku-śvaṃ-tāṃ tathāgata dadā-he me darśana kāma-darśana prahlādayamānasa svāhā siddhāya svāhā mahā-siddhāya svāhā siddha-yogeśvarāya svāhā nīlakaṇṭhāya svāhā varāha-mukhāya svāhā siṃha-mukhāya svāhā mahā-nara-siṃha-mukhāya svāhā siddha-vidyā-dharāya svāhā padma-hastāya svāhā mahā-padma-hastāya svāhā vajra-hastāya svāhā mahā-vajra-hastāya svāhā kṛṣṇa-sarpa-kṛtāya jivu-pavitāya svāhā mahā-lakuṭa-dharāya svāhā cakra-yuddha-dharāya svāhā śaṅkha-śabda-nirghātana-karāya svāhā bodhana-karāya svāhā māmas-kaṃda-tiṣe-sthita-kṛṣṇa-ajina svāhā māmas-hasta-vajra-carma-ni-vāsanāya svāhā lokiteśvarāya svāhā mahā-lokiteśvarāya svāhā sarva-siddhi-śvarāya svāhā rākṣa rākṣa maṃ svāhā kuru kuru rākṣa mṛtināṃ svāhā namo bhagavate ārya-avalokiteśvarāya bodhisattvāya mahāsattvāya mahā-kāruṇikāya sidhyantu me mantra-padāya svāhā*
