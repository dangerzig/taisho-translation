# 七曜星辰別行法
## Separate Methods of the Seven Luminaries and Star Deities

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1309

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, composed by Yixing (一行).

---

At the beginning of the Kaiyuan era of the Emperor Xuanzong, on the night of deploying troops to the east, beside the encampment at an outlying palace, Xuanzong had the monk Yixing accompanying him. Yixing suddenly performed a rite, summoning down all the ghosts governed by the various star deities. They all gathered and, being recorded and controlled by the imperial command, were all properly interrogated. The deities of the various stars and the twenty-eight lunar mansions communicated fully regarding the disease-bringing ghosts under their governance: altogether thirty ghosts, who roam the human world day and night without ceasing, feeding on those in decline to their fill and satisfaction, but unable to obtain food and drink from the households of those whose fortune is at its peak. At that time this was recorded and presented to Xuanzong: "Only Your Majesty alone shall receive this method." Later, when Xuanzong fled to Shu, someone obtained a copy from the household of Gao Lishi, and to this day it has been passed down throughout the realm, though valued beyond ten thousand pieces of gold and rarely transmitted. If one obtains it, one must follow the method clearly and precisely in performing the rituals, and not a single case will fail. There is no method of transmitting healing that surpasses this. Appended: The Method by Which the Ghost King Upavāsa Imprisons and Restrains the Disease-Bringing Ghosts Who Follow the Twenty-Eight Mansions.

To know the illnesses among people, one must understand that they are all caused by the disease-bringing ghosts governed by the twenty-eight lunar mansions. This method communicates with supernatural powers throughout and is called the Separate Methods of the Seven Luminaries from the Western Lands. The seven luminaries govern the twenty-eight mansions, and the twenty-eight mansions in turn govern the various disease-bringing ghost-kings. One must first learn the days of the seven luminaries. The invocation says: "Today is the day of Mitra [the Sun], etc. Among the twenty-eight mansions, today is the duty-day of the Horn Mansion, etc. With utmost urgency, on behalf of so-and-so, seize and imprison this ghost. The patient must recover by this very day." If the timing is missed, one should use iron nails and nail the ghost's hands and feet above and below. To determine the days of the twenty-eight mansions, examine which month among the preceding twelve it is, and from the day at the base, starting before and after the fifteenth, count in the order of the mansions. This arrangement of mansions is fixed and without error. Counting before the fifteenth: the fourteenth, the thirteenth. Counting after the sixteenth: the seventeenth, the eighteenth, the nineteenth. Among these are thirty ghosts. Two mansions, the Literary Star and the Clock Star, are not listed among the twenty-eight mansions. To establish the names, simply arrange them according to the day, and the twenty-eight mansions are complete. These two stars, the Literary and Clock Mansions, wander without a fixed abode, so they are not given fixed positions in the list. The discerning reader will search and find their ghosts. When painting [the ghost forms], each one must be done according to the method; the drawings and coloring must all be proper. The wine, dried meat, and spirit-money must all follow the order listed below for each of the twenty-eight mansions. The worthy should pass this on with the utmost secrecy, for it is not transmitted even for ten thousand pieces of gold. This is truly the Way of transmitting healing; one must treasure it greatly, store it in one's box and chest, and never let a petty person obtain it. The Way of Heaven does not permit its disclosure. This Way is supremely effective; in the world, nothing surpasses this method. The Ghost King Upavāsa leads the hosts of ghosts, constantly roaming the world and causing trouble, so that sentient beings often suffer from illness. The human lifespan is one hundred years, and within that span these twelve share a single cycle. Those of strong fortune will live their full hundred years, but those of weak destiny will all be tripped and killed by these spirits, in numbers beyond reckoning, which is truly lamentable. At that time, the Great Brahmā Heavenly King, together with the deities of the various constellations, caused all sentient beings to know good from evil. "I shall now summon them, and the ghost king shall respond accordingly." For the one among them who is ill, give their name, and the ghost king will confront the thirty disease-bringing ghosts, who roam without fixed dwelling, always on the roads and lanes of the human world. Ordinary people are unable to deal with them, for they are all governed by the twenty-eight mansions. One need only remember the day the illness began and the time of the ritual offering. When the time comes to speak, one must write out the form of the ghost on duty that day, write its name, and command the mansion's duty-king to seize and imprison it. The patient will recover that very day. By following this method in practice, there will be no case of failure. But for every illness, one must record the day it was contracted and follow this method to restrain the ghost through writing. For one hundred years thereafter, it will never dare approach the household again.

**Pleiades Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Black Forest Nun. On this day, this ghost brings illness: it suddenly strikes a person down on the roadside, causing them to lose their voice and be unable to speak. Paint the form of this ghost, write its name, and in the patient's home light one lamp. Set out one cup of clear wine and one stack of white dried meat as a sacrificial offering to the Pleiades Mansion. The patient will recover their speech that very day; there is no need to seek other deities. On the day of the ritual, say the patient's name before the Pleiades Mansion and bow twice. Do not add any other words. The mansion will know of itself and will certainly seize and imprison its ghost.

**Net Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Knowing Cai. This ghost brings illness, causing the person's chest to feel hot and stuffy as if on fire, with confusion and inability to turn, forgetting what came before and losing what follows. This is the work of this ghost. Burn forty-nine strings of spirit-money, and with clear wine and white dried meat, make the offering at exactly noon facing south. Be sure to say the patient's name. Pour wine seven times. Bow only to the Net Mansion; do not add other words. The patient will recover that day. If suddenly a person in yellow garments appears, they must not be allowed to enter the gate; this must be strictly prevented. As before, paint the ghost's form and nail it for good fortune.

**Three-Stars Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Crouching Response of the Three Stars. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to suddenly become nauseated, vomiting without ceasing, or alternating between chills and fever. This is the work of this ghost. With clear wine and white dried meat, make the offering to the Three-Stars Mansion according to the method. Say the patient's name. Bow only to the Three-Stars Mansion. Pour wine nine rounds and stop. Write the ghost's form and restrain it. The patient will recover, and thereafter the ghost will never again dare approach the person's gate. On the day of this ritual, if a monk or nun comes, do not call them inside the gate, or the patient will die of drunkenness within twenty-one days. This taboo must be strictly observed and kept firmly in mind; do not forget even for a moment.

**Willow Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Aśoka. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to suddenly speak incoherently like a person who has lost their mind. This is the work of this ghost. If believed, the patient will die within one year. This ghost belongs to this mansion. Record the day the illness began. With clear wine and white dried meat, make the offering to the Willow Mansion according to the method; there will be a response that very day. At the time of the ritual, one must not see the face of wife or children; wait one day after the ritual before seeing them again. The ghost will never again dare approach the gate for the rest of its existence, and the patient's fortune and lifespan will be extended to a hundred years without premature death, and they will be free from plague for their entire life.

**Star Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Ten-Thousand Pine Stone. On this day, this ghost brings illness, making the person unable to walk, or causing irregular chills and fever. This is the work of this ghost, belonging to this mansion. With one hundred strings of spirit-money and clear wine, make the offering on the appointed day. Pour wine seven full rounds and stop. Do not allow a person in white garments to come. Even if they arrive, do not ask them to sit, and do not let this person know about the ritual method. The one who comes is a ghost seeking to take the patient's place in death. This must be clearly understood.

**Extended-Net Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Cloud Lord. On this day, this ghost brings illness. It especially loves to afflict young boys and girls in people's homes, causing alternating chills and fever, or dysentery with water that will not stop. This is the work of this ghost. On the day the illness began, with forty strings of spirit-money, clear wine, and white dried meat, set up the offering at noon. Light one lamp within the home. The invocation should name the boy or girl. Simply say it, and the child will recover that day. If an adult falls ill on this day, no offering is needed; they will recover on their own within three days.

**Wing Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Traveling Sound Wind. On this day, this ghost brings illness. It especially loves to enter people's homes and afflict young boys and girls, causing them to suddenly collapse while eating, lose their voice, and be unable to speak. This is the work of this ghost. With clear wine and white dried meat, make the offering. The patient will recover that day. If one does not perform this ritual and does not know the ghost's name, the patient will die within three days. If one understands this ritual method, calls the ghost by name, and commands the mansion's deity to seize and imprison it, the afflicted boy or girl will recover that day. If an adult encounters illness on this day, there is nothing to fear; no ritual is needed.

**Chariot Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Same-Day Flow. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to suddenly vomit bile, with constant, unceasing stomach and intestinal pain. This is the work of this ghost, belonging to the Chariot Mansion. With seven strings of spirit-money, clear wine, and white dried meat, make the offering at noon. Pour wine seven rounds and stop. Write the ghost's form and restrain it. On the day of the ritual, if a woman suddenly appears, do not call her inside. If one does not make this offering to the mansion, the patient will die within seven days. If one follows this method, lifespan will extend to a hundred years. One must deeply believe this.

**Winnowing-Basket Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is A-zhī-pó. On this day, this ghost brings illness: young boys and girls in people's homes suddenly develop fever and cry incessantly through the night. If one does not know about this ghost and seek to make the offering, the afflicted child will die within fourteen days. With one hundred strings of spirit-money, at the hour of the monkey [3-5 PM], in the home within the main room, light two lamps and with clear wine and white dried meat make the offering to the Winnowing-Basket Mansion. The child will recover that very day. If an adult encounters illness on this day, they will recover within three days on their own.

**Maiden Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Worry-Night-Much. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to run out on the road, talking to themselves, unable to distinguish good from bad. This is the work of this ghost. The patient will die within one year. This belongs to the Maiden Mansion. With two hundred and ten strings of spirit-money, clear wine, and white dried meat, make the offering. Say: "Maiden Mansion, seize and imprison it." The patient will recover that day. On the day of the ritual, if a visitor comes, absolutely do not allow them inside. If they come in unnoticed, though the ritual is performed, it will ultimately be without effect. Avoid this for good fortune.

**Dipper Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Many-Dwelling Night. On this day, this ghost brings illness, suddenly causing the hands and feet to become numb and the person unable to walk. All this is the work of this ghost. If one does not know about this method, recovery will come on its own only after three years. With three hundred strings of spirit-money, good wine, and white dried meat, at the hour of the dragon [7-9 AM], in the patient's room within the home, light seven lamps. Pour wine seven rounds and stop. Say the patient's name, bow seven times, and the patient will be moving within seven days. On that day, if a monk or nun comes to the gate and urgently requests entry, there will certainly be a delay [in recovery].

**Beak Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Eternal Limitless. On this day, this ghost brings illness: it causes the people of a household, for no reason, to have husband and wife quarrel over trivial matters and break up the family. All this is the work of this ghost. Offer forty-nine strings of spirit-money with wine and dried meat. Simply bow. Pour wine thirteen rounds and stop. If one does not encounter this ritual method, someone in that household will certainly die within the home. On the day of the ritual, if a lone woman comes, do not let her enter the gate. Even if she is a relative, do not let her in. Avoid this.

**Horn Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Night-Dwelling Mountain. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to suddenly suffer from unceasing chills and be unable to eat. This is the work of this ghost, governed by the Horn Mansion. With clear wine and white dried meat, on the patient's day of illness, offer seven strings of spirit-money. Make the offering at exactly noon. The patient will recover that day. On the day of this ritual, if a person in yellow garments comes, you must urgently invite them inside. They will certainly be beneficial for the patient. Good fortune.

**Well Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Hearing-Crossing Direct. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to suddenly collapse on the ground, appearing dead then reviving. This is the work of this ghost. If one does not encounter this method, the patient will die within seven days. Record the day of illness. With forty-nine strings of spirit-money and fine brewed tea, make the offering. Bow to this mansion seven times only; do not add other words. At the time of the ritual, if a woman comes to the house, absolutely do not invite her inside. If a young boy comes, urgently invite him in to sit and serve him fine brewed tea. He must be made to drink at least one sip, and then the patient will also recover. Remember this clearly.

**Ghost Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Peaceful Little Cry. On this day, this ghost brings illness, appearing in a person's dream as a woman, pressing upon the person's spirit and appearing frequently. This is the ghost's true form. After a long time, if not noticed, the ghost grows daily, and it becomes a wasting disease that spreads corruption and infection, taking all the people of one household one after another. Within three years, the patient will die. One must urgently use this method to make the offering. Record the time of the dream and determine that it was the Ghost Mansion's duty-day. With Buddhist incense, one hundred strings of spirit-money, and fine brewed tea, make the offering. The ghost will never again dare come to cause trouble. The ghost must also be nailed above and below; this is permanently effective.

**Base Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Hard Talisman. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to suddenly have morning chills and evening fever. All this is the work of this ghost. It belongs to the Base Mansion. With forty-nine strings of spirit-money, good wine, and white dried meat, make the offering. Two portions of dried meat are needed. Pour wine nine rounds. Bow to the mansion with utmost sincerity; do not add other words. On the day of the ritual, if a young child around ten years old comes to the gate, do not let them inside. This must be strictly prevented. This is the most important thing to remember; prepare in advance for good fortune.

**Room Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Hundred-Year Elder. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person while walking to feel as though someone is following behind them, with hair standing on end from cold. This is the work of this ghost. If one does not know about this ritual method, within one year the person will develop a condition of losing their vital spirit, leading to madness and death on the road. With two hundred strings of spirit-money and good wine and dried meat, make the offering. At the hour of the monkey [3-5 PM], light lamps. Offer five cups of wine and stop; do not add other words. On the day of the ritual, if someone enters the home alone, the patient will recover and live to a hundred without dying.

**Heart Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Eternal Night Builder. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to suddenly alternate between chills and fever, worsening day by day, with the heart area disturbed so that they cannot eat. This is the work of this ghost. With one hundred and twenty strings of spirit-money, clear wine, and white dried meat, make the offering. The illness will certainly improve that very day, and lifespan will be increased. If one does not encounter this method, within three years the patient will die in a foreign land.

**Tail Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Hundred Black Mountain. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing genital swelling; whether male or female, the entire body cannot move or turn. This is the work of this ghost, belonging to the Tail Mansion. With forty-nine strings of yellow spirit-money, good wine, and dried meat, make the offering. The patient will recover that day; there is no need to seek other deities. Three cups of wine, poured nine rounds, and stop. On the day of the ritual, if an old man comes to the gate, you must urgently invite him into the home and treat him with courtesy and offerings. Do not be disrespectful.

**Dipper Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Wood Forest Sound. On this day, this ghost brings illness, making the person afraid to walk alone at night; when walking, every step feels like running, and they cannot control it. If one does not encounter the ritual method, within one year or one hundred days the person will suddenly die. With one hundred strings of spirit-money, good wine, and white dried meat, make the offering. The patient will recover that day. After the ritual, that very night one may walk alone without fear, and forever be free from dread. On the day of the ritual, one should not allow women to come. Prevent this; do not take it lightly. This must be maintained.

**Neck Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Garden-Fork Rooster. On this day, this ghost brings illness, making the person unable to open their eyes, with nausea and irregular chills and fever so that they cannot get up or lie down. This is the work of this ghost. It belongs to the Neck Mansion. With forty-nine strings of spirit-money, at noon, with good clear wine and white dried meat, make the offering. If one does not encounter this method, the patient will die within forty-nine days. During the ritual, no outsiders or young children may enter the home. This must be strictly avoided. Even relatives must not be allowed inside. Strictly avoid this.

**Danger Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Hill-Walking First. On this day, this ghost brings illness, causing the person to lose their wits, becoming like a fool. If within one year one does not encounter the ritual method to restrain and command the ghost, the patient will certainly die. This ghost belongs to the Danger Mansion. With one hundred and twenty strings of yellow spirit-money and good wine and dried meat, make the offering. That day the person's spirit will be restored to normal. The ghost will never again dare approach the gate. The ghost must also be nailed on the body above and below.

**Encampment Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Word-Breaking Love. It causes the person to suffer from watery dysentery that will not stop. If one does not encounter the ritual method, it will become malignant dysentery and will certainly lead to death. With one hundred strings of spirit-money, good wine, and white dried meat, make the offering. The patient will recover that day. On the day of the ritual, if a lone ghost enters the home, do not harm it. Simply find a way to let it out. It is sufficient not to let it enter. Avoid this.

**Wall Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Wave-of-Field. It causes the person to collapse while at the latrine, and they become unable to speak. With forty-nine strings of spirit-money, good wine, and white dried meat, make the offering according to the method. One must write the form of this ghost; then the person will not die but will gradually recover within seven days, and their lifespan will be extended by an additional hundred years. This must be reverently observed.

**Bond Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Commanding Nun Dwelling. It causes pain deep in the bones of both legs, so that the person can neither sit nor lie down. This is the work of this ghost. If one does not encounter the ritual method, the legs will be lost. With two hundred strings of five-colored spirit-money, good wine, and white dried meat, make the offering according to the method. The patient will recover within seven days and be able to walk again. On the day of the ritual, absolutely do not let monks or nuns enter the home. This must be strictly prevented. After the ritual, from the next day onward, they may enter.

**Stride Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Vast Dwelling Builder. It causes the person to be unable to urinate, with pain in the private parts, unable to move. If within three days one does not encounter the ritual method, the condition will certainly become critical. With one hundred and twenty strings of yellow spirit-money and good wine and dried meat, make the offering at the hour of the ram [1-3 PM]. If after three days there is no improvement, it will lead to death. This ghost traverses the four continents in a single day. Upon awareness, one must urgently perform the ritual to command its seizure and imprisonment, and recovery will certainly follow.

**Southern Dipper Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Lone Finger Tower. It causes obstruction of urination and bowel movements, with alternating chills and fever and inability to move. If one does not encounter the ritual method, death will certainly occur within three to nine days. With forty-nine strings of spirit-money, good wine, and dried meat, make the offering. The patient will be restored to normal that very day. If one waits until the third day to perform the ritual, the patient will be difficult to save.

**Void Mansion duty-day.** The ghost's name is Eternal Night Guide. It causes pain throughout the entire body so that the person cannot move or turn. This is the work of this ghost. If one calls upon another healer, the patient will recover on their own within three days. With fourteen strings of spirit-money and wine and dried meat, make the offering according to the method. The patient will be restored to normal immediately.

**Clock Star duty-day.** The ghost's name is Hundred Breaker. It causes the person to have unceasing alternating chills and fever, with cold in the heart area, constant screaming and crying without stopping. If one does not encounter the ritual method, death will certainly occur within five days. With one hundred and twenty strings of spirit-money and wine and dried meat, make the offering as in the previous methods. If one waits until the seventh day to perform the ritual, it will be ineffective, and the patient will certainly die and cannot be saved.

**Literary Star duty-day.** The ghost's name is Form-Offering Fork. It causes pain in the lower back as if pierced by an arrow, unbearably intense, so that the person cannot get up or sit down. This is the work of this ghost. As soon as one becomes aware, one must urgently make the offering with wine and dried meat according to the method. The patient will recover that day. If the ritual is performed after the third day, the illness will have already entered the four limbs and will be difficult to treat.

An imperial decree to all star-mansion ghosts and spirits: do not roam the human world without cause. If they violate this, one must use the ritual method to address it. With the utmost urgency, as commanded by the statutes. For the method of making offerings to the star mansions, use dried meat, spirit-money, and all other items, one by one following the methods listed above, without any deficiency. For the patient to recover, simply state the patient's name, the day and time the illness was contracted, and do not say any words wishing for household peace and safety. Focus only on bowing before the form of the star mansion. State the patient's name, and one must write out the forms and appearances of all the disease-bringing ghosts listed above, so that people may see the ghost forms with their own eyes. Nail the ghost forms above and below with the hand. The next day, command their seizure and imprisonment. The patient will recover that day. Then remove the nails. Once the ghost is released, it will never again dare approach the gate. Whenever it goes about bringing illness, having once been known by a patient and nailed and imprisoned, it will pass by that household from a distance of five hundred paces and not even dare to look directly at the gate of that mansion's home. At the time of the invocation, one must clearly say each time: "All star mansions: the patient so-and-so requests that you seize and imprison it. Do not allow the illness to become incurable." The patient, as well as all ordinary people, must not sleep exposed under the stars. If one encounters this illness and does not know about it, they will die in madness. Caution! Caution! Caution!
