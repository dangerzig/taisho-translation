# 佛說文殊師利一百八名梵讚
## Sanskrit Praise of the One Hundred and Eight Names of Mañjuśrī Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1197

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dharmadeva (法天).

---

I shall now proclaim the surpassing merit of the one hundred and eight names of Mañjuśrī. If one receives, upholds, reads, and recites them three times daily, whatever one wishes and aspires to will certainly manifest. If one practices and recites them according to the dharma, one's body will be constantly purified and karmic hindrances extinguished. If one enters a battle array or is amid various terrors, Mañjuśrī will appear in person to provide protection. If one constantly recites them, one will swiftly realize bodhi.

Sanskrit Praise, the First:

*Praṇipatya munī-mūrdhanā su-prasannena citta svākṣa-mayā aniyā namanī saṃbhu-tairātu-varaṇi-tāṃ*

Sanskrit Praise, the Second:

*Surūpa-rūpa-dhāri-ca sarva-rūpa-hayaḍ duntara-sarva-lakṣaṇa-saṃpūraṇa mañjuśrī duttama-śrīya*

Sanskrit Praise, the Third:

*Acintya-cintya-vigata acintyū-bhūta-vikrama acintya-sarva-dharmaṇa acintyū-mānasa-satthā*

Sanskrit Praise, the Fourth:

*Śūnyatā-bhāvita-ātmānāṃ śūnya-dharma-samādhita śūnya-mādhī-muktiś-ca śūnya-tri-bhava-deśaka*

Sanskrit Praise, the Fifth:

*Sarvajña-sarva-darśī-ca sarva-bhūmi-patir-vibhu mañjuśrī-vaśa-vartī-ca padmākṣa-padma-saṃbhava*

Sanskrit Praise, the Sixth:

*Padma-kiñjalka-kā-varaṇaś-ca padma-paryaṅka-māsana nīlotpala-dhara-pūta pavitra-śata-māsana*

Sanskrit Praise, the Seventh:

*Pratyeka-buddha-buddha-satvaṃ anibuddha-nirūcaya-te ṛddhi-māśīta-prāpta-praśāntu satya-upanaśaka*

Sanskrit Praise, the Eighth:

*Loka-pāla-sahasra-kṣa-Iśvara-satvaṃ prajāpati-śiva-satvaṃ sarva-bhūtānāṃ-satvaṃ vidu-guṇa-sāgara*

Sanskrit Praise, the Ninth:

*Ṛṣi-satvaṃ puṇya-śrī-ṣṭhaca-jeṣṭha-jāti-smara-satthā vināya-ku-vinīta-ca jñāna-putra-jñāna-ātmaja*

Sanskrit Praise, the Tenth:

*Bhatu-sahasra-raśmi-satvaṃ sumā-satvaṃ-ca bṛhaspati-dhāna-nuvaruṇa-śraiva-satvaṃ viṣṇu-satvaṃ maheśvara*

Sanskrit Praise, the Eleventh:

*Ananta-nāga-rāja-satvaṃ skandha-senā-pati-sa-tathā vaimacitra-sundara-satvaṃ bhā-maśakra-tanā-satthā*

Sanskrit Praise, the Twelfth:

*Sarva-deva-māyu-vīra-sarva-devai-nāma-skṛta loka-dharma-mārāti-tu-satvaṃ lokeca-gara-puna-gara*

Sanskrit Praise, the Thirteenth:

*Loka-gāya-loka-vigāya-tu-jatīnāṃ pravālu-vara-vara-nurāyaṇa-trāṇa adūrṣa-yūma-rākarmī-naṃ*

Sanskrit Praise, the Fourteenth:

*Gāṃbhīra-śca-nāvanīya-śca-kālāyaṇa mitra-saṃpanna vaidya-satvaṃ śarāya-hṛta-ca-nāra-nāmāya su-sāratthī*

Sanskrit Praise, the Fifteenth:

*Mati-gati-śraiva-buddhi-śca-vicakṣaṇa puṇya-vaṃ-kāra-pa-bṛkṣa-śca bodhy-aṅga-puṣpa-maṇita*

Sanskrit Praise, the Sixteenth:

*Vimuktī-phala-saṃpanna asarāya-sarva-nihināṃ manu-haru-manu-gāya-śca anargha-brahma-cāriṇaṃ*

Sanskrit Praise, the Seventeenth:

*Ketu-satvaṃ-gara-ha-śrīṣṭha-satvaṃ ṛṣi-bhī-muni-pū-gavā-yū-vara-jā-bhīṣikta-tatvaṃ naśa-bhūmi-śvarū-prabhū*

Sanskrit Praise, the Eighteenth:

*Sārtha-vāhu-gaṇa-śrīṣṭha nirvāṇi-śūta-mādhiśāka-kha-sama-mādhya-kārapa-satvaṃ tad-te-jñāva-yūrvaca*

Sanskrit Praise, the Nineteenth:

*Tatvaṃ-cinta-maṇi-sattvānāṃ sarva-śa-paripūraka namostu te mahā-vidyā sarva-bhūta-namas-kṛta*
