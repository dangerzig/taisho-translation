# 佛說普賢曼拏羅經
## Sūtra of Samantabhadra's Maṇḍala Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1126

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dānapāla (施護).

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time the Buddha was at Vulture Peak Mountain in Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of fifty hundred-thousand bhikṣus.

There were also great bodhisattva mahāsattvas, all fully endowed with every wholesome dharma, who had attained immeasurable wisdom and skillful means and were able to give the lion's roar. Their names were:

Samantabhadra Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, Jewel-Seal-Hand Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, Sport Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, Merit-Adornment Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, Merit-Sound Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, Great-Intent Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, and Virtue-Adornment Bodhisattva Mahāsattva. Such bodhisattva mahāsattvas all came and assembled.

At that time the World-Honored One, with great compassion, for the sake of all present and future practitioners who wished to practice the secret corresponding method of Vajrasattva and attain swift accomplishment, proclaimed the great maṇḍala method of the Vajradhātu.

The Buddha said: "If there are good men who wish to enter the great maṇḍala of the Vajradhātu and practice the secret corresponding method of Samantabhadra Vajrasattva for swift accomplishment, they should first generate a courageous, firm, and supreme mind. Seek a pure and excellent teacher in the world, and request the abhiṣeka. Having received it, receive from that teacher the transmission of the secret corresponding seal-mudrā ritual methods, remembering each one without error, with focused attention, not allowing forgetfulness or mistakes.

"Then, in whatever pure and excellent place one wishes, practice this dharma to seek siddhi. Having obtained the excellent location, first bathe and purify the body and mind in clean purity. Then at that place, first establish the boundary and protect the body. Then, in accordance with the dharma, apply fragrant mud to the maṇḍala with utmost propriety. When the application is complete, within this maṇḍala, burn incense, scatter flowers, and make various offerings. Facing this maṇḍala, perform visualization: visualize within the maṇḍala the principal deity tathāgata. When he appears before you, without rising from your seat, take unguent and apply it to the hands, form the seal, and recite the secret word.

"Next, form the purification-of-three-karmas seal. Both hands in vajra clasp. Recite the root secret word three times:

*Oṃ svabhāva-śuddhaḥ sarva-dharma svabhāva-śuddho haṃ*

"Having recited this secret word, release the seal. Then say: 'From beginningless time until now, all my coarse and heavy karmas of body, speech, and mind are now purified. Having been purified, may all the deeds I now perform become the cause for entering the vajra dharma.' Then further generate the intention to visualize the principal deity tathāgata: 'May all the deeds I perform be accomplished.' Then take refuge and offer praise. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ sarva-tathāgata-kāya-vāk-citta praṇāme vajra-bandhanāṃ karomi*

"Having recited this secret word, bow to the principal deity tathāgata in the maṇḍala with the four types of prostration. After bowing, kneel with hands in vajra añjali and speak these words: 'I now generate the aspiration for supreme bodhi. I beseech the Perfectly Awakened One, Teacher of the Three Times, to skillfully accept me. May I rightly receive the three types of training precepts. For the sake of all sentient beings, with vajra firmness, may I uphold the Three Jewels and benefit sentient beings. I now, within the true-suchness corresponding secret dharma of the Tathāgata's Vajradhātu Vajrasattva, receive from the ācārya the great vajra family and practice the four types of giving at six times daily. In the great jewel family, I receive the dharma of equality correspondence. In the great lotus family, I receive the subtle and wondrous secret dharma of the three vehicles. In the great karma family, I receive all the offering activities. From pure true suchness, the dharma of great bodhi is born. Having generated the aspiration for supreme bodhi and received these dharmas, I shall practice in accordance with the dharma, for the benefit of all sentient beings. For all sentient beings without a sovereign, I shall serve as sovereign. For those not yet liberated, I shall cause them to be liberated. For those not yet at ease, I shall cause them to be at ease. For those who have not yet reached the other shore, I shall cause them to reach the other shore.'

"Having made these vows, the practitioner should recite the vajra-gaze secret word:

> 'Press upon both eyes,
> And contemplate them as sun and moon.
> Both hands in vajra fists,
> Each placed at the waist.
> The maṇḍala, incense, flowers,
> And all other offering implements:
> Through this gaze upon them,
> Defilements are removed and purity is attained.'

"The vajra-gaze secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-dṛṣṭi maṭha*

"Both hands in vajra bond, able to release all fetters and purify the eighth consciousness of all its defiled seeds. The vajra-bond secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-bandha*

"By this seal and reciting the secret word, one obtains the release of all fetters and bonds from beginningless time, and causes the practice of all merits to be perfected. Next, with the bond-seal, open it apart at the heart. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-bandha trāṭ*

"By this seal and reciting the secret word, one's inherent vajra wisdom is suddenly revealed. Then ring the vajra bell three times. Next, visualize within one's own body the appearance of the *Aḥ*-syllable wheel, possessing great awesome virtue. Because of this, all māra-obstacles will be unable to disturb, and one further obtains the vajra firm mind of non-retrogression and attains all accomplishments. Next recite the heart secret word:

*Ha ha ha ha hoḥ*

"Having recited the heart secret word, enter the Vajrasattva firm wisdom. Visualize the entire space-realm: all natures are equal, identical to the sixteen sounds. Next, visualize one's own mind becoming a round moon-disc. Within the moon-disc, visualize the syllable *Ka*. When the syllable appears clearly and distinctly, further visualize outside the moon-disc a single syllable *Ka*, circling like a star. When it appears before you, further visualize that from the *Ka*-syllable heart moon-disc arises a golden vajra lotus. From the center of this lotus emerges a lotus seal, and upon this seal contemplate the syllable *Haṃ*. When the syllable appears clearly and distinctly, further contemplate one's own body as identical to Vajrasattva, without difference or distinction, unmoving and unshaking. Then cast the vajra pestle and speak thus: 'I now, without changing this body, am born from the vajra dharma and am Vajrasattva.'

"Next, visualize one's body becoming the great samaya-sattva. Then form the vajra great samaya seal and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ samāyu haṃ mahā-samāyu haṃ*

"Then with the preceding samaya seal, seal the heart, forehead, throat, and crown. Next form the World-Honored One's great seal and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ samaya-sattva adhitiṣṭha svāṃ*

"Then recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-dharma hrīḥ*

"By this seal and secret word, my body, speech, and mind become the three vajra karmas. Next form the accomplishment great seal and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ samaya aḥ*

"Next, visualize within the preceding heart moon-disc the vajra-dharma *Aḥ*-syllable wheel and lotus, up to and including the syllable *Haṃ* within the lotus: each and every one is manifested from within my own vajra-dharma self-nature body. Having visualized thus, form the vajra-hook seal. With both hands make vajra fists, both little fingers hooking in reverse, both index fingers bent like hooks. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-kuśa jaḥ*

"Next form the vajra-lasso seal. Without changing the preceding seal, both index fingers hook in reverse. This is called the lasso seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-pāśa hūṃ*

"Next form the vajra-chain seal. Interlock both hands internally. This is called the vajra chain. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-sphoṭa vaṃ*

"Next form the vajra-bell seal. Both hands in vajra bond, shake three times like ringing a bell. This is called the vajra bell. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-āveśa aḥ*

"Next, contemplate one's own body as identical to Vajrasattva's wisdom body. Having seen it appear before you, recite the secret word:

*Jaḥ hūṃ vaṃ hoḥ*

"Next form the samaya seal and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ samaya sattvaṃ*

"Having recited this secret word, again visualize one's own mind becoming a round moon-disc. Visualize one's body entering this moon-disc and becoming the great Vajrasattva. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ mahā-samaya sattvaṃ*

"Next recite the vajra-dharma secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-dharma hrīḥ*

"Having recited this secret word, further visualize the preceding heart moon-disc transforming to red. Within it, the principal deity tathāgata, with supreme and extraordinary marks, eyes like lotus petals, wearing a gentle smile, compassionate, joyful, serene, and at ease. Having thus visualized, next form the four tathāgata samaya secret seals, each empowering the body with their respective true words.

"First, form the Vajrasattva seal. Both hands make a vajra bond with both middle fingers standing upright like needles. This is named the Vajrasattva seal. Place this seal at the heart and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ sarva-vajrī adhitiṣṭha svāṃ*

"Without changing the preceding seal form, bend both middle fingers like a jewel. This is named Vajraratna. Place this seal at the forehead and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ ratna-vajrī adhitiṣṭha svāṃ*

"Without changing the preceding seal form, place both middle fingers like lotus petals. This is named the Vajra-Lotus seal. Place this seal at the throat and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ dharma-vajrī adhitiṣṭha svāṃ*

"Without changing the preceding seal form, cross both middle fingers standing upright. This is named the Karma seal. Place this seal at the crown and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ karma-vajrī adhitiṣṭha svāṃ*

"Next, further visualize the principal deity tathāgata within the red moon-disc. Then form the finger-snap invitation seal: cross both arms and snap the vajra finger-snap, extending to summoning all buddhas equally the same. Snap the left fingers: all wholesome deeds are swiftly accomplished. Snap the right fingers: swift assembly is obtained. The finger-snap secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-samāja jaḥ*

"Next, recite the one-hundred-and-eight-name hymn:

> "Vajra-born Great Being, Vajra of all Tathāgatas,
> Vajra-vehicle Samantabhadra: homage to Vajrapāṇi!
> Vajra King of Wondrous Awakening, Vajra Hook Tathāgata,
> Firm and Unerring King: homage to Vajra-Staff!
> Vajra Love of Great Bliss, Vajra Arrow of Subjugation,
> Vajra Great Māra-Desire: homage to Vajra-Bow!
> Vajra Mind of Wondrous Peak, Vajra Serene Great Love,
> Vajra King of Delight: homage to Vajra-Joy!
> Vajra Jewel of Wondrous Benefit, Vajra Space Great Treasure,
> Vajra Ākāśagarbha: homage to Vajra-Treasury!
> Vajra Light of Great Flame, Vajra Light Buddha-Sun,
> Vajra Flame of Great Illumination: homage to Vajra-Light!
> Vajra Banner of Wondrous Benefit, Vajra Flag of Wondrous Joy,
> Vajra Great Jewel-Banner: homage to Firm Banner!
> Vajra Laughter of Great Joy, Vajra Thought of Great Wonder,
> Vajra Love and Delight: homage to Vajra-Love!
> Vajra Dharma of Wondrous Benefit, Vajra Lotus of Wondrous Purity,
> Wondrous-Eyed Avalokiteśvara: homage to Vajra-Eye!
> Vajra Benefit of Great Vehicle, Vajra Hook of Great Armor,
> Mañjuśrī Most Profound: homage to Vajra-Wisdom!
> Vajra Cause of Great Kṣetra, Vajra Wheel of Great Vehicle,
> Vajra Wondrous Wisdom-Turning: homage to Vajra-Kṣetra!
> Vajra Speech of Wondrous Light, Vajra Mindfulness of Wondrous Accomplishment,
> Vajra Accomplishment Beyond Words: homage to Vajra-Speech!
> Vajra Karma of Wondrous Wisdom, Vajra Action of All,
> Vajra Space of Great Power: homage to Vajra-Karma!
> Vajra Protection of Great Vigor, Vajra Armor of Great Firmness,
> Wrathful Wondrous Vigor: homage to Firm Vigor!
> Firm Food of Great Skill, Vajra Fang of Great Terror,
> Vajra Peak Māra-Crusher: homage to Firm Wrath!
> Vajra Fist of Wondrous Intent, Vajra Bond of Liberation,
> Vajra Fist of Equality: homage to Firm Fist!"

"By reciting these one hundred and eight supreme-name phrases, one receives the initial abhiṣeka of all. If there are those who can honor and praise these names, they receive and uphold the great vajra. If one constantly sings these name-phrases, one obtains the fame of holding the vajra. For this reason I have already proclaimed it.

"Having visualized the principal deity tathāgata and seen him appear before you, form the vajra-hook seal as the karma-seal for summoning. When the deity has descended, form the samaya seal and recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-dharma jaḥ hūṃ vaṃ hoḥ samaya sattvaṃ ma-haṃ*

"Then recite the vajra-dharma secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-dharma hrīḥ*

"Next, with both index fingers touching, bent like lotus petals: this is named the Vajra-Dharma samaya seal. Next the great karma seal, the World-Honored One's great seal, then the abhiṣeka seal. Having offered the five offerings, uphold and recite the secret word of one's practice. When the count is fulfilled, form the seal and recite the eight offering vidyās, making offerings to buddhas, worthies, and holy ones.

"With both hands make vajra fists and place them at the waist accordingly. Tilt the head slightly to the left. This is named the Sport seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-lāse*

"Next, with both hands make vajra fists. With both index fingers make the gesture of tying a garland at the forehead and behind the head. This is named the Garland seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-māle*

"Next, with both hands in vajra clasp, spread open all five fingers in reverse, as if holding flowers. This is named the Song seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-gīte*

"Next, with both hands make vajra fists. First join the palms with hollow center, then assume a dancing posture. This is named the Dance seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-nṛtye*

"Next, with both hands make a vajra bond, then scatter downward. This is named the incense-offering seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-dhūpe*

"Next, with eight fingers in vajra internal bond, open upward before the heart as if holding flowers. This is named the flower-offering seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-puṣpe*

"Next, with both hands make a vajra bond and stand both index fingers upright and open. This is named the lamp-offering seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-āloke*

"Next, with both hands make a vajra bond and open at the heart to apply unguent. This is the unguent-offering seal. Recite the secret word:

*Oṃ vajra-gandhe*

"All these secret words and seal-mudrās possess great awesome power. One should apply the mind to benefit self and others. Furthermore, abiding in the samaya correspondence, visualize within the heart the appearance of an *Oṃ*-syllable wheel. When the syllable appears, further visualize flowing forth from the *Oṃ* syllable jeweled canopies, jeweled banners, jeweled pennants, wrist-bangles, ear-rings, necklaces, garments, crowns, and other jeweled adornments, and even a hundred flavors of food and drink, and clouds of the seven treasures. Apply the mind to each, pervading before all buddhas and bodhisattvas, reverently presenting them as offerings.

"Then make the vajra añjali seal, and with scattered visualization, offer to all buddhas and bodhisattvas of the ten directions. Having thus made offerings, take the merit of recitation or whatever wholesome deeds have been performed, dedicate and make vows, then in accordance with the dharma dispatch and send off the worthies and holy ones."

At that time, when the World-Honored One had proclaimed this dharma, all the great bodhisattva mahāsattvas and the entire assembly of great bhikṣus who were present, having heard what the Buddha proclaimed, joyfully and faithfully received it, bowed to the Buddha, and withdrew.
