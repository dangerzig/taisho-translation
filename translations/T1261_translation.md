# 訶利帝母真言經
## Sūtra of the Mantras of Hārītī

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1261

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Amoghavajra (不空).

---

At that time, the yakṣa woman Hārītī, who was in the Buddha's assembly, rose from her seat, prostrated with her entire body touching the ground, bowed at the Buddha's feet, and addressed the Buddha: "I possess a heart mantra that is like a cintāmaṇi jewel, able to fulfill all wishes. For the benefit and happiness of the good men and good women of Jambudvīpa, I humbly beseech the World-Honored One to compassionately grant me permission, and I shall now proclaim it."

The mantra:

*Oṃ nunu mārī-ka hīdī svāhā*

"If there are women who cannot conceive children, or whose pregnancies miscarry and fail, all due to the four elements being out of balance, or ghosts and spirits creating obstacles, or past karmic causes preventing conception: one should take white cotton cloth, one cubit or one span or five inches in length as desired, and paint Hārītī in the form of a celestial woman, entirely golden in color, wearing celestial garments, a crown, and necklaces. She is seated upon a platform with both feet hanging down. On either side of the hanging feet, paint two children standing beside the platform. Upon each knee sits one child, in the left arm she cradles one child, and in the right hand she holds an auspicious fruit. The painter should observe the eight precepts, and no animal-based glue should be used in the pigments.

"When the painting is complete, purify a room and arrange it with proper dignity, plastering it clean. Apply fragrant paste to make a square altar and place the image within the altar. Scatter various flowers upon the altar. Further set out sweet delicious food and drink, milk porridge, yogurt rice, and various fruits, arghya fragrant water, and burn aloeswood incense as offerings. Have the image face west. The practitioner should face east, and facing the image recite the mantra, three times daily, one thousand times per session. Begin on the fifth day of the new moon, first reciting one hundred thousand times. Afterward, recite before the image, and all that is sought will be perfectly fulfilled.

"Another method: if a woman wishes to conceive, after her monthly course she should bathe. Take a yellow cow whose calf is the same color, and milk one *sheng* of milk into a silver vessel. Stir the milk with the ring finger of the right hand, reciting the mantra with empowerment one thousand and eighty times, then drink it. Within seven days she will conceive.

"Another method: if one wishes to cause another person to be delighted and loving, take food, fruit, flowers, or incense and empower it one hundred and eight times. Within the mantra phrases, insert that person's name. Give it to that person, and they will be delighted and filled with loving respect.

"Another method: if someone is causing obstacles or disputes, paint that person's form and step on it with the left foot while reciting the mantra one thousand and eighty times. The difficulties will then cease.

"Another method: if one wishes all people to be delighted, take powdered ox-yellow and place it in a silver vessel. As before, stir it with the ring finger, reciting the mantra with empowerment one hundred and eight times, and apply it to the forehead. All people who see you will be delighted and submit.

"Another method: if one obtains new garments, empower them one hundred and eight times before the image, then wear them. All people who see you will likewise be delighted and loving.

"Another method: if one has evil dreams, recite the mantra one hundred and eight times, and the evil dreams will be eliminated.

"Another method: during a lunar eclipse, take five *liang* of ghee and place it in a golden vessel. Stir it with golden chopsticks without interruption while reciting the mantra with empowerment, continuing until the moon is restored to fullness. Then take a portion and offer it to Hārītī, and gradually consume the rest. One will then conceive, and the children born will be wise, blessed, and virtuous.

"Another method: take an *aśvattha* root tooth-stick, empower it one hundred and eight times, and chew it while cleaning the teeth. Whatever words one speaks will delight those who hear them, and whatever one wishes to obtain from others will be accomplished as desired.

"Another method: if one wishes for a long lifespan, take tender shoots of *kuśa* grass, dip them in ghee, honey, and yogurt, and perform a homa for seven nights. Each night recite the mantra one thousand and eighty times, throwing one offering into the fire per recitation. One will thereby obtain longevity.

"Another method: if one wishes for all deeds and undertakings to be accomplished as desired, on the fifth and twentieth days of each new moon, plaster the practice-hall clean and scatter flowers. Set out various food and drink, milk porridge, yogurt rice, and various fruits, arghya fragrant water, and burn aloeswood and frankincense as offerings. First make offerings to all tathāgatas, Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva, Samantabhadra, and all bodhisattvas, then afterward make offerings to me, Hārītī. Why? Because I was originally a yakṣa woman. The Tathāgata granted me the three refuges, five precepts, and bodhicitta discipline precepts before the assembly of the ten-stage bodhisattvas. Therefore, first make offerings to the buddhas and bodhisattvas. After the offerings, recite ten thousand times before this image, and all undertakings sought will be completely fulfilled."

The recitation method for Hārītī.

The loving-child heart mantra:

*Oṃ jivi-jivinī svāhā*

The mudrā: join the palms and bend both thumbs into the palms. Recite the heart mantra to make offerings. Then release both thumbs above the crown, and the deity departs. For invitation and dispatch, both use this mudrā.

Take white sandalwood, measure six fingers in width, and carve the form of a child with both hands each holding a fruit to give to people, with three topknots on the head. Install it in a quiet, pure place and recite the mantra ten thousand times. The deity will then manifest and ask: "Why have you called me?" Answer as your heart desires.

Take yellow beef, one square inch per piece, empower and burn one per recitation, three times daily. After four days one will attain great mastery and have all wishes fulfilled.

"Another method: make pills from beef and frankincense. On the eighth day of the month, recite three times daily, burning the incense one hundred and eight times per session. By the tenth day, at midnight a great radiance will appear and one will obtain great supernatural power.

"Make pills from frankincense, empowering and burning one per recitation, three times daily, one thousand and eighty per session. In five days one will be able to tame all people.

"Another method: empower flowers of a hundred grasses one by one and scatter them upon the body. One will obtain clothing and food for a thousand people.

"Another method: daily recite the mantra while burning storax for seven days, and one will see golden treasuries beneath the earth. Offer various flowers, fruits, food, and drink within the altar daily, and one will obtain all wealth and treasures.

"Another method: mix frankincense with milk porridge, empower and burn one per recitation for seven days, three times daily, one thousand and eighty per session. The deity will manifest and converse, answering questions as desired.

"Another method: if imprisoned, recite ten thousand times and one will be released. To cure illness, empower an āmra fruit. Soak sprouts in milk, burn with empowerment, and all diseases will be eliminated."
