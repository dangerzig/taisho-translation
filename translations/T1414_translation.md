# 佛說金身陀羅尼經
## Sūtra on the Golden Body Dhāraṇī Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1414

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dānapāla (施護).

---

At that time, the World-Honored One, for the universal benefit of all sentient beings, proclaimed this dhāraṇī:

*namo buddhānām atīta-anāgata-pratyutpannānāṃ | namo amitābhāya tathāgatāya arhate samyak-saṃbuddhāya | tadyathā oṃ [ma]vīri [ma]vīri medhāvati medhāvati amṛtodbhave buddha-mati buddha-bhaṣite sarva-dharmo[lka]-jvālinī buddhi buddhi mahā-buddhi mahā-vīrye-[ga]vati karuṇa-vegavati indra-vajra-vegavati buddha-vilokite muni muni mahā-muni phaṭ | namo buddha-dharma-saṃghānāṃ mālinā sarva-yakṣa-rākṣasa-piśāca-kūṣmāṇḍa-pūtana-kaṭapūtana sarva-grahān devān-duṣṭāṃś-citāṃ parā[bhe]ṇa gṛhṇa gṛhṇa grasa grasa māra māra bhañja bhañja daha daha paca paca hana hana sarva-buddhānāṃ mālinā nāśaya nāśaya bhinda bhinda chinda chinda [mu]ru [mu]ru vidrāpaya vidrāpaya sarva-rakṣāṃś-ca mānuṣa-amānuṣān bandha bandha saṃkuca saṃkuca vikuca vikuca sphuṭa sphuṭa [tri]ja [tri]ja garjja garjja hana hana sarva-mantrāṃ sarve[ṣāṃ] vihana vihana rakṣa māṃ sa-parivāraṃ sarva-sattvānāṃ ca svāhā*

The Buddha said: "This dhāraṇī possesses great merit. If a person can wear it upon the crown of their head, all rākṣasas, piśāca ghosts, and the like within a thousand yojanas will be unable to cause harm.

"Furthermore, if a person recites this dhāraṇī just once, various devas and their retinues will provide protection from within a thousand yojanas.

"Furthermore, when a person recites this dhāraṇī, all enemies, great evil tigers, wolves, insects, and beasts will subdue themselves and be unable to cause harm.

"Furthermore, if a person can give rise to utmost sincerity and each day recites this dhāraṇī a full hundred times, that person will attain the accomplishment of añjana eye-medicine. When applied to the eyes, one will be able to see events of the three times.

"Furthermore, when a person recites this dhāraṇī, it can cause those with wrathful minds to generate minds of respect and love.

"Furthermore, if a person single-mindedly recites this dhāraṇī two hundred thousand times, all afflictions will be entirely extinguished. Furthermore, if a person recites this dhāraṇī for a long time, that person will attain the knowledge of past lives.

"Furthermore, if a person empowers pure oil with this dhāraṇī and applies it to the head and abdomen, if that person has headaches, abdominal illnesses, or any other evil and poisonous diseases, wherever the oil is applied, the disease will dissolve and scatter.

"Furthermore, if a person takes a single grain of black pepper, empowers it with this dhāraṇī twenty-one times, and brings it before a king, whatever one wishes to request will be fulfilled. This dhāraṇī possesses great merit. If one wishes to accomplish such matters as described above, one should receive and uphold it."
