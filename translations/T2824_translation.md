# 天台分門圖
## Tiantai Classification Diagram

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 2824

Translated from the Chinese. Translator unknown (失譯)

---

The first gate of Tiantai has already been set out as listed above. We now come to the second: clarifying the two prefaces, ancient and modern. The main gate divides into two.

> The ancient and modern [texts have been] verified through three cycles of auditing and collation;
> later students, please harbor no doubts.

Among the three gates, the Tiantai [discussion of] ancient and modern [prefaces] has been explained above and is now complete. We now come to the third: the proper explanation of the sūtra title. With respect to the title, it is briefly divided into two.
