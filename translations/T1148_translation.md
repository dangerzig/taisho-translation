# 佛說虛空藏菩薩陀羅尼
## Dhāraṇī of the Bodhisattva Ākāśagarbha, Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1148

Translated from the Chinese. Song Dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢)

---

[*Dhāraṇī:*] Namo vipulanāma-prasṛta-nāya nabhas-surabhī-kṣata-gagana-maṇḍalaye ākāśa-garbhāya gagana-gocarāya sāgara-bhuvana-maṇḍala-vaśayate hrīḥ oṃ svasti malaṭa vipula-saṃbhava-dharmadhātu-gocara svāhā

---

*Note: The body of this text consists entirely of a phonetic transcription of a Sanskrit dhāraṇī rendered in Chinese characters. The above represents a syllabic reconstruction. High-confidence identifications include: namo (那謨), gagana (誐誐那), maṇḍala (曼拏羅), ākāśa (阿哥舍), garbhāya (誐哩婆野), gocarāya (悟左囉野), sāgara (薩哥羅), bhuvana (部嚩拏), vaśayate (嚩舍拽帝), oṃ (唵), dharma (達哩摩), dhātu (馱覩), gocara (悟左囉), and svāhā (娑嚩賀). Other syllables admit greater uncertainty in reconstruction.*
