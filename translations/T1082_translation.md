# 觀世音菩薩祕密藏如意輪陀羅尼神呪經
## Sūtra of the Divine Spell of the Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī of the Secret Treasury of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1082

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Śikṣānanda (實叉難陀).

---

## Chapter One: Dhāraṇī for Eliminating and Destroying All Evil Karma

Thus have I heard:

At one time the Buddha was on Mount Gṛdhrakūṭa, together with a great assembly of bodhisattvas. At that time Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, knelt with palms joined, and addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I have a great dhāraṇī dharma named the Mahā-padma-candana-maṇi Heart-Wheel, which can accomplish whatever is sought in any endeavor. If the Tathāgata in his great compassion permits me to speak it, I shall, empowered by the Buddha's divine power, speak it for the benefit of all sentient beings. Why? World-Honored One! This dhāraṇī has great divine power. It is like a cintāmaṇi jewel and like a wish-fulfilling tree, able to fulfill all wishes." At that time the World-Honored One praised Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva: "Excellent, excellent! Out of compassion, you ask thus for sentient beings. I grant you permission to speak this dhāraṇī without obstruction." Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, having received permission, rose with palms joined, made obeisance, returned to his original seat, contemplated the assembly with great compassion, and immediately spoke the spell:

*Namo ratna-trayāya namaḥ ārya-avalokiteśvarāya bodhisattvāya mahā-sattvāya mahā-kāruṇikāya tadyathā oṃ cakra-varti candana-maṇi mahā-padme ruru-tiṣṭha jvala ākarṣāya hūṃ phaṭ svāhā*

The above is the body spell.

*Oṃ padme candana-maṇi mahā-jvala hūṃ*

The above is the heart spell.

*Oṃ varada-padme hūṃ*

At that time, when Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva had spoken this Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī, the great earth shook in six ways. The palaces of the devas, nāgas, yakṣas, gandharvas, asuras, garuḍas, kiṃnaras, and mahoragas all trembled. The Māra King and all the hosts of māras were greatly terrified, and the Māra King's palaces all burst into flames. All the other yakṣas and evil ghosts were stricken with panic and fell face-down upon the ground. The gates of all the hells opened, and the sinners within were liberated and received celestial happiness. At that time jeweled flowers rained from the sky along with various precious ornaments, and celestial music in space produced all manner of sounds as offerings to the Tathāgata. At that time the World-Honored One, with the voice of Brahmā, spoke verses praising Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva:

> "Excellent, excellent! Good man! Out of compassion for sentient beings,
> You speak this dhāraṇī that can grant sentient beings the greatest benefit."

At that time the World-Honored One further told Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva: "Good man! You are able, for the sake of sentient beings, to speak this dhāraṇī dharma of the Great Supernatural King." Then Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva addressed the Buddha: "If good men, good women, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, upāsikās, boys, or girls, in this present life, wish to obtain immediate results, they should, day and night, with single-minded diligence, not forget this dhāraṇī. There is no need to discriminate between times and days, pure or impure. Once one has mastered the recitation, results will immediately appear. For whatever is sought, one should recite one hundred and eight times, and a hundred thousand things will be accomplished. There is no other divine spell that surpasses this Wish-Fulfilling Wheel King Dhāraṇī. Why? Because it can destroy all the heavy karmic obstructions of past and present evil. If one can recite this dhāraṇī, even one who should fall into the Avīci hell will be liberated. The five heinous crimes are also completely eliminated; how much more so for other evil karma and all adversities and illnesses. If one suffers from a febrile illness, whether occurring daily, every other day, every third day, or every fourth day, whether by day or night, or from wind, bile, phlegm, or other illnesses, reciting this spell will bring complete cure. If one has been afflicted with sorcery, cursing magic, boils, scabies, leprosy, itching, madness, or headache, or pain in the ears, nose, lips, tongue, teeth, throat, mouth, face, head, brain, chest, ribs, heart, abdomen, waist, back, legs, hands, head, or feet, all will be cured. Whatever illness exists in the body, this spell treats them all. Yakṣas, rākṣasas, vināyakas, evil māras, and malevolent spirits are all unable to cause harm. Nor need one fear weapons, water, fire, evil wind, rain, or hail. Enemies, robbers, evil kings, and evil bandits can never cause harm, and one will never die an untimely death. All evil dreams, vipers, snakes, asps, scorpions, geckos, centipedes, spiders, and all evil venomous beasts, lions, tigers, and wolves are unable to cause harm. In battles and warfare, one will always be victorious. If there are lawsuits or disputes with officials, all will be resolved. If one recites this dhāraṇī even once, all the above matters will be accomplished as wished. If one recites this dhāraṇī one hundred and eight times daily, Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva will appear and say: 'Good men and others, do not be afraid. Whatever vow you wish, I will grant you all of it.' Amitābha Buddha will manifest in person, and one will see the adornments of the Land of Ultimate Bliss as described in the sūtras. One will also see the assembly of bodhisattvas in the Land of Ultimate Bliss. One will also see all the buddhas of the ten directions. One will also see Mount Potalaka, the dwelling place of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva. One will immediately attain purity of body and will always be reverently honored by kings, nobles, ministers, and all people. Wherever one is reborn, one will not pass through a womb but will be born upon a lotus, subtly adorned. In every place of rebirth, one will always possess the knowledge of past lives. From this day until the attainment of buddhahood, one will never fall into the evil destinies and will always be born in the presence of a buddha."

## Chapter Two: The Secret Treasury of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva: Dharma of Inspiring Love and Delight in All

At that time Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, out of compassion for sentient beings, further spoke the Secret Wish-Fulfilling Heart-Wheel Dhāraṇī: "Whatever one needs will all come of its own accord. There are two kinds of wealth: worldly wealth and supramundane wealth. Worldly wealth consists of gold, silver, and other treasures. Supramundane wealth consists of merit and wisdom, endowed with both adornments, bringing joy of body and mind and the love and respect of all people. It can rescue all sentient beings from suffering, increase compassion, provide wise beings with delightful resources and benefits, and augment one's power. This is the realm of the secret treasury and should not be revealed to others. If one wishes to obtain the most supreme efficacy of this Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī, one should, with utmost sincerity at all times and in all places, pure or impure, constantly recite and uphold it without any fault, completing one's daily recitation. One should also call upon the name of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva and the Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī, together with the name of the desired person, and at times think of them. Whether it is a king, prince, queen, princess, brāhmaṇa, kṣatriya, vaiśya, śūdra, man or woman, boy or girl, or any kind of non-Buddhist practitioner, if one wishes an audience with them, one should call their name. Every day at the fifth watch, one should complete the daily recitation. If one wishes the most supreme efficacy, such as an audience with a king, one should recite one thousand and eight times at the fifth watch for seven days, and the audience will be granted. If one wishes to see the queen, recite nine hundred times. If one wishes to see a prince, recite eight hundred times. If one wishes to see palace attendants, recite seven hundred times. If one wishes to see a princess, recite six hundred times. For an audience with a brāhmaṇa, recite five hundred times. For a kṣatriya, four hundred times. For a vaiśya, three hundred times. For a bhikṣu or bhikṣuṇī, one hundred times. For an upāsaka or upāsikā, ninety times. For a boy or girl, sixty times. This is called the method of the daily recitation for gaining proximity, and it can accomplish all affairs. Whatever material goods, servants, horses, or delightful provisions one desires, even if far away, will come as swiftly as the wind when one's mind seeks them. For whatever one wishes to accomplish, once the daily recitation is complete, the matter will be accomplished. If one wishes to see Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, recite one thousand and eight times, and one will see his true body, and all things will be fulfilled. If one wishes to see a vajra-being, recite ten thousand times, and it will appear before one, caring for that person as a father loves a child, granting whatever the heart desires. If one wishes to see all the buddhas and the great assembly, recite thirteen thousand times and one will see them. If one recites continuously for seven days and seven nights, all the spell-deities of every dhāraṇī one has upheld will all appear, each bestowing the efficacy of their own spell upon that person and constantly following as protectors. On the seventh day, the lord of the three-thousand great-thousand world-system and Lord Śakra, together with their retinues, will all come and grant one's wishes. If one can practice the daily recitation as described above, all wished-for vows will be fulfilled."

## Chapter Three: The Dhāraṇī and Agada Medicine of Avalokiteśvara for Inspiring Love and Delight

Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, again out of compassion for sentient beings, spoke: "This is the dharma of the medicine of love and delight, which causes all who see one to generate a joyful mind. Once the compounding is complete, carry it on your person. This supreme accomplishment will cause all things to be fulfilled as wished. The ingredients are: ox-yellow, white sandalwood, *kuṅkuma* saffron, camphor, musk, *dhustūra* seeds, clove, *kakkola*, lotus, blue lotus, and gold leaf.

"Take equal parts of each, and white honey in equal proportion to the medicine. Pound and blend them while reciting the above spell one thousand and eight times. Use the incense to fumigate the body and clothing, or apply it to the eyelids, or dot the forehead and anoint the body. At that time, whether kings, queens, princes, officials, palace attendants, men, or women, all will love and delight in you, revere the dharma, generate the mind of awakening, and will not begrudge their bodily strength or material wealth, offering all in service. The benefits are beyond description. Like the sun and moon, all will wish to see you, and all affairs can be accomplished. If a person carries this medicine, karmic obstructions will be eliminated and all adversities will be resolved. Whether through the power of kings, by drowning or burning, by various weapons or poisons, by imprisonment or affliction, all will be resolved, if only one's sincerity is genuine. However, this medicine must not be placed in the mouth, as it is poisonous."

## Chapter Four: The Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Oral Medicine of Avalokiteśvara

Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, out of compassion for sentient beings, further spoke: "This is the oral pill that causes all to love and delight in one.

"Camphor, musk, and *kuṅkuma* saffron: pound them finely and blend with ox-yellow. Recite each of the above three spells one thousand and eight times. Mix with pure water to form pills the size of parasol-tree seeds. Further recite the three spells each one hundred and eight times. Dry the pills in shade; do not let wind or sunlight reach them. Over each individual pill, recite each of the three spells seven times. Then place one pill in the mouth. Whether speaking with kings, ministers, great assemblies, or any other kind of person, they will immediately generate respect and will not begrudge their treasures, giving whatever is needed. Whatever one says, all will trust and accept. Whatever one wishes will be accomplished. While the medicine is in the mouth, one must constantly recite the spell, and Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva will grant one's wishes. Before an image of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, use fragrant water to make a square altar four cubits on each side. Place various flowers within the altar, using whatever forest or garden flowers are available. Burn white sandalwood incense. Place the pills within the altar. Erect four banners and spread a white canopy over the altar. Hang four white pennants. Make offerings to Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva. Then recite the heart spell and the heart-within-heart spell each one hundred and eight times, and the body spell one hundred and eight times. Burn white sandalwood incense and scatter flowers. At that time, whatever vow one makes will be fully granted. Take the medicine from the altar and carry it on one's person. Wherever one goes and whatever one seeks will be obtained. Whatever one says will be trusted and accepted, and all affairs will be accomplished, except for those lacking sincerity."

## Chapter Five: The Heart-Wheel Eye Medicine of Avalokiteśvara

At that time Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, out of compassion for sentient beings, spoke: "This is the method for the eye medicine, which causes all people who see one to generate love, delight, and joy. The ingredients are: *māṃsīka kuṣṭha*, red lotus, blue lotus, cuttlefish bone powder, ox-yellow, *kuṅkuma* saffron, Chinese saffron, long pepper, black pepper, and dried ginger.

"Take equal parts, pound finely, and sieve. For every *liang* of this medicine, add half a *liang* of finely ground musk and camphor. Blend them before an image of Avalokiteśvara. Recite each of the above three spells one thousand and eight times, generating a mind of compassion toward all sentient beings. Place this medicine at the feet of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva. Then touch it and apply it, using a copper probe to dot the medicine in the corners of the eyes.

"For treating all eye ailments, cataracts, white films, tearing, red membranes, and blindness, as well as headache: apply this medicine to the eyes once daily, and all eye diseases will be cured. After two days of application, it treats all bodily illnesses. After three days, it treats eighty-four kinds of epilepsy. After four days, all internal and external obstacles are unable to obstruct. After five days, one will be victorious in all battles against enemies. After six days, all evil karma, afflictions, the four grave offenses, five heinous crimes, evil dreams, and sorcery will be destroyed, and one will never fall into the three evil destinies. After seven days, kings, ministers, and all great assemblies will comply with, respect, trust, accept, and delight in one. After two times seven days, one attains great mastery. After three times seven days, one gains audience with kings and ministers. After four times seven days, all yakṣas and their retinues become one's servants. After five times seven days, asuras, nāgas, yakṣas, and rākṣasas all become one's servants. After six times seven days, powerful flying rākṣasas, sorcery, and all dark arts will guard and protect one until the attainment of buddhahood. After seven times seven days, Mahākāla and the eight classes of spirits will all come and attend as servants. After eight times seven days, one becomes invisible to all. After nine times seven days, one sees all hidden treasures. After ten times seven days, the gates of the asura palace open of themselves, and one sees everything within and moves in and out without obstruction. After eleven times seven days, all medicinal substances appear like fire for healing, and if one seeks long life and great strength, they are immediately obtained. After twelve times seven days, mountains open and precious objects appear, to be taken and used at will. After thirteen times seven days, the nāga palace opens of itself, precious objects appear, and whatever one sees is without obstruction. After fourteen times seven days, all the celestial palaces of the desire realm open, and all can be seen. After fifteen times seven days, in the darkness of night, all is as bright as day. After sixteen times seven days, one sees the golden ground, vajra ground, water disc, wind disc, and space disc beneath the earth. After seventeen times seven days, one sees all the sentient beings in the hells throughout the four continents. By the power of having seen them, all the suffering beings are liberated. After eighteen times seven days, that person's power and virtue are like the sun. After nineteen times seven days, one sees the true body of the vajra, and all vows are fulfilled. After twenty times seven days, one sees the greatly compassionate Avalokiteśvara, and all wishes are completely fulfilled. After twenty-one times seven days, one can fly through space and see all the celestial palaces of the form realm opened wide, and also see the buddhas, bodhisattvas, and pure lands of the ten directions. If one applies it for one year, one attains the five kinds of pure eyes. If one can practice this method, one should deeply trust this teaching, have compassion for sentient beings, and not generate doubt. If doubt arises, the method will be difficult to accomplish, as described above."

## Chapter Six: The Fire-Homa Dhāraṇī Medicine of Avalokiteśvara

Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, out of compassion for sentient beings, spoke: "This is the auspicious fire-homa method, which can accomplish all affairs and can destroy all afflictions, karmic obstructions, and evil karma. If there are enemies, all will be subdued. Even a Vināyaka harboring hostility will of itself cease its anger and cause no further harm, and all people will generate love and respect.

"Dig a pit in the ground, one cubit square. Take equal parts of *marīca* twigs, *mālañca*, white mustard seeds, *dadhi*, honey, and ghee and blend them. Cut aloeswood and sandalwood sticks each twelve finger-widths long and of finger-width thickness, and place them in the pit to burn. Take a small amount of the medicine with the hand, recite the spell once, and cast it into the fire. Complete one thousand and eight recitations in this manner. This can destroy all karmic obstructions and extend the lifespan to two hundred and twenty years. If one performs the homa continuously for seven days, the lifespan will be one thousand years and the body will be purified. If one performs it for two times seven days, kings, princes, ministers, and all common people will submit in reverence. If one performs it for three times seven days, the thirty-three heavens and their retinues, together with the sun, moon, and four heavenly kings and their retinues, will all come to provide protection. The attendant vajra will bestow great efficacy, and Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva will fulfill one's great vows. If a kingdom lacks rain, empower white mustard seeds and ghee with the spell for three days and burn them in the fire; rain will immediately fall. If there is too much rain that will not stop, take ash from the pit, empower it with one hundred and eight recitations, and scatter it upward in the four directions; the rain will stop. If destructive hail or sudden violent wind arises, use this ash again, empower it with one hundred and eight recitations, and scatter it toward the clouds from a distance; the storm will stop. If one constantly recites this spell, one's strength will be like Nārāyaṇa. After leaving this body, one will be reborn in the Land of Ultimate Bliss. In every place of rebirth, one will always possess knowledge of past lives, until the attainment of buddhahood."

At that time Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! This Candana Heart-Wheel Dhāraṇī, as I have spoken it: if bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, upāsikās, men, or women receive and uphold it, they will certainly attain accomplishment. Do not harbor doubt. Whatever the mind thinks of, all will be obtained. One need only have deep faith, and must not generate doubt." At that time the Buddha praised Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva: "Excellent, excellent! Greatly compassionate Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva! You are able to speak this subtle Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī dharma, benefiting and delighting all the sentient beings of Jambudvīpa. If one generates the aspiration and recites it aloud, efficacy will be immediately obtained. Nevertheless, relying on my teaching, give to sentient beings, and repeatedly urge and instruct them with diligence, so that they may obtain efficacy. Manifest your body for them. Do not contradict my words, and I shall rejoice."

At that time Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! Since countless kalpas past, with a compassionate mind I have taken sentient beings in trust, constantly providing protection and granting them efficacy. The Buddha himself bears witness to this. For the sake of sentient beings, I have spoken this Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī. If there are those who receive, uphold, and constantly make their daily recitation, all vows will be granted. Empowered by the Buddha, I thus rescue suffering sentient beings."

At that time, when Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva had finished speaking this Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī Sūtra, the entire great assembly was greatly delighted, accepted with faith, and put it into practice.
