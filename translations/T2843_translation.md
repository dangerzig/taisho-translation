# Invocation of Great Compassion

## *Dàbēi Qǐqǐng* (大悲啟請)

Taishō 2843

Author unknown. A liturgical text for invoking the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteśvara and the Great Compassion Dhāraṇī.

### Invocation

> I reverently invoke Avalokiteśvara (*Guānzìzài*, 觀自在) of the Moon Wheel,
> Whose vast, perfectly complete body is of purple gold.
> A thousand arms are ever extended, manifesting in the world;
> A thousand eyes of radiance always illuminate everywhere.
>
> Twelve hundred true-word mudrās
> Can fulfill the wishes of all sentient beings.
> The face is adorned with three eyes in full splendor;
> The crown bears Amitābha (*Mítuó*, 彌陀) and holds the precious vessel.
>
> Proclaiming and praising the subtle and wonderful true words,
> Those who receive and uphold them shall realize the bodhi of the buddhas.
> With unobstructed eloquence, converting humans and devas,
> Among all the bodhisattvas, foremost in the assembly.
>
> Merely hearing it, one extinguishes sins as numerous as dust and sand;
> Chanting and reciting only increases the causes for attaining buddhahood.
> The vow-power of great compassion is inconceivable;
> Therefore I now constantly praise and recite.

Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I swiftly know all dharmas.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I quickly attain the eye of wisdom.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I swiftly deliver all beings.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I quickly attain skillful means.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I swiftly board the prajñā ship.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I quickly cross the sea of suffering.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I swiftly attain the way of precepts and samādhi.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I quickly ascend the mountain of nirvāṇa.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I swiftly meet the house of the unconditioned.
Homage to Great Compassion Avalokiteśvara: may I quickly realize the dharma-nature body.

> If I face the mountain of swords, the mountain of swords will crumble of itself.
> If I face the cauldron of fire, the cauldron of fire will be extinguished of itself.
> If I face the hells, the hells will dry up of themselves.
> If I face the hungry ghosts, the hungry ghosts will be sated of themselves.
> If I face the asuras, their evil minds will be subdued of themselves.
> If I face the animal realm, great wisdom will be attained of itself.

### The Great Compassion Heart Dhāraṇī of the Thousand-Armed, Thousand-Eyed Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva: Vast, Perfectly Complete, Unobstructed, Divine and Wonderful Phrases

[Dhāraṇī:] *Namo ratna-trayāya. Namaḥ āryāvalokiteśvarāya bodhisattvāya mahāsattvāya mahākāruṇikāya. Oṃ sarva-rabhaye sudhanadasya. Namas-kṛtvā imaṃ āryāvalokiteśvara-ramdhava. Namo narakindi hrīḥ mahā-vat-svāme. Sarva-arthato-śubhaṃ ajeyaṃ. Sarva-sat nama-vasat namo vāka mavitāto. Tadyathā: Oṃ avaloki lokate krate e hrīḥ mahābodhisattva. Sarva sarva mala mala mahima hṛdayaṃ. Kuru kuru karmaṃ. Dhuru dhuru vijayate mahāvijayate. Dhara dhara dhrīṇī śvarāya. Cala cala mama vimala muktele. Ehi ehi śīna śīna āraṣaṃ pracali viṣa viṣaṃ praśaya. Hulu hulu mara hulu hulu hrīḥ. Sara sara siri siri suru suru. Bodhiya bodhiya bodhaya bodhaya. Maitreya narakindi dhrṣṇīna bhayamāna svāhā. Siddhāya svāhā. Mahāsiddhāya svāhā. Siddhayogeśvarāya svāhā. Narakindi svāhā. Māraṇara svāhā. Śira-siṃha-mukhāya svāhā. Sarva mahā-asiddhāya svāhā. Cakra-asiddhāya svāhā. Padma-kastāya svāhā. Narakindi-vagalāya svāhā. Mavari-śaṅkarāya svāhā. Namo ratna-trayāya. Namaḥ āryāvalokiteśvarāya svāhā. Oṃ sidhyantu mantra padāya svāhā.*

### Transfer of Merit for the Great Compassion Heart Dhāraṇī of the Thousand-Armed, Thousand-Eyed Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva

> May the merit of reading and reciting extend universally to all sentient beings.
> May we and all beings together accomplish the buddha-way.
