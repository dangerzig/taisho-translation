# 佛說宿命智陀羅尼經
## Sūtra of the Dhāraṇī of Knowledge of Past Lives, Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1383

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢)

---

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed Ānanda, saying: "There is a dhāraṇī called 'Knowledge of Past Lives.' If any sentient being hears this dhāraṇī and is able to receive and uphold it with a sincere and resolute mind, all extremely grave karmic transgressions accumulated over a thousand kalpas will be entirely extinguished. If one is able to practice it throughout one's entire life without interruption, that person will, for seven koṭi lifetimes, constantly know their past lives."

He then proclaimed the dhāraṇī:

> *namo ratna-triṇe tathāgatāya arhate samyaksaṃbuddhāya | tathā | oṃ ratne ratne suratne ratnaudbhave mahāratna-kiraṇi ratna-sambhave svāhā*

At that time, Ānanda, having heard the Buddha proclaim this dhāraṇī of knowledge of past lives for the benefit of all sentient beings, marveled at what had never before been, and his joy was immeasurable. He then prostrated himself with his head at the Buddha's feet and withdrew.
