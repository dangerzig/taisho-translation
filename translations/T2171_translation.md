# 青龍寺求法目錄
## Catalog of Dharma-Seeking at Qinglong Monastery

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 2171

Compiled in Chinese. Tang dynasty, compiled by Enchin (圓珍) of Japan

---

*Catalog of Dharma-Seeking by the Bhikṣu Enchin of Enryakuji Monastery in the Capital of the Country of Japan*

A combined total of 115 fascicles of sūtras and ritual texts of the Garbhakośa and Vajradhātu two-maṇḍala systems, together with two maṇḍalas and ritual implements.

## Sūtras and Ritual Texts

The above two sections are recorded in the Kāiyuán Catalog.

The above are recorded in the Zhēnyuán Supplementary Catalog.

## Maṇḍalas, Ritual Implements, and Related Items

The above two items were entrusted by the Dharma-Transmitting Ācārya.

The foregoing sūtras, ritual texts, Buddha images, and other items were obtained from the Dharma-Transmitting Preceptor (*upādhyāya*) at Qinglong Monastery in Xinchang Ward on the Left Street of Chang'an, in the Country of the Great Tang. Copies were requested, transcribed, and verified as complete. Accordingly, the catalog is briefly presented as follows.

The fifteenth day of the eleventh month of the ninth year of the Dàzhōng era of the Great Tang.

Record of the Dharma-seeking bhikṣu.

Regarding the maṇḍalas and Dharma-gates listed above: the Preceptor himself attended to each matter, personally overseeing the inspection and verification. Each item conforms to the Dharma without discrepancy. Fearing that later students might harbor doubts, I respectfully submit this record.

May the Great Dharma-Transmitting Ācārya graciously bestow his name, to serve as a permanent basis of authentication.

The Dharma-Transmitting disciples of the Country of Japan respectfully offer this monastic petition.

On the fifteenth day of the seventh month of the ninth year of the Dàzhōng era: [Enchin] entered the five-vase abhiṣeka of the Great Compassion Garbhakośa [Maṇḍala] and received the [mudrā-name] Prajñāpāramitā Bodhisattva. He then received complete instruction in the great teachings of the Garbhakośa. Then on the third day of the tenth month: he entered the five-section abhiṣeka of the Vajradhātu [Maṇḍala] and received the [mudrā-name] Vajrapāramitā Bodhisattva. He then received complete instruction in the Susiddhikara of the Vajradhātu and the yoga of the various honored deities, encompassing close to one hundred or more texts. Then on the fifth day of the eleventh month: he entered the abhiṣeka ceremonial ground for the position of Great Ācārya who transmits the great teachings of the five sections, and received the [mudrā-name] Maṇḍala Bodhisattva.

The aforementioned Great Virtue Enchin has already received instruction in the dhāraṇī teachings of the Supreme Vehicle and has mastered them all completely. His questions and answers in doctrinal debate are inexhaustible. He is deemed qualified to transmit and uphold [the teachings] for the broad benefit of those with karmic affinity. As we part ways here, may we meet again in the Pure Land. A thousand and ten thousand expressions of precious care; a thousand and ten thousand expressions of precious care.

The twenty-first day of the tenth month of the ninth year of the Dàzhōng era. The Dharma-Transmitting śramaṇa of Qinglong [Monastery], former Great Virtue of dhāraṇī-recitation at Changsheng Hall, Faquán. This document is submitted to Enchin. A thousand and ten thousand [expressions of care]; a thousand and ten thousand.

---

**Translator's Notes:**

- *Kāiyuán Catalog*: The *Kāiyuán shì jiào lù* (開元釋教錄, 730 CE), the authoritative Tang-dynasty catalog of the Buddhist canon compiled by Zhìshēng.
- *Zhēnyuán Supplementary Catalog*: The *Zhēnyuán xīn dìng shì jiào mùlù* (貞元新定釋教目錄), compiled 800 CE by Yuánzhào.
- *Garbhakośa*: Also known as the Garbhadhātu Maṇḍala, the "womb-realm" maṇḍala of Vajrayāna Buddhism.
- *Susiddhikara*: A major Vajrayāna ritual text (*Susiddhikaramahātantrasādhanopāyikapaṭala*), T893.
- *Faquán* (法全): A Tang Esoteric master at Qinglong Monastery who served as Enchin's teacher; the date of this endorsement (10th month, 21st day) precedes the catalog's finalization date (11th month, 15th day).
