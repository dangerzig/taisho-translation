# 佛說一切諸如來心光明加持普賢菩薩延命金剛最勝陀羅尼經
## Sūtra of All Tathāgatas' Heart-Light Empowerment of Samantabhadra Bodhisattva's Life-Extending Vajra Supreme Dhāraṇī Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1136

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Amoghavajra (不空).

---

Thus have I heard. At one time the Buddha was beside the Ganges River, together with great bhikṣu monks, bodhisattva mahāsattvas, and an assembly of devas and humans. At that time in the assembly, Samantabhadra Bodhisattva was abiding in the Tathāgata's Secret Samādhi. Rising from samādhi, he manifested great spiritual powers. Empowered by all the buddhas, he proclaimed the Vajra Longevity Dhāraṇī so that sentient beings would have their lifespans increased, be free from untimely or premature death, and also attain the vajra longevity that is firm and indestructible, accomplish bodhi, and reach the stage of non-retrogression.

At that time the World-Honored One turned the light from his heart to summon the buddhas of Ganges-sand worlds in the ten directions. They filled the sky, each emitting light like Indra's net, and with their light illumined and touched Samantabhadra Bodhisattva, empowering him to proclaim the Vajra Longevity Sūtra.

At that time Samantabhadra Bodhisattva, having received the heart-seal of all the buddhas, abided in the Vajra Longevity Samaya. From the pores of his body he emitted rays of light equal in number to immeasurable motes of dust, pervading the worlds of the ten directions. With his sovereign supernatural power he then spoke the dhāraṇī:

[Dhāraṇī]

*Tadyathā cale calā cale vināci svastike cakraṃgaṇi praśamantu sarvaloka sarvasattvānāṃ ānāḍi kunāḍi mahānāḍi cale cale hemaguṇakāuri hemanichāni hemaśikhi korave korame hekurave vekura vaiśimakuri viśamaṇi maṇi śuśubhiva ācari vicāri māvilaṃ vahūṃ muhūṃ oṃ vajrāyuṣe svāhā*

When this dhāraṇī had been spoken, the great earth, mountains, and rivers all trembled. Hells were shattered, palaces shook, and beings suffering from illness were immediately revived. In the assembly there were innumerable vajradhara spirits who, with different mouths but the same voice, also proclaimed dhāraṇīs. All the great bodhisattva mahāsattvas joined in proclaiming the life-extending dhāraṇī, so that all would attain the vajra longevity.

At that time the buddhas of the ten directions, numerous as dust-motes and sand-grains, bestowed their heart-seal empowerment, causing Samantabhadra Bodhisattva and the Four Heavenly Kings to attain increased vajra longevity. Then the World-Honored One snapped his fingers in praise: "You have done excellently well in benefiting sentient beings.

"If any good man or good woman fears untimely death, premature death, or inauspicious events, they should bathe, put on fresh clean clothing, burn incense, scatter flowers, and uphold my life-extending heart true word, and their lifespan will be increased. If any being suffering from illness seeks longevity and freedom from illness, they should establish a ritual site in a clean room or at a monastery, invite twenty-one bhikṣu monks of pure conduct, and have them turn and read this sūtra forty-nine times each. Separately, one should uphold this dhāraṇī to the full count of one hundred thousand recitations, and the lifespan will be attained. The sick person will then, in a night dream, see for themselves a supremely wondrous realm.

"For this life-extending method, one must first paint Samantabhadra Bodhisattva in color, in the form of a full-moon youth, wearing a five-buddha crown, with the right hand holding a vajra and the left hand holding the summoning vajra bell, wearing garlands and a flowing sash, seated upon a thousand-petaled jeweled lotus. Below is a white elephant king. The elephant has three heads, each trunk coiling around a single-pronged vajra, and each head has six tusks. The elephant's four feet tread upon a great golden vajra wheel. Below the wheel are five thousand herds of elephants, each bearing the wheel. From the bodhisattva's body radiate a hundred jeweled rays of light, with white light all around. The moon-disc is adorned with various colors. When the image is fully completed, reverently establish a ritual site in a clean place. Have monks of pure conduct uphold this true word and turn this sūtra, and even copy it and uphold the Vajra Longevity Dhāraṇī to the full count of one hundred thousand recitations, and the lifespan will be increased and untimely death removed."

At that time the Four Heavenly Kings, sustained by the Buddha's empowerment, assisted Samantabhadra Bodhisattva's life-extension and each established a vow: "Wherever in the Sahā world and Jambudvīpa this sūtra and dharma circulate, we Four Kings will provide protective sealing so that there is no untimely death. If a clean ritual site for this sūtra exists and we do not descend to attend it, may we lose our awesome light, may our karmic reward be diminished, may we regress from the bodhicitta, and may we fail to attain liberation."

At that time the tathāgatas of the ten directions empowered all the vajradhara bodhisattvas, who with different mouths but the same voice also spoke a life-extending dhāraṇī:

*Oṃ vajrāyuṣe hūṃ hūṃ śikhi svāhā*

When this had been spoken, all the tathāgatas and all vajradhara bodhisattvas of the ten directions disappeared into space and were no longer seen.

At that time Vaiśravaṇa Heavenly King addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I too, assisting with the Buddha's spiritual power, for the sake of empowering, protecting, and removing untimely death, speak a dhāraṇī:

*Oṃ vajrāyuṣe śvete śvete śvetālī rī svāhā*

Having spoken this dhāraṇī, all sentient beings attained lifespan, free from untimely death.

At that time Virūḍhaka Heavenly King addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I too, assisting with the Buddha's spiritual power, proclaim a life-extending dhāraṇī:

*Oṃ vajrāyuṣe mātaṅgī mātaṅgī mātaṅgīnī śumā śumāmu svāhā*

Having spoken this dhāraṇī, they attained lifespan, free from untimely death.

At that time Virūpākṣa Heavenly King addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I too, assisting with the Buddha's spiritual power, assist in proclaiming the life-extending dhāraṇī:

*Oṃ vajrāyuṣe cāri cāri cārile*

Having spoken this dhāraṇī, all sentient beings attained the vajra longevity, free from untimely death.

At that time Dhṛtarāṣṭra Heavenly King addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I too, through the Buddha's spiritual power, proclaim the life-extending dhāraṇī:

*Oṃ vajrāyuṣe milima vava va svāhā*

Having spoken this dhāraṇī, all sentient beings attained the vajra longevity, free from untimely death.

At that time the Buddha told the Four Heavenly Kings: "If any sentient being fears death, calamity, illness, or untimely death, and has such sufferings, they need only copy this sūtra and receive, uphold, read, and recite it, or separately uphold this dhāraṇī, or paint the Samantabhadra Life-Extending Image. Performing this method on the first, eighth, and fifteenth days of the month, establish a ritual site, burn forty-nine lamps, and place flowers, incense, fruits, and medicines in sixteen vessels scattered upon the altar. Join palms, bow, and in a loud voice praise and chant this dhāraṇī and repent of past faults. One will not fall into the three evil paths. Upon leaving this body, one will attain the vajra longevity and no longer undergo the cycle of rebirth.

"If any bhikṣuṇī or any of the four assemblies turns and reads this sūtra without forgetting, they will be freed from the fear of short life and untimely death, as well as evil night-dreams, nightmares, spells and curses, evil-formed rākṣasas, ominous bird-calls, and the hundred strange portents. Nor will they be harmed by water, fire, weapons, or poison. All buddhas and great bodhisattvas will gather them in and protect them, and they will also be protected by all the buddhas."

When the World-Honored One had spoken this sūtra, the bodhisattva mahāsattvas, devas, humans, the four assemblies, and Vaiśravaṇa Heavenly King and all the great assembly greatly rejoiced, accepted it with faith, and put it into practice.
