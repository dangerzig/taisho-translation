# 佛說智光滅一切業障陀羅尼經
## The Buddha's Teaching: Jñānolkā Dhāraṇī Sūtra for Destroying All Karmic Obstacles

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1398

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dānapāla (施護).

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time the World-Honored One was in the palace of Sūrya and Candra. Also present were Jñānolkā Tathāgata, Satya-Vacana Tathāgata, Suvarṇa-Raśmi-Kūṭa Tathāgata, Gambhīra-Svara-Rāja Tathāgata, Krodha Tathāgata, Ratna-Candra Tathāgata, as well as Samantabhadra Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, Dhāraṇī-Dhara-Rāja Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, and Vajrapāṇi Bodhisattva Mahāsattva, together with all such buddhas and bodhisattva mahāsattvas.

At that time the World-Honored One, together with all the tathāgatas and bodhisattva mahāsattvas, was in the Victorious-Banner Pavilion within that palace. Each sat upon a jāmbūnada-gold lion throne adorned with jewels, majestic as golden mountains, their radiance blazing and illuminating one another.

At that time the lord of the palace, the celestial prince Sūrya-Candra, seeing the buddhas and great bodhisattvas with their majestic virtue blazing and surpassing all devas, gave rise to a mind of wonder, joy, and exultation. Standing before the buddhas, he thought within himself: "These buddhas and great bodhisattvas are the lamps of all the realms of sentient beings. They can surely illuminate and destroy all the darkness of evil. How can I too obtain the transformative wisdom of the dhāraṇī, and bring forth such majestic power in the ten directions, becoming a great illumination for all sentient beings?" At that time the Tathāgata and all the buddha-bodhisattva mahāsattvas all knew what the celestial prince was thinking in his mind.

Then the World-Honored One addressed the celestial prince Sūrya-Candra: "Prince! Listen carefully. I shall now teach you the names of the buddhas and bodhisattvas and the dhāraṇī." Then the celestial prince Sūrya-Candra received the teaching and listened. The names of the buddhas and bodhisattvas were then proclaimed:

Homage to Jñānolkā Tathāgata. Homage to Satya-Vacana Tathāgata. Homage to Suvarṇa-Raśmi-Kūṭa Tathāgata. Homage to Gambhīra-Svara-Rāja Tathāgata. Homage to our Śākyamuni Tathāgata. Homage to Krodha Tathāgata. Homage to Ratna-Candra Tathāgata. Homage to Samantabhadra Bodhisattva Mahāsattva. Homage to Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva Mahāsattva. Homage to Dhāraṇī-Dhara-Rāja Bodhisattva Mahāsattva. Homage to Vajrapāṇi Bodhisattva Mahāsattva.

He then spoke the dhāraṇī:

*Tadyathā: sāya-the daṃ cakṣu-nāna cakṣu prabhā-tu-rā mi-cā kāra i-i-the-daṃ-sa su-rā-nā su-rā-nā a-thā-sa i-cā-sa ve-rā ve-rā pā-ri-ṇa ma-cā-ru mṛ-ti-ni ā-va-ni ā-ra-ni kāra-pā-ni kāra-pā-ni tu-ru-tu-se tu-ru-tu-se tu-ri-tu-ru-tu-se rā-sun-ti sun-ti be-ri be-ri tu-ru tu-ru tu-ru tu-ru kāra kāra sa-cā-su sa-cā-su ge-rā ge-rā ge-rā pā-ya ge-rā pā-ya ha-su-ru ha-su-ru dhā-su sun-tu sun-bu se-cā-su a-thā-sa pā-ni ja-ya ru-ri-thā su-ri ki-rā gu-ri mā-gu-ri mā-gu-ri mā-pā-ni ke-ru ke-ru ke-rā-pā-ni kā-rā kā-ri leṃ-tu-ne tu-ru-ne mahā-tu-ru-ne kā-rā kā-rā ki-ri ki-ri vi-pu-se vi-pu-se tu-su tu-su ha-su ha-su ha-sa-pā-ni svāhā.*

At that time, when the World-Honored One had spoken this dhāraṇī, Samantabhadra Bodhisattva Mahāsattva addressed the celestial prince Sūrya-Candra: "Prince! This dhāraṇī was proclaimed in common by eighty-eight koṭis of buddhas in the past. The Tathāgata, with great compassion, wishing to protect and benefit all sentient beings, has now proclaimed it again. Prince! This dhāraṇī is wondrous, rare, difficult to encounter, and difficult to hear. It is like the udumbara flower, which is rare and difficult to see. It is also like the appearance of a tathāgata in the world, which is rare and difficult to encounter. This dhāraṇī is likewise. Prince! If after the Buddha's parinirvāṇa, when the true Dharma is perishing, there are sentient beings who have committed the five rebellious acts and are bound to fall into the Avīci Hell, and such a person encounters this dhāraṇī and can purify body and mind with brave and resolute determination, for twenty-one days and nights, three times during the day and three times during the night, with mind-to-mind continuity without interruption, reciting these dhāraṇī sentences and sincerely confessing, that person's sins of immediate retribution will be entirely eliminated without remainder.

"Prince! Furthermore, through the majestic power of these dhāraṇī sentences, the Avīci Hell can be shattered into a hundred parts, until nothing remains. Moreover, the sentient beings within it who bear the burden of sin will also be liberated. Each will be born in the southern continent of Jambudvīpa, obtain a human body, and come to know: 'It was the merit-power of the dhāraṇī of the buddhas and bodhisattvas that rescued and delivered me.' How much more so for the karmic obstacles of the person who recites it themselves!"

At that time Samantabhadra Bodhisattva further addressed the celestial prince Sūrya-Candra: "Prince! I too, wishing to benefit and bring joy to all sentient beings, and to protect those who recite this dhāraṇī in the final age, shall now speak a dhāraṇī so that all humans and non-humans will be unable to cause them disturbance." He then spoke the dhāraṇī:

*Tadyathā: tu-nā-ci mahā-tu-nā-ci su-ru su-ru svāhā. Śuklā vi-śu-dhā-ni dhā-rā svāhā. Ju-tu spe-rā-di be-tu-ru svāhā. Padma māle-ni satya-rā-te su-mra-te su-ta-be a-prati-ha-mu-dhe su-ru su-ru svāhā. Dhāra dhāra mahā-dhāra-ni yaṃ-tī-ye svāhā. Rā-kṣa-ri-ji-te tu-pa-pā-ri-kā-ri ca-ḍu-ru ḍu-ru svāhā. Dhāra dhāra mahā-dhāra-ni iṃ-tī-ye svāhā. Su-rā-te su-mra-te a-pra-ti-ha-ta-mu-dhe dhāra dhāra dhā-riṃ-tī-ye svāhā. Namo bhagavate jñānolkāya tathāgatāya. Siddhantu-mī mantra-padāni svāhā.*

At that time the celestial prince Sūrya-Candra, having heard the Buddha, the World-Honored One, and Samantabhadra Bodhisattva Mahāsattva proclaim these names of the buddhas and bodhisattvas and the dhāraṇī, had all the doubts in his mind entirely removed. He aroused the mind of enlightenment with a vow never to regress, and with joy and exultation he bowed in reverence and withdrew.
