# 佛說大吉祥陀羅尼經
## Sūtra of the Great Auspicious Dhāraṇī Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1381

Translated from the Chinese. Song dynasty, translated by Dharmabhadra (法賢)

---

Thus have I heard:

At one time the Buddha was dwelling in the buddha-field of Sukhāvatī, together with a great assembly of bodhisattvas and bhūtas. At that time, the Bodhisattva Mahāsattva Avalokiteśvara came to where the Buddha was; upon arriving he bowed with his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. There was also another Bodhisattva Mahāsattva named Mahāśrī, who likewise came to where the Buddha was; upon arriving he circumambulated the Buddha three times, bowed with his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

At that time, the World-Honored One, having seen the Bodhisattva Mahāśrī, addressed the Bodhisattva Avalokiteśvara, saying: "This Bodhisattva Mahāśrī possesses a dhāraṇī. In the turbid and evil age of Jambudvīpa, should any bhikṣu, bhikṣuṇī, upāsaka, or upāsikā see, hear, and rejoice in it; read, recite, and uphold it; have faith in and understand this Dharma; or contemplate and keep it in mind — those who are poor and suffering will obtain great wealth and nobility, and even the bhūtas will universally come to love and revere them. The merit thus accrued cannot be fully told." When the Bodhisattva Avalokiteśvara heard the Buddha's words, he was overjoyed and immediately rose from his seat, joined his palms, and addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One, I now delight in hearing this. I pray that the World-Honored One will proclaim it for me."

Thereupon the World-Honored One proclaimed the Great Auspicious Dhāraṇī:

> *tathā roci-mi śrī padme vāhinī dhāna-dhipati aurī mahāyaśa padmani tri mahāni yuti ānandaye ratna-prabhā mahāśrī bhāya-tena giriṇi giriṇi sarva-akārya śame dini dini sisi sisi nidi nidi hini hini arakṣami manasāye sarva-rakṣami misāthānāye svāhā — namaḥ sarva-buddha-bodhisattvebhyaḥ svāhā*

When the World-Honored One had finished speaking this sūtra, the Bodhisattva Avalokiteśvara, the Bodhisattva Mahāśrī, and all the bodhisattvas and the assembly of bhūtas, having heard what the Buddha had proclaimed, were all filled with great joy, received it with faith, and put it into practice.
