# 宿曜儀軌
## Ritual Procedure for the Constellations and Luminaries

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1304

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, composed by Yixing (一行).

---

Mudrā of Ākāśagarbha: Interlace the palms, with the water-fingers [ring fingers] clasped. The wind-fingers [index fingers] form the shape of a jewel. The mantra states:

*Oṃ ākāśa-garbha-pāla iya oṃ māri-ga māri mūli svāhā.*

If a person wishes to seek merit and wisdom, they should take refuge in this bodhisattva. The sun, moon, and stars are all transformations of Ākāśagarbha.

Mudrā of Mañjuśrī: Form a hollow-palmed añjali. With each thumb, press upon the nails of the two water-fingers [ring fingers]. Bend the two wind-fingers [index fingers] and pinch the tips of the space-fingers [thumbs]. The mantra states:

*Oṃ a-vi-ra hūṃ kha-ca-ra.*

If one seeks pacification, wishing to extinguish the seven kinds of calamities, namely: eclipses of the sun and moon, the five planets deviating from their proper courses, soldiers and bandits rising in conflict, untimely floods and droughts, winds and rains losing their measure, treacherous ministers rebelling and harming the people of the nation, and fierce wolves and wild beasts devouring sentient beings, and the five grains failing to ripen. When such calamities, crises, official difficulties, royal perils, mortal dangers, and terrors arise, all shall be dispelled and scattered.

Mudrā of Samantabhadra: A hollow-hearted añjali. The mantra states:

*Ji-pa-ṭa vi-ni pa-ṭa u-su pa-ṭa.*

If one recites this mantra seven times at dawn and seven times at night, all sorcery, wild-path curses, and insect-poisons will be entirely extinguished. One can attain the three liberations of body, mind, and wisdom. In future lives one will not receive a body of birth-and-death but will obtain the Dharma-body. Enemies and bandits within and beyond the land, all evil persons, all ghosts and spirits, all thieves, tigers, wolves, lions, venomous insects, and wild beasts, upon hearing the sound of this mantra, will all have their mouths sealed shut and will not be able to cause disturbance. Celestial anomalies and crises, evil dreams, calamities, and all manner of strange bird-omens will naturally be extinguished. The efficacious power of this mantra cannot be fully described.

Mudrā for Extending Life: With the left hand, pinch the fire-finger [middle finger] and space-finger [thumb] as if holding a lotus. The right hand extends straight downward. The mantra states:

*Oṃ vajra-āyuṣe svāhā.*

If one recites this mantra, one's life will be extended and afflictions removed.

Mudrā of Śakra Devendra: The right hand forms a fist placed at the waist. The left hand stands upright with fingers together. The earth-finger [little finger] and water-finger [ring finger] bend at the middle joint. The wind-finger [index finger] is placed against the back of the fire-finger [middle finger], and the space-finger [thumb] bends at the middle joint. The mantra states:

*Oṃ indrāya svāhā.*

If one recites this mantra, one will be granted official rank and all wishes will be fulfilled.

Mudrā of Vaiśravaṇa Deva: Form a hollow-hearted añjali. Cross the two space-fingers [thumbs] and stand them upright together. The two wind-fingers [index fingers] attach to the sides of the two fire-fingers [middle fingers] like hooks, their tips separated by about one inch without touching. The mantra states:

*Oṃ vaiśravaṇāya svāhā.*

If one recites this mantra, one will obtain great wealth and abundance and be guarded day and night.

Mantra of Sūrya Deva:

*Namo ratna-trayāya. Namaḥ sūrya sarva-nakṣatra-rājāya. Oṃ amogha uṣṇīṣe svāhā.*

Mantra of Candra Deva:

*Oṃ candra nakṣatra-rājāya śānti svāhā.*

Mantra of Mars:

*Oṃ aṅgāraka-rūpāya svāhā.*

Mantra of Mercury:

*Oṃ budha-nakṣatra vi-nakṣe-nu-ma svāhā.*

Mantra of Jupiter:

*Oṃ bṛhaspati-namāḥ pitā-vana-śiṃ-śāla-vara-dhani svāhā.*

Mantra of Venus:

*Oṃ śukra-gandha-vara-rājāya śrī-kāri svāhā.*

Mantra of Saturn:

*Oṃ śanaiścara-nakṣatra-brahma-rūpāya puṣṭi-kāri svāhā.*

Mantra of Rāhu:

*Oṃ rāhu-nā asura-rājāya sma-śatru-nāya śānti-kāri svāhā.*

Mantra of Ketu:

*Oṃ vajra-ketu-nakṣatra-rājāya svāhā.*

Mudrā of the Nine Grahas: Form a firm añjali. The two space-fingers [thumbs] stand upright together. The two wind-fingers [index fingers] extend; this is called "the root opens, standing, coming and going." The mantra states:

*Oṃ graha-īśvarīya-prāpta-jyoti-māya svāhā.*

Mudrā of the Twenty-Eight Mansions: Form a firm añjali. Stand the two space-fingers [thumbs] upright together at the heart. Use the two space-fingers to summon them. The mantra states:

*Oṃ nakṣatra-jñā sunā-ni-ye svāhā.*

First, make offerings to Ākāśagarbha, Mañjuśrī, Samantabhadra, [the deity of] Life-Extension, Śakra, Vaiśravaṇa, and the other bodhisattvas and devas. Afterward, invite and request the nine luminaries, the twenty-eight mansions, the star of the Northern Dipper that governs one's natal destiny, as well as the Lord of Mount Tai and the Officers of Fate and Fortune, making offerings to them. Pray for the removal of calamity, the extension of life, and the elimination of adversities.

Mantra of Auspiciousness:

*Namo ratna-trayāya. Namaḥ su-maḥ sarva-nakṣatra-rājāya. Ja-ja-tu-di-pa āloka-rāya. Tadyathā: nu-ma-ti pa-nu-ma-ti sa-pi-ni kha-se svāhā.*

One should recite this mantra of the nine luminaries for averting calamity, together with the great dhāraṇī of Pāṇḍaravāsinī Avalokiteśvara. If the sun or moon is positioned within one's natal mansion, or if the five planets are contending in one's natal mansion and deviating from their courses, one should establish a ritual site of Pāṇḍaravāsinī Avalokiteśvara or the Blazing Radiance Buddha-Crown of Mañjuśrī's Eight-Syllable [Mantra], and recite according to each method. All calamities will naturally be dispelled and scattered. When any luminary is inauspicious, reciting this mantra transforms it into auspiciousness.

Mantra of the Seven Stars of the Northern Dipper:

*Oṃ saptāna-ji-nāya pañja-mīya jamphu-tha-ma svā-vi-nā rakṣaṃ-bhava-tu svāhā.*

If Rāhu or Ketu secretly enters one's natal star-mansion, one should recite this Northern Dipper mantra.

The mantra spoken by all tathāgatas for destroying all constellation obstacles and bringing auspiciousness:

*Oṃ sarva-nakṣatra-samāye śrīye śānti-kuru svāhā.*

The *Vajra Constellation Accomplishment Sūtra*, "Chapter on Auspicious Accomplishment," states: "I now proclaim the wondrous secret method of worldly accomplishment. These luminaries travel through space. Whether one, two, three, four, or five of them enter sentient beings' natal mansion, opposing mansion, migration mansion, great killing-karma mansion, stability mansion, weak-aspect mansion, or servant mansion, they create various adversities. The four great malefic luminaries, namely Mars, Saturn, Rāhu, and Ketu, afflict sentient beings most severely. At such times, one should cultivate meritorious deeds, practice broad benevolence and compassion, and rely upon the eight-syllable mantra of Mañjuśrī, or the Blazing Radiance Buddha-Crown, or the Pāṇḍaravāsinī Avalokiteśvara, or the One-Syllable King Buddha-Crown. Establish a great pacification homa altar and recite and make offerings according to each method. All calamities will naturally be extinguished.

"For performing the method of accomplishment: on days ruled by the Heart Mansion, the Willow Mansion, the Pleiades Mansion, or the Ox Mansion, regardless of whether the day's astrological conditions are auspicious or inauspicious, on such mansion-ruled days, fast for one full day and recite 1,080 times. All heart-wishes will be fulfilled at that very time, and one will attain the great siddhi. In times of adversity, one's own birthday is most suitable. For performing the method of accomplishment of Jupiter, the sixteenth of the month is most suitable. For performing the method of accomplishment of Mars, the very day it rules is most suitable. For performing the method of accomplishment of Saturn, the seventh of each month is most suitable. For performing the method of accomplishment of Venus, the direction in which it is visible at dawn is most suitable. For performing the method of accomplishment of Mercury, perform it on the day ruled by Mercury. For performing the method of Rāhu or Ketu, one's own birthday is most suitable. Furthermore, for making offerings to one's natal mansion, accomplishment may be sought in one's natal year and month, or on one's birthday. If born under a malefic mansion, make monthly offerings, or offerings every three or seven days, and the malefic influence will be transformed into an auspicious one. Keep this secret; do not let the uninitiated know."
