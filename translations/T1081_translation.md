# 佛說觀自在菩薩如意心陀羅尼呪經
## Sūtra of the Wish-Fulfilling Heart Dhāraṇī Spell of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, Spoken by the Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1081

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Yìjìng (義淨).

---

Thus have I heard. At one time the Bhagavān was on Mount Gṛdhrakūṭa, together with an immeasurable assembly of great bodhisattvas. At that time Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated three times to the right, knelt with both knees on the ground, joined his palms respectfully, and addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One, I now possess a great dhāraṇī vidyā-mantra and a great altar-field method, called the Blue Lotus Summit Candana Maṇi Heart Vajra Secret Constant Protection. It is the Unobstructed Avalokiteśvara Lotus Wish-Fulfilling Jewel Wheel King Dhāraṇī Heart-Spell, foremost and most rare. In all that one seeks, it brings benefit according to one's heart, and all is accomplished. World-Honored One, out of great compassion, if you permit me to speak, I shall, sustained by the Buddha's majestic power, bestow it upon all sentient beings. World-Honored One, this dhāraṇī has great spiritual power and great skillful means. I now, directly before the Buddha, shall proclaim it in due order. I humbly beseech the World-Honored One to extend his compassionate protection, raining wondrous treasures upon me and upon all who hold the vidyā-mantras, like a wish-fulfilling tree, like a wish-fulfilling jewel, causing all their hopes and desires to be fulfilled in due time."

At that time the World-Honored One praised Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, saying: "So it is, so it is. You are able to have compassion for all classes of sentient beings. I give you my protection. Now, before me, I shall cause all your wishes to be fulfilled. If you wish to proclaim the Unobstructed Avalokiteśvara Lotus Wish-Fulfilling Jewel Wheel King Dhāraṇī, the most extremely profound secret heart-spell, speak as you wish."

Then Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, having received the Buddha's permission, his heart brimming with compassionate vows, before the Buddha, with a heart of great compassion spoke the spell:

*Namo buddhāya namo dharmāya namo saṃghāya namaḥ avalokiteśvarāya bodhisattvāya mahāsattvāya mahā-kāruṇikāya tadyathā oṃ cakra-vartī cintā-maṇi mahā-padme ruru ruru tiṣṭha jvala ākarṣāya hūṃ phaṭ svāhā*

Next he spoke the great heart-spell:

*Oṃ padma cintā-maṇi jvala hūṃ*

Next he spoke the following-heart spell:

*Oṃ varada padme hūṃ*

When Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva had spoken this Great Wheel Dhāraṇī Spell-King, the great earth immediately quaked in six ways. All the heavenly palaces, nāga palaces, and yakṣa palaces, and the palaces of the gandharvas, asuras, kiṃnaras, and others all spun and trembled in confusion. All evil māras who create obstructions saw their own palaces burst into flames, and all were struck with terror. Evil-minded beings, evil nāgas, evil ghosts, yakṣas, and rākṣasas all tumbled and fell. Those beings suffering in the hells were all freed from their suffering and reborn in the heavens. At that time, in the assembly before the World-Honored One, heavenly jeweled flowers and jeweled adornments rained down, and in the sky heavenly music played, producing all manner of sounds in abundant offering.

Then the World-Honored One, with a fine and wondrous voice, praised Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Mahāsattva: "Excellent, excellent, Avalokiteśvara! The great spell-king you have proclaimed is truly rare and hard to encounter. It can cause sentient beings to have their wishes fulfilled and to obtain great rewards. As for the ritual procedures for reciting this spell, I shall now explain them.

"If any good man or good woman, bhikṣu, bhikṣuṇī, upāsaka, or upāsikā sets their heart on seeking results in this present life, they should single-mindedly receive and uphold this spell. When undertaking to uphold it, there is no need to consult the stars or celestial signs for auspicious or inauspicious days, no need for special fasting or precept observance, and no need for bathing or wearing clean garments. One need only concentrate the mind and recite without ceasing, and a hundred thousand kinds of wishes will all be accomplished. There is no other vidyā-mantra that can match the power of this Wish-Fulfilling Spell-King.

"Therefore it first removes all karmic obstructions, then accomplishes all undertakings. It can also extinguish the grave offenses of the five heinous crimes that would lead to the Avīci Hell. It can also eliminate all illness and suffering, curing all of them completely. All heavy karma it can entirely destroy. As for fever-diseases, whether by day or by night, one-day malaria or up to four-day malaria, wind disorders, bile disorders, phlegm disorders, and the entanglement of all three humors: all such diseases are cured by reciting the spell.

"If another person has used curses, sorcery, or gu-poison, all are utterly extinguished without any remainder. Even if all manner of abscesses, goiters, malignant sores, scabies, leprosy, carbuncles, and ringworm cover one's entire body, and even pain of the eyes, ears, nose, tongue, lips, mouth, teeth, throat, crown of the head, chest, ribs, heart, abdomen, waist, back, feet, hands, head, face, and all the joints, or hemiplegia, abdominal bloating and hardness, indigestion: from head to foot, whatever illness or suffering exists, all are completely cured.

"If any yakṣas, rākṣasas, vināyakas, evil māras, ghosts, or spirits, or any evildoer, none shall prevail against one. Nor shall weapons, swords, soldiers, arrows, water, fire, evil poisons, malicious storms, hail, enemies, bandits, or robbers touch one's body. There shall be no royal punishment or banditry, no untimely death, no hostile invasion.

"Evil dreams, vipers, venomous snakes, scorpions, geckos, centipedes, spiders, and all manner of venomous beasts, tigers, and wolves cannot cause harm. In battle and warfare one shall always be victorious. If there is a lawsuit, it shall be resolved in reconciliation.

"If one recites it but once, all the above matters shall be accomplished as wished. If one recites one hundred and eight times daily, one shall see Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, who will say: 'Good man, do not be afraid. Whatever wish you desire, I shall bestow it all upon you.' Amitābha Buddha himself shall appear, and one shall also see the Land of Ultimate Bliss with all its wondrous adornments, as extensively described in the sūtras, and shall also see the assembly of bodhisattvas in the Land of Ultimate Bliss. One shall also see all the buddhas of the ten directions, and shall also see Mount Potalaka, the dwelling place of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva.

"One shall immediately attain purity of body and shall always be honored, revered, and served by kings, ministers, prime ministers, and officials. All people shall cherish and respect one. In every place of rebirth one shall not enter the mother's womb but shall be born from a lotus, with all the marks complete. In every place of birth one shall always possess the knowledge of former lives. From this very day until the attainment of buddhahood, one shall never fall into evil destinies but shall always be born in the presence of the Buddha."

Then Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One, this Candana Heart-Wheel Dhāraṇī as I have spoken it: if any bhikṣu, bhikṣuṇī, upāsaka, or upāsikā, with an utterly sincere heart, recollects it and is able to receive and uphold it, they will certainly attain accomplishment. One need only have deep faith and must not give rise to doubt."

Then the World-Honored One praised Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva: "Excellent, excellent! Your great compassion is immeasurable, to be able to speak this wondrous Wish-Fulfilling Heart-Wheel Dhāraṇī dharma. Among the sentient beings of Jambudvīpa, if one sets their heart and recites it with the mouth, one shall immediately obtain direct verification. Following my teaching, you should ceaselessly and diligently encourage, instruct, and guide all sentient beings, enabling them to attain verification and manifesting your body for them. Do not disobey my command, and I shall rejoice."

Then Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva addressed the Buddha: "World-Honored One, throughout immeasurable kalpas I have, with a compassionate heart, always protected suffering sentient beings. I humbly beseech you to bear witness. For the sake of sentient beings I have spoken this Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī. If any person receives and upholds it, constantly making it their practice, single-mindedly reciting it, all their wishes shall be accomplished. I now, sustained by the Buddha's majestic power, thus save beings from suffering."

When Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva had spoken this Wish-Fulfilling Wheel Dhāraṇī Sūtra, the entire great assembly all rejoiced, accepted it with faith, and put it into practice.
