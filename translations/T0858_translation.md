# 大毘盧遮那略要速疾門五支念誦法
## The Five-Limb Recitation Method of the Abbreviated Essential and Swift Gate of Mahāvairocana

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 0858

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translator unknown

---

**Three Vajra Mantra:**

> [Dhāraṇī] HŪṂ HŪṂ HŪṂ

**Samaya Mantra:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ a samaye tri-samaye samaye svāhā

**Dharmadhātu-Arising Mantra:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ dharmadhātu svabhāva kuhaṃ

**Mantra for Turning the Great Wheel of the Dharma:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-vajrāṇāṃ vajra-dharma kuhaṃ

**Vajra Body-Protection Mantra:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-vajrāṇāṃ vajra-kavace hūṃ

**Root Mantra of Vairocana:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ a svāhā

**Mantra of the Venerable Acala:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-vajrāṇāṃ caṇḍa mahāroṣaṇa sphaṭaya hūṃ trāṭ haṃ māṃ

**Universal Offering Mantra:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ sarvatha khaṃ udgate sphara māṃ gagana khaṃ svāhā

**Incense-Burning Mantra:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ dharmadhātu-anāgate svāhā

**Argha-Offering Mantra:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Namaḥ samanta-buddhānāṃ gagana sama-sama svāhā

**Ucchuṣma Vajra Mantra:**

> [Dhāraṇī] Oṃ krodhanāṃ hūṃ jaḥ

---

The Record of Jikaku states: *Five-Limb Recitation Method of the Abbreviated Essential and Swift Gate of Mahāvairocana*, one fascicle.
