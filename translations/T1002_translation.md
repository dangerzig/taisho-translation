# 不空羂索毘盧遮那佛大灌頂光真言
## Great Consecration Radiance True Word of Amoghapāśa Vairocana Buddha

Taishō Tripiṭaka No. 1002

Translated from the Chinese. Tang dynasty, translated by Amoghavajra (不空)

---

*Oṃ amogha-vairocana mahā-mudrā maṇi padma jvāla pravartāya hūṃ*

Vairocana Tathāgata, in bestowing the mudrā dhāraṇī samaya supernatural-power Dharma chapter, declared this to be foremost. If anyone has committed all the ten evils, the five heinous crimes, and the four pārājikas and other transgressions of the past, they will be entirely consumed and extinguished. If any sentient being, wherever they may be, hears this Great Consecration Radiance True Word two, three, or seven times passing through the ear-faculty, all their karmic obstructions will immediately be eliminated. If any sentient being has committed the ten evils, the five heinous crimes, and the four pārājikas, with transgressions as numerous as atoms filling this entire world, and upon the body's destruction and life's end they fall into the evil destinies: if one empowers earth or sand with this true word one hundred and eight times and scatters it upon the corpse of the deceased in a charnel ground, or scatters it upon their grave, or upon anyone encountered, scatters it upon them, then that deceased person, whether in hell, the hungry-ghost realm, the asura realm, or the animal realm, by the power of all the Tathāgatas, the true and real fundamental vow of the Amoghapāśa Vairocana Tathāgata Great Consecration Radiance True Word, and the supernatural awesome power of the empowered sand, will immediately obtain radiance upon their body, all karmic retributions will be removed, they will abandon the body of suffering, and proceed to the western land of Sukhāvatī, to be born by transformation from a lotus, and until they attain bodhi they will never again fall.

Furthermore, if there are sentient beings who for years and months have been emaciated, sallow, and afflicted with illness, suffering ten thousand kinds of pain, and this sickness is due to the karmic retributions of former lives: if, before the sick person, for one, two, or three days, one recites this true word aloud one thousand and eighty times each day, the karmic obstructions of previous lives and the illness-obstructions will be eliminated. If one is harassed by ghosts, the consciousness is confused and disordered, and one loses one's voice and cannot speak: the true-word holder should empower their hand with one hundred and eight recitations and rub the head and face. Place the hand upon the heart and the forehead, empowering with one thousand and eighty recitations, and the illness will be cured. Even illnesses caused by Mahākāla spirits can be treated and dispelled. If there are illnesses caused by ghosts, spirits, or wangliáng, empower a five-colored thread-cord with one hundred and eight knots and tie it to the waist, arm, or neck of the sick person, and the illness will immediately be cured. If there are malarial fevers, empower a white thread-cord with one hundred and eight knots and tie it around the head and neck, and also empower their garments; the illness will immediately be cured. If one empowers calamus root with one thousand and eighty recitations and holds it in the mouth, then in conversation or debate with others, one will always prevail. If one takes pepper, tagara incense, blue sandalwood, small white sandalwood, yellow, and rasāñjana, etc., grinds them into powder, mixes with water, and forms pills the size of jujubes, empowering them with one hundred thousand recitations, and then dries them in the shade: for any illness caused by ghosts and spirits, various malarial fevers, poisoning, or loss of voice, one should grind the medicine with water, empower it with one hundred and eight recitations, and apply several drops to both eyes, the forehead, and the heart. One should empower wrathfully, and the illness will immediately be cured. If the disease-causing ghost or spirit does not release its hold, its head will split like the branch of an arjaka tree. If one has been stung by poisonous insects, snakes, or scorpions, applying the medicine to the eyes will immediately cure it.

Furthermore, by another method: take a new rice doll, bathe the body clean, and put on clean garments. Then grind the medicine with water, empower it with one hundred and eight recitations, and apply drops to the eyes of the rice doll. Wrathfully empower with one thousand and eighty recitations, and the doll will immediately sit up. Whatever is asked, it will answer. When one wishes to release it, empower white mustard-seed water with twenty-one recitations and scatter it upon the rice doll, and it will return to its original state. If one is summoned to meet a person of high rank, apply the medicine to the eyes before going to see them, and one will be received with honor and respect.
